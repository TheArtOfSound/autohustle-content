FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before getting started.

How does AutoHustle actually work? +
AutoHustle runs 13 AI-powered monetization strategies simultaneously on a 5-minute cycle loop. An orchestrator uses UCB1 allocation to distribute resources where they perform best. The system autonomously creates content, publishes it, manages your Shopify store, trades crypto, and optimizes everything — all while you monitor results through a real-time dashboard.
Do I need technical skills to use it? +
The onboarding wizard walks you through setup step by step — connecting your API keys, configuring strategies, and setting preferences. Some integrations (like Shopify or Stripe) require accounts on those platforms, but AutoHustle handles the automation from there.
What LLM providers does it use? +
AutoHustle supports 15+ LLM providers including Ollama (local/free), Groq, Cerebras, Gemini, Together, Fireworks, Mistral, DeepSeek, OpenRouter, OpenAI, Claude, and more. It prioritizes free/local providers first and falls back to paid ones only when needed. Most users run entirely on free tiers.
What safety systems are in place? +
Five independent safety layers: a kill switch that instantly halts all strategies, circuit breakers that auto-pause after consecutive failures, trading guards with max drawdown and daily loss limits, a budget guard for daily spend caps, and a resource manager that monitors RAM and kills runaway processes. A stall watchdog also force-restarts any strategy that runs too long.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. There are no contracts or commitments. Cancel anytime from your dashboard. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee so you can try it risk-free.
How does the crypto trading work? +
Two strategies handle crypto: the Crypto Trading engine uses technical analysis to generate signals and execute trades on Coinbase, while the Solana Sniper runs a Node.js bot that scores new tokens in two phases and executes rapid swaps via Raydium and Jupiter. Both include trading guards with configurable drawdown limits.
Does AutoHustle generate original content? +
Yes. All content is generated fresh by AI with built-in deduplication — the system checks the last 60 titles before every generation to ensure no topic repeats. A mass auditor runs every 30 minutes to check for dead links, empty content, and quality issues, auto-fixing anything above 80% confidence.
