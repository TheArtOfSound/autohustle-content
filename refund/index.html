Refund Policy

Last updated: March 2026

At AutoHustle, we want you to be completely satisfied with your subscription. If the platform does not meet your expectations, we offer a straightforward refund process.

✓ 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

1. 30-Day Money-Back Guarantee

All new AutoHustle subscriptions are backed by a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you are not satisfied with the Service for any reason within the first 30 days of your initial purchase, you are entitled to a full refund of your first monthly payment.

This guarantee applies to:

  • First-time subscribers only
  • The initial monthly payment of $99
  • Requests made within 30 calendar days of the original purchase date

2. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to support@autohustle.online with the subject line "Refund Request"
  2. Include your account email address and the approximate date of purchase
  3. Briefly let us know why the Service did not meet your expectations (optional, but helps us improve)

No phone calls, complicated forms, or hoops to jump through. One email is all it takes.

3. Processing Time

Once we receive your refund request:

  • We will confirm receipt within 1 business day
  • Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
  • The refund will appear on your original payment method (credit card, debit card, etc.)
  • Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional 5-10 business days for the credit to appear on your statement

4. After the 30-Day Period

After the 30-day guarantee window has passed:

  • Monthly subscription payments are non-refundable
  • You may cancel your subscription at any time to prevent future charges
  • Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you retain access until then
  • No partial refunds are issued for unused portions of a billing period

5. Exceptions

Refunds may be denied if:

  • The account was used in violation of our Terms of Service
  • The refund request is made after the 30-day window
  • The subscriber has previously received a refund for an AutoHustle subscription (the guarantee is one-time per customer)

6. Account Access After Refund

Once a refund is issued:

  • Your subscription will be canceled immediately
  • All active strategies will be stopped
  • Access to the dashboard will be revoked
  • Content and products already generated and published during your subscription remain yours
  • Your account data will be retained for 30 days in case you choose to resubscribe, then permanently deleted

7. Chargebacks

If you have a billing concern, we strongly encourage you to contact us first at support@autohustle.online before initiating a chargeback with your bank or card issuer. Unauthorized chargebacks may result in permanent account suspension.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy or need to request a refund, reach out to us:
