Buying Guide: What to Look for in the Best Pet Dental Care Products

When choosing the best pet dental care products, keep these key criteria in mind:

  • Veterinary Approval: Look for products with the VOHC (Veterinary Oral Health Council) seal, which indicates they’ve been scientifically proven to reduce plaque and tartar.
  • Ease of Use: The best product is one you’ll actually use. If your pet hates brushing, a water additive or spray might be more effective than a toothbrush.
  • Pet-Specific Formulation: Dogs and cats have different needs—ensure the product is designed for your pet’s species and size.
  • Ingredients: Avoid artificial flavors, xylitol (toxic to dogs), and harsh chemicals. Opt for enzymatic, natural, or probiotic-based formulas for safer, long-term use.

FAQ

Can I use human toothpaste on my pet?

No—human toothpaste contains fluoride and xylitol, which are toxic to pets. Always use toothpaste specifically made for dogs or cats.

How often should I clean my pet’s teeth?

Ideally, brush your pet’s teeth daily. If that’s not possible, aim for at least 3–4 times per week, supplemented with dental chews or water additives.

Are dental chews enough for good oral health?

While helpful, chews alone aren’t a substitute for brushing. For optimal results, combine chews with other methods like brushing or water additives.
