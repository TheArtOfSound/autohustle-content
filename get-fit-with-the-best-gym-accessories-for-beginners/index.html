Buying Guide: 3-4 Criteria to Consider

---------------------------------------------

When choosing gym accessories, there are several criteria to consider:

  • Purpose: What do you want to use the accessory for? (e.g. weightlifting, cardio, stretching)

  • Budget: What is your budget for the accessory?
