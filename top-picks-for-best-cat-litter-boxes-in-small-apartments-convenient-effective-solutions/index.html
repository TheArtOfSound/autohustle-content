Buying Guide

When choosing the best cat litter box for your small apartment, there are several factors to consider. Here are three key criteria to keep in mind:

  • Size: Consider the size of your apartment and the number of cats you have. A compact litter box is ideal for small apartments.
  • Ease of use: Look for a litter box with an easy-to-use design that minimizes mess and maintenance.
  • Odor control: Choose a litter box with advanced odor control features to keep your home clean and hygienic.

FAQ

#### Q: How often should I change the litter in my cat's litter box?

A: It's recommended to change the litter in your cat's litter box every 7-10 days to maintain cleanliness and hygiene.

#### Q: What type of litter is best for cats with sensitive paws?

A: Cats with sensitive paws prefer non-clumping litter that is gentle on their paws.

#### Q: How do I clean a litter box with an automatic rake system?

A: To clean a litter box with an automatic rake system, simply empty the waste and refill the litter. The rake system will take care of the rest.
