#### Buying Guide: 3-4 criteria to consider

When selecting a pet care brand, consider the following key factors:

  • Nutritional quality: Look for products with named protein sources and whole grains or fruits.
  • Ingredient safety: Ensure that ingredients are free from artificial preservatives, colors, and additives.
  • Life stage suitability: Choose products tailored to your pet's specific life stage (puppy, adult, senior).
  • Lifestyle compatibility: Opt for products suitable for your lifestyle (active vs. sedentary).
