FAQ

Q: How often should I bathe my puppy after a flea treatment?

A: It's recommended to wait at least 24 hours after bathing your puppy before applying a topical flea medication or using a flea preventative shampoo.

Q: Can I use human flea shampoos on my puppy?

A: No, it's not safe to use human flea shampoos on puppies under 12 weeks old. These products may contain ingredients that are toxic to young animals.

Q: How long does flea shampoo take to work?

A: Flea shampoos typically start killing fleas within 30 minutes and provide protection against infestations for up to 2 weeks, depending on the product and usage instructions.
