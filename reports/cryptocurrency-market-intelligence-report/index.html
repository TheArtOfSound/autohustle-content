Risk Factors

Bitcoin's continued dominance, while providing stability, creates systemic risk where market-wide sentiment heavily depends on Bitcoin's performance trajectory.

Regulatory Uncertainty

The uniform decline against fiat currencies may indicate regulatory overhang or policy uncertainty affecting institutional adoption rates.

Liquidity Concerns

Altcoin outperformance against Bitcoin could signal either healthy diversification or potential liquidity rotation that may reverse rapidly in volatile conditions.

Macroeconomic Sensitivity

The consistent fiat currency weakness pattern suggests cryptocurrency markets remain sensitive to broader macroeconomic conditions and monetary policy shifts.
