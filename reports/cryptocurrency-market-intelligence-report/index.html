The cryptocurrency market continues to demonstrate significant volatility and innovation, with NFT-backed tokens emerging as a notable trend. Key Market Trends

NFT-to-Token Evolution

The emergence of PENGU represents a significant trend where established NFT projects are launching native tokens to enhance utility and community engagement. This evolution demonstrates the maturation of the NFT ecosystem beyond simple collectibles toward functional digital assets with broader market applications.
