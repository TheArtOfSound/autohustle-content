4. Risk Factors

4.1 Market Volatility & Corrections

  • Crypto markets remain highly volatile, with sudden price swings possible.
  • Overleveraged positions could trigger liquidations, exacerbating downturns.

4.2 Regulatory Uncertainty

  • Governments (e.g., U.S., EU, China) are tightening crypto regulations, which may impact trading and adoption.
  • Potential bans or restrictions on certain tokens could cause market disruptions.

4.3 Macroeconomic Pressures

  • Rising interest rates and inflation may reduce risk appetite, leading to capital outflows from crypto.
  • Geopolitical tensions (e.g., conflicts, sanctions) could further destabilize markets.

4.4 Security & Scalability Challenges

  • Smart contract vulnerabilities and exchange hacks remain risks.
  • Scalability issues in Layer 1 blockchains could limit adoption.
