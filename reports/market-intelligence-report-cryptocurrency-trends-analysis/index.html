4. Risk Factors

A. Extreme Volatility

  • Meme coins like STIBA can collapse just as quickly as they rise.
  • Liquidity risks—many low-cap coins have thin order books, leading to sharp price swings.

B. Regulatory Risks

  • SEC crackdowns on exchanges and stablecoins could disrupt trading.
  • Global regulations (e.g., EU MiCA, U.S. stablecoin rules) may impact market structure.

C. Market Manipulation

  • Pump-and-dump schemes are common in low-cap meme coins.
  • Whale activity can artificially inflate prices before a sudden sell-off.

D. Macroeconomic Factors

  • Fed rate decisions and inflation data influence risk appetite.
  • Bitcoin’s correlation with traditional markets remains a concern.

C. Strategic Recommendations

  • Monitor regulatory developments to anticipate market shifts.
