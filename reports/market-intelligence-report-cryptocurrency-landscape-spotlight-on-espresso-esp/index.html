  • Extreme Volatility: The cryptocurrency market is inherently volatile. Prices can fluctuate dramatically in short periods, leading to significant losses for investors. Espresso's substantial 24-hour gains highlight this volatility.
  • Regulatory Scrutiny: The evolving regulatory landscape poses a significant risk. Unfavorable regulations or enforcement actions could negatively impact the value and usability of cryptocurrencies.
  • Market Manipulation: Smaller-cap cryptocurrencies, like Espresso, can be more susceptible to market manipulation, including "pump and dump" schemes, where prices are artificially inflated and then sold off rapidly.
