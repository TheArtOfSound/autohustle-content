Bitcoin, the largest cryptocurrency by market cap, is trading at $63,283, down 3.9% in 24 hours. This decline aligns with broader market corrections, possibly due to:

  • Profit-taking after recent rallies
  • Macroeconomic uncertainty (e.g., Fed rate decisions, inflation data)
  • Increased regulatory scrutiny in key markets

2.2 Altcoin Performance: Selective Strength

While Bitcoin struggles, some altcoins are showing resilience:

  • Solana (SOL) (+0.84%) – Benefiting from ecosystem growth and NFT activity.
  • EOS (+1.10%) – Gaining traction in decentralized applications (dApps).
  • Polkadot (DOT) (+0.53%) – Strengthening due to cross-chain interoperability advancements.
  • Ethereum (ETH) (-0.28%) – Slightly negative but outperforming Bitcoin.

  • DeFi tokens (e.g., UNI, AAVE) are mixed, with some showing minor gains amid yield-seeking behavior.
