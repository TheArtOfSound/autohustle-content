The Best Budget Gaming Monitors for Home Office Use in 2023

=================================================================

As a gamer and remote worker, you likely spend a significant amount of time staring at screens. Investing in a good monitor can make a huge difference in your productivity and overall gaming experience.

While high-end gaming monitors are often out of budget for most people, there's no reason to sacrifice quality just because you're not a professional esports athlete. In this article, we'll explore the best budget gaming monitors that are perfect for home office use.

Key Insights

  • Refresh Rate Matters: A higher refresh rate can reduce eye strain and make gaming feel more immersive. Look for monitors with a minimum of 144Hz refresh rate.
  • Response Time is Crucial: Fast response times can help eliminate ghosting and blurring, making your gaming experience smoother. Aim for monitors with response times under 5ms.
  • IPS Panels are the Way to Go: IPS (In-Plane Switching) panels offer wider viewing angles and better color accuracy than TN panels, making them ideal for home office use.
