Been using an adjustable phone ring holder for half a year and wanted to share some insights:

Key findings:

2. Kickstand function saves desk space - Works great for video calls, watching content, or following recipes. Way more stable than propping against random objects

3. Grip security vs case compatibility - Strong adhesive is crucial, but make sure it works with your case material (struggled with textured cases initially)
