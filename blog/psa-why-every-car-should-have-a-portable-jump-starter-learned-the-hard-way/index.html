PSA: Why every car should have a portable jump starter (learned the hard way)

Published: February 22, 2026

emergencycarpreparednesssafety

Got stranded last month when my battery died in a parking garage with no cell service. Made me realize how vulnerable we are without backup power.

Key things I learned about portable jump starters:

• 12000mAh capacity can jump start most cars 10+ times on single charge - way more reliable than calling for help

• Dual purpose - also works as power bank for phones/devices during emergencies

• Compact size fits in glove compartment, unlike bulky jumper cables that need another car

Real game-changer for peace of mind, especially if you:

  • Park in remote areas
  • Have an older vehicle
  • Live somewhere with extreme weather
  • Travel frequently

Worth noting: look for ones with safety features like reverse polarity protection and overcharge prevention.

TL;DR: Portable jump starters are more reliable than jumper cables, work without another car, and double as emergency phone chargers. Essential car emergency kit item that takes up minimal space.
