Template 1: Welcome Email to New Client

  2. I’ll draft a project proposal outlining scope, timeline, and deliverables.
  3. Once approved, we’ll sign a contract and schedule our kickoff.

You’ll receive the questionnaire shortly. Feel free to reply to this email with any initial thoughts or files you’d like to share.

Looking forward to collaborating!

Best regards,

Template 2: Client Discovery Questionnaire

When to use: Send after the welcome email to gather essential project details, brand context, and client expectations.

Subject: A Few Quick Questions About Your [Project Name] Project

Hi [Client Name],

To ensure I deliver exactly what you’re looking for, I’d love to learn more about your vision, audience, and goals. Please fill out the questions below — the more detail, the better!

1. Project Overview

  • What is the main goal of this project? [e.g., rebrand, launch a new website, create marketing materials]
  • Who is your target audience? [Describe demographics, interests, pain points]
  • What message do you want this design to communicate?

2. Brand Context

  • Do you have an existing brand style guide? (Yes/No) If yes, please attach.
  • List 3 adjectives that describe your brand personality: [e.g., modern, trustworthy, playful]
  • What are your brand colors and fonts? [Provide HEX codes and font names if available]

3. Design Preferences

  • Share 3 examples of designs you love (URLs or images). What do you like about them?
  • Are there any designs you dislike? Why?
  • Do you have any must-have elements (e.g., logo placement, specific imagery)?

4. Logistics

  • What is your ideal project timeline?
  • Who will be the main point of contact for feedback and approvals?
  • Are there any technical or platform restrictions (e.g., CMS, file formats)?

Please reply to this email with your answers by [Due Date]. Let me know if you’d prefer a quick call instead!

Thanks,

[Your Name]
