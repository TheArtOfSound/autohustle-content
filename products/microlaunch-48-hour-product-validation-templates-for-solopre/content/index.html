Pre-Launch Planning & Idea Validation

Template 1: Rapid Idea Validation Framework

Purpose: Quickly assess if your product idea has market potential before investing time in development.

Instructions: Fill in each section honestly. If you can't complete a section easily, it may indicate a weak point in your idea that needs addressing.

#### Product Concept Overview

  • Product Name: [YOUR PRODUCT NAME]
  • One-Line Description: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT IN 10 WORDS OR LESS]
  • Target Problem: [WHAT SPECIFIC PROBLEM DOES THIS SOLVE?]
  • Solution Summary: [HOW DOES YOUR PRODUCT SOLVE THIS PROBLEM?]

#### Market Validation Checklist

  • Target Audience: [WHO SPECIFICALLY NEEDS THIS SOLUTION?]
  • Audience Size: [HOW MANY PEOPLE HAVE THIS PROBLEM? (ROUGH ESTIMATE)]
  • Pain Level: [RATE THE PROBLEM SEVERITY: 1-10, WHERE 10 = EXTREMELY PAINFUL]
  • Current Solutions: [WHAT ARE PEOPLE USING NOW TO SOLVE THIS?]
  • Solution Gaps: [WHAT'S MISSING FROM EXISTING SOLUTIONS?]

#### Validation Evidence

  • Personal Experience: [DO YOU PERSONALLY HAVE THIS PROBLEM? Y/N]
  • Community Mentions: [WHERE HAVE YOU SEEN PEOPLE DISCUSSING THIS PROBLEM?]
  • Search Volume: [KEYWORD SEARCH VOLUME FOR RELATED TERMS]
  • Competitor Analysis: [LIST 3 DIRECT OR INDIRECT COMPETITORS]

#### Quick Feasibility Check

  • Technical Complexity: [RATE 1-10, WHERE 1 = VERY SIMPLE TO BUILD]
  • Time to MVP: [HOW LONG TO BUILD A BASIC VERSION?]
  • Required Skills: [WHAT SKILLS DO YOU NEED? WHICH DO YOU HAVE?]
  • Initial Investment: [ESTIMATED COST TO GET STARTED]

Template 7: Customer Feedback Collection & Analysis

Purpose: Systematically gather and analyze customer feedback to guide product development.

Instructions: Use multiple feedback collection methods. Focus on understanding the "why" behind customer responses.

#### Feedback Collection Channels

Survey Questions (Send to all users)

1. How would you describe [PRODUCT NAME] to a friend?

2. What problem were you trying to solve when you found us?

3. How well did [PRODUCT NAME] solve your problem? (1-10 scale)

4. What almost prevented you from trying [PRODUCT NAME]?

5. What would make [PRODUCT NAME] significantly better?

Interview Questions (For select customers)

1. Walk me through the moment you realized you needed a solution like ours.

2. What other solutions did you try before finding us?

3. What was your first impression of [PRODUCT NAME]?

4. How has [PRODUCT NAME] changed your [WORK/LIFE/PROCESS]?

5. If [PRODUCT NAME] disappeared tomorrow, what would you miss most?
