18 Client Feedback Templates for Remote Design Teams

Updated: March 05, 2026

18 client feedback templates for remote design teams — streamline reviews, reduce back-and-forth, and get clearer input on design deliverables.

What's Inside

  • 18 customizable feedback templates
  • designed for remote workflows
  • reduces revision time and miscommunication

Template 1: Initial Project Brief Feedback

When to use: After sending the initial project brief to the client for review.

Subject: Feedback Request: Initial Project Brief - [PROJECT NAME]

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

Thank you for taking the time to review the initial project brief for [PROJECT NAME]. We've outlined our understanding of your project goals, target audience, and design requirements based on our initial discussions.

Please take a moment to provide your feedback using the following questions as a guide:

  1. Does the brief accurately capture your project goals and objectives? If not, please provide any missing details or corrections.
  2. Have we correctly identified your target audience and their needs? If not, please share any additional insights.
  3. Are the design requirements and constraints clear? If you have any additional requirements, please let us know.
  4. Are there any other aspects of the project that we should consider or address?

Please provide your feedback by [DEADLINE]. Once we've incorporated your input, we'll share the revised brief for your approval before moving forward with the design phase.

Looking forward to your feedback!

Best,

[YOUR NAME]
