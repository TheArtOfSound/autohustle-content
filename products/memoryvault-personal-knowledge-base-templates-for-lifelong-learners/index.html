Information Capture Templates

Template 1: Source Synthesis Sheet

Purpose: Capture key insights from books, articles, videos, and other learning materials

Best for: Research, professional development, academic study

```markdown

Source Synthesis: [SOURCE TITLE]

Source Details

  • Type: [Book/Article/Video/Podcast/Course]
  • Author/Creator: [AUTHOR NAME]
  • Date Accessed: [DATE]
  • Source URL/Citation: [FULL CITATION]
  • Recommended by: [PERSON/PLATFORM]

Key Concepts (3-5 main ideas)

  1. [CONCEPT NAME]: [Brief explanation in your own words]
  2. [CONCEPT NAME]: [Brief explanation in your own words]
  3. [CONCEPT NAME]: [Brief explanation in your own words]

Golden Quotes

> "[EXACT QUOTE 1]" - [Page/Timestamp]

> "[EXACT QUOTE 2]" - [Page/Timestamp]

Personal Insights

  • Surprised me: [What challenged your existing beliefs?]
  • Connects to: [How does this relate to other knowledge you have?]
  • Action items: [What will you do differently based on this?]

Future Reference

  • Tags: #[tag1] #[tag2] #[tag3]
  • Related sources: [Links to connected materials]
  • Follow-up questions: [What do you want to explore further?]

```

Instructions: Use this template immediately after consuming any significant piece of content. Fill out sections while the material is fresh in your mind. The "Personal Insights" section is crucial—it transforms passive consumption into active learning.

Template 2: Meeting Intelligence Capture

Purpose: Extract maximum value from meetings, conferences, and conversations

Best for: Professional networking, team meetings, expert interviews

```markdown

Meeting Intelligence: [MEETING TITLE/PURPOSE]

Context

  • Date & Time: [DATE, START-END TIME]
  • Participants: [LIST KEY PEOPLE]
  • Meeting Type: [1:1/Team Meeting/Conference/Workshop]
  • My Role: [Observer/Participant/Presenter]

Key Decisions Made

  1. [DECISION 1] - Owner: [PERSON] - Deadline: [DATE]
  2. [DECISION 2] - Owner: [PERSON] - Deadline: [DATE]

Valuable Insights Shared

  • [PERSON NAME] mentioned: [Insight and context]
  • [PERSON NAME] recommended: [Resource/approach/contact]
  • Industry trend discussed: [Trend and implications]

Questions That Emerged

  • [QUESTION 1] - [Who might have the answer?]
  • [QUESTION 2] - [Where can I research this?]

Action Items for Me

  • [ ] [SPECIFIC ACTION] by [DATE]
  • [ ] [SPECIFIC ACTION] by [DATE]
  • [ ] [SPECIFIC ACTION] by [DATE]

Network Intelligence

  • New connections: [Names and how to follow up]
  • Strengthened relationships: [Who did I connect with better?]
  • Future opportunities: [What doors might have opened?]

Tags & Links

  • Tags: #[meeting-type] #[project] #[topic]
  • Related notes: [Links to connected information]

```

Instructions: Complete this template within 24 hours of any important meeting or conversation. The "Network Intelligence" section is often overlooked but crucial for building professional relationships. Use the action items section to ensure insights translate into concrete next steps.
