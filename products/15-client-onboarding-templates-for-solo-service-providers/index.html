Here’s what’s next:

✅ You’ll receive your Client Onboarding Form shortly (just fill in a few details about your goals and preferences).

📁 All project files will be shared via [File Sharing Platform, e.g., Google Drive].

Warmly,

Template 3: Freelance Contract Template

When to use: Before work begins, to formalize scope, payment, and terms

Independent Contractor Agreement

This agreement is made between [Your Full Name], operating as [Your Business Name] (“Contractor”), and [Client Full Name], (“Client”), effective as of [Effective Date].

1. Services

Contractor agrees to provide the following services:

  • [Service 1, e.g., Website Design]
  • [Service 2, e.g., Brand Style Guide Development]
  • [Service 3, e.g., 3 Revisions per Design]

Detailed scope: [Insert brief project description or attach separate Scope of Work].

2. Project Timeline

  • Start Date: [Start Date]
  • Estimated Completion: [End Date]
  • Milestones: [List key deliverables and due dates]

3. Compensation

Total Project Fee: [Total Amount]

Payment Schedule:

  • [Percentage]% ([Amount]) due upon signing (deposit)
  • [Percentage]% ([Amount]) due on [Milestone Date]
  • Final [Percentage]% ([Amount]) due upon project completion

Payments made via: [Payment Method, e.g., Bank Transfer, PayPal]

4. Revisions & Edits

Client is entitled to [Number] rounds of revisions per deliverable. Additional revisions billed at [Hourly Rate] per hour.

5. Ownership & Deliverables

Final deliverables will be provided in [File Formats] upon full payment. Copyright transfers to Client upon final payment. Contractor retains the right to display work in portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

6. Cancellation

If Client cancels:

  • Before work begins: Deposit is non-refundable.
  • After work begins: Client pays for time and materials incurred.

7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree not to disclose sensitive business information shared during the project.

8. Signatures

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms of this agreement.

___________________________

[Your Full Name]

[Your Business Name]

Date: [Date]

___________________________

[Client Full Name]

[Client Company (if applicable)]

Date: [Date]

Template 4: Project Kickoff Call Agenda

When to use: To structure your first meeting with the client after onboarding

Project Kickoff Meeting Agenda

Project: [Project Name]

Client: [Client Name]

Date: [Meeting Date]

Time: [Start Time] – [End Time]

Location: [Video Call Link or In-Person Address]

Objective: Align on project goals, expectations, timeline, and communication

1. Welcome & Introductions (5 min)

  • Brief personal intro
  • Confirm main point of contact

2. Project Overview (10 min)

  • Recap of agreed scope (from contract)
  • Review deliverables and formats

3. Goals & Success Metrics (10 min)

  • What does the client want to achieve?
  • How will we measure success?

4. Target Audience & Brand Alignment (10 min)

  • Review brand voice, style, or preferences
  • Discuss audience needs

5. Timeline & Milestones (10 min)

  • Walk through key dates
  • Confirm approval turnaround expectations (e.g., 3 business days)

6. Communication Plan (5 min)

  • Preferred communication channels (email, Slack, etc.)
  • Response time expectations
  • Meeting frequency (e.g., biweekly check-ins)

7. Next Steps (5 min)

  • What you’ll deliver first and when
  • What client needs to provide (e.g., content, feedback)

Action Items:

  • [ ] [Task] – Owner: [Name] – Due: [Date]
  • [ ] [Task] – Owner: [Name] – Due: [Date]

Thank you! Looking forward to a successful collaboration.

[Your Name]

[Your Business Name]

Template 6: Scope of Work (SOW) Document

When to use: To clearly define project deliverables, timeline, and responsibilities

Scope of Work

Project: [Project Name]

Client: [Client Name]

Prepared by: [Your Name], [Your Business Name]

Date: [Date]

Project Overview

This project involves [brief description of services, e.g., designing a responsive website for a coaching business]. The goal is to [state primary objective, e.g., increase lead generation through a professional online presence].

Deliverables

You will receive:

  • [Deliverable 1, e.g., 1 Homepage Design]
  • [Deliverable 2, e.g., 3 Inner Page Designs]
  • [Deliverable 3, e.g., Mobile-Responsive Layout]
  • [Deliverable 4, e.g., Final Files in PNG, PDF, and Figma]

Project Timeline

| Milestone | Due Date |

|---------|----------|

| Initial Concepts | [Date] |

| First Draft Delivery | [Date] |

| Client Feedback Due | [Date] |

| Final Revisions | [Date] |

| Project Completion | [Date] |

Client Responsibilities

To keep the project on track, the client agrees to:

  • Provide all necessary content (text, images, logos) by [Content Deadline]
  • Respond to feedback requests within [Number] business days
  • Designate one primary point of contact

Assumptions

  • Final content will be provided on time
  • Revisions limited to [Number] rounds per deliverable
  • Project starts upon signed agreement and deposit receipt

Exclusions

This project does not include:

  • Copywriting (unless specified)
  • Stock photo licensing
  • Ongoing maintenance or updates post-launch

Approved by:
