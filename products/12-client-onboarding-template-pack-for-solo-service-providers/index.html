Template 7: Independent Contractor Agreement (Simplified)

When to use: As a legally sound contract for solo service providers to protect both parties

Independent Contractor Agreement

This Agreement is made between:

Client: [Client Full Name or Company], with principal address at [Client Address]

Contractor: [Your Full Name], doing business as [Your Business Name], with principal address at [Your Address]

Effective Date: [Start Date]

1. Services

Contractor agrees to provide the following services:

[Detailed description of services, e.g., “Design a responsive website homepage in Figma”]

As outlined in the Project Scope document dated [Date].

2. Compensation

Client agrees to pay Contractor a total fee of [Amount] according to the payment schedule in the Payment Terms document.

3. Project Timeline

Work will begin on [Start Date] and is expected to be completed by [End Date], subject to timely feedback and deliverables from Client.

4. Revisions

[Number] rounds of revisions are included. Additional revisions billed at [Hourly Rate] per hour.

5. Ownership & Rights

Upon full payment, Contractor assigns all rights, title, and interest in the final deliverables to Client.

Contractor retains the right to display work in their portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

6. Confidentiality

Both parties agree not to disclose confidential information received during the project.

7. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with [Number] days’ written notice. Client is responsible for payment of all services rendered up to termination date.

8. Independent Contractor Status

Contractor is not an employee. Contractor is responsible for their own taxes and insurance.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of [State/Country].

Signatures

___________________________

[Client Full Name]

Date: [Date]

___________________________

Template 9: Confidentiality Agreement

When to use: To protect sensitive client information and maintain confidentiality.

Confidentiality Agreement for [PROJECT NAME]

This Confidentiality Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on [DATE] ("Effective Date") by and between [COMPANY NAME] ("Service Provider") and [CLIENT NAME] ("Client").

  1. Definition of Confidential Information:

For the purpose of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" means all information, whether written or oral, that is disclosed by the Client to the Service Provider, including but not limited to [CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION, e.g., business strategies, customer lists, financial data].

  1. Obligations of the Service Provider:

The Service Provider agrees to:

  • Hold the Confidential Information in confidence and not disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the Client
  • Use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing the services outlined in the project scope statement
  • Take reasonable precautions to protect the Confidential Information, including [PRECAUTIONS, e.g., encrypting sensitive data, using secure communication channels]
  1. Exclusions:

The obligations of confidentiality shall not apply to any information that:

  • Is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the Service Provider
  • Is already known to the Service Provider at the time of disclosure
  • Is disclosed with the prior written consent of the Client
  • Is required to be disclosed by law or court order

By signing below, we acknowledge that we have read, understand, and agree to the terms of this Confidentiality Agreement.
