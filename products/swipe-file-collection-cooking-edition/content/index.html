Cooking Techniques and Tips

-------------------------------

Cooking Methods

#### 1. Stovetop

  • Use a non-stick pan or cast iron skillet for even heat distribution.
  • Cook at medium-high heat to achieve a nice sear.

#### 2. Oven

  • Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  • Cook for 20-30 minutes, depending on the dish.

Kitchen Essentials

#### 1. Pots and Pans

  • Choose non-reactive materials (e.g., stainless steel or cast iron) for cooking.
  • Don't overcrowd the pot or pan; cook in batches if necessary.

#### 2. Utensils

  • Use silicone, wooden, or plastic utensils for safe handling.
  • Avoid using metal utensils that can scratch non-stick coatings.

Common Issues

#### 1. Food Safety
