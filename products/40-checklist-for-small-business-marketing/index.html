40 Checklist for Small Business Marketing

Updated: March 05, 2026

40 checklists for small business marketing — covers social media, email, SEO, and more.

What's Inside

  • 40 comprehensive checklists
  • Covers digital and traditional marketing
  • Easy to follow steps
  • [ ] Item 1: Define the mission statement and unique value proposition of the business
  • [ ] Item 2: Develop a consistent brand voice and tone across all marketing channels
  • [ ] Item 3: Create a style guide for logos, typography, and color schemes
  • [ ] Item 4: Design a professional website with clear navigation and mobile responsiveness

  • [ ] Item 6: Set up social media accounts on relevant platforms (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram, etc.)

  • [ ] Item 8: Create high-quality, engaging content (blog posts, videos, infographics, etc.)
  • [ ] Item 9: Optimize website and social media content for SEO keywords
  • [ ] Item 10: Build an email list through opt-in forms on the website and social media
  • [ ] Item 11: Design a welcome email sequence for new subscribers
  • [ ] Item 12: Set up email marketing automation tools (e.g., Mailchimp, Constant Contact)
  • [ ] Item 13: Develop a lead magnet to capture email addresses (e.g., eBook, webinar, free trial)
  • [ ] Item 14: Create a sales funnel to guide leads through the conversion process
  • [ ] Item 15: Set up and track key performance indicators (KPIs) for website and social media
  • [ ] Item 16: Conduct keyword research to inform SEO and content strategies
  • [ ] Item 17: Optimize website images and videos for faster loading speeds

  • [ ] Item 26: Create a referral program to incentivize customer referrals
  • [ ] Item 27: Develop a customer loyalty program to reward repeat customers
  • [ ] Item 28: Set up affiliate marketing programs to partner with other businesses
  • [ ] Item 29: Conduct A/B testing and experimentation to optimize marketing channels
  • [ ] Item 30: Analyze and report on website and social media analytics (e.g., Google Analytics)
  • [ ] Item 31: Track email open rates, click-through rates, and conversion rates
  • [ ] Item 32: Use data and analytics to inform marketing strategy and decision-making
  • [ ] Item 33: Develop a content repurposing strategy to maximize content value
  • [ ] Item 34: Create a video marketing strategy (e.g., YouTube, Facebook Watch, etc.)
  • [ ] Item 35: Develop a podcasting strategy to reach new audiences
  • [ ] Item 36: Set up and optimize online directories and citations (e.g., Yelp, Bing)
  • [ ] Item 37: Create a reputation management plan to address online reviews and feedback
  • [ ] Item 38: Develop a partnerships and collaborations strategy to work with other businesses
  • [ ] Item 39: Set up and track customer feedback and survey tools (e.g., SurveyMonkey)
