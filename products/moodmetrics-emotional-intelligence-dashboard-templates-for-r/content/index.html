Emotionally Intelligent Feedback Framework

Purpose: Structure for giving and receiving feedback with emotional awareness

Frequency: As needed for feedback sessions

```markdown

EI-Based Feedback Session

Date: [DATE]

Participants: [FEEDBACK GIVER] ‚Üí [FEEDBACK RECEIVER]

Topic: [FEEDBACK TOPIC]

Pre-Feedback Emotional Check:

  • Giver's emotional state: [DESCRIPTION]
  • Receiver's emotional state: [DESCRIPTION]
  • Environmental factors: [FACTORS AFFECTING CONVERSATION]

Feedback Structure:

1. Emotional Context Setting

"I want to share some feedback with you about [TOPIC]. I'm feeling [EMOTION] about this conversation because [REASON]. How are you feeling right now about receiving feedback on this topic?"

Receiver response: [RESPONSE]

2. Specific Situation Description

"In [SPECIFIC SITUATION/DATE], I observed [SPECIFIC BEHAVIOR/ACTION]. The impact was [SPECIFIC IMPACT]."

3. Emotional Impact Sharing

"When this happened, I felt [EMOTION] because [REASON]. I imagine others might have felt [ANTICIPATED EMOTIONS] because [REASONING]."

4. Collaborative Problem-Solving

"What was your experience of this situation? How were you feeling? What factors contributed to this outcome?"

Receiver perspective: [RESPONSE]

5. Future-Focused Solutions

"Going forward, what could we do differently? What support do you need? How can we prevent similar emotional impacts?"

Agreed solutions:

  • [SOLUTION 1]
  • [SOLUTION 2]
  • [SOLUTION 3]

Emotional Check-Out:

  • How is the receiver feeling now? [RESPONSE]
  • What emotions came up during this conversation? [EMOTIONS]
  • What support is needed moving forward? [SUPPORT NEEDED]

Follow-up Plan:

  • Check-in date: [DATE]
  • Success indicators: [INDICATORS]
  • Additional support: [SUPPORT ITEMS]

```

Instructions: Use this framework for all significant feedback conversations. Allow extra time for emotional processing. Focus on collaborative problem-solving rather than criticism. Schedule follow-up within one week.
