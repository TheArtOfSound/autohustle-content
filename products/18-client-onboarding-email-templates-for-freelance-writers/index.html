18 Client Onboarding Email Templates for Freelance Writers

Updated: March 05, 2026

18 ready-to-use email templates for freelance writers to onboard clients quickly — from welcome messages to project setup requests.

What's Inside

  • 18 customizable email templates
  • covers project kickoff, info collection, and next steps
  • professionally worded for clear client expectations

Template 1: Welcome Aboard

When to use: After a client signs a contract or agrees to work with you.

Subject: Welcome Aboard, [CLIENT NAME]! Let's Get Started

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

I'm thrilled to have you on board! I'm looking forward to working together and helping you achieve your [SPECIFIC GOAL, e.g., content marketing objectives].

To get started, I've outlined a few next steps for us:

  1. Please review and sign the attached contract (if not already done).
  2. Let's schedule a quick kickoff call to discuss your project in detail. Here's my [CALENDLY LINK/OTHER SCHEDULING TOOL].
  3. If you have any brand guidelines, existing content, or inspirational materials, please share them with me.

I'm excited to collaborate and create something amazing together!

Best,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 2: Kickoff Call Agenda

When to use: Before your initial call with a new client.

Subject: Agenda for Our Kickoff Call on [DATE] at [TIME]

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

I'm looking forward to our call on [DATE] at [TIME]. To make the most of our time, I've prepared an agenda:

  1. Project Overview (10 min)
  • Your objectives and expectations
  • Target audience and desired outcomes
  1. Project Details (15 min)
  • Scope, deadlines, and deliverables
  • Any specific guidelines or preferences
  1. Next Steps (5 min)
  • Immediate action items and responsibilities

Please review the attached project brief (if applicable) and come prepared with any questions or additional information.

Looking forward to our conversation!

Best,

[YOUR NAME]

When to use: When you need more information about a project to provide an accurate quote or start working.

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

  1. Project Details

  • [QUESTION 2, e.g., Who is the target audience?]
  • [QUESTION 3, e.g., What are the key messages or topics to cover?]
  1. Project Requirements
  • [QUESTION 4, e.g., What is the desired tone or style?]
  • [QUESTION 5, e.g., Are there any specific guidelines or preferences?]
  • [QUESTION 6, e.g., What is the desired length or format?]

Once I have this information, I can provide a more accurate quote and timeline.

Looking forward to your response!

Best,

[YOUR NAME]
