BurnoutShield: Mental Health Check-In Templates for High-Performance Remote Teams

Introduction

Remote work has transformed how we collaborate, but it's also created new challenges for maintaining team mental health and preventing burnout. BurnoutShield provides evidence-based templates designed specifically for remote team managers and HR professionals who want to proactively support their team's wellbeing while maintaining high performance standards.

These templates are built on psychological safety principles and incorporate best practices from organizational psychology, helping you create a culture where mental health is prioritized alongside productivity.

Quick Daily Check-In Templates

Template 1: The 3-Minute Energy Check

Purpose: Quick daily pulse check to gauge team energy and identify potential issues early.

Instructions: Use this template in your daily standup or send as a Slack form. Keep responses anonymous to encourage honesty.

```

Daily Energy Check - [DATE]

Team: [TEAM NAME]

On a scale of 1-10, how would you rate your:

  • Energy level today: [1-10]
  • Stress level: [1-10]
  • Workload manageability: [1-10]
  • Connection with team: [1-10]

One word to describe how you're feeling right now: [WORD]

Is there anything blocking your productivity today?

☐ Technical issues

☐ Unclear priorities

☐ Personal challenges

☐ Workload concerns

☐ Communication gaps

☐ Nothing major

☐ Other: [SPECIFY]

Would you benefit from:

☐ A 15-minute check-in with manager

☐ Help prioritizing tasks

☐ Technical support

☐ Just wanted to share

☐ Nothing needed right now

```

Weekly Team Wellness Assessments

Template 3: Comprehensive Team Wellness Survey

Purpose: Weekly deep-dive into team wellness across multiple dimensions.

Instructions: Deploy every Friday via survey tool. Review responses before Monday planning. Flag scores below 6 for follow-up.

```

Weekly Team Wellness Assessment

Week of: [DATE RANGE]

Team: [TEAM NAME]

Manager: [MANAGER NAME]

WORKLOAD & CAPACITY

Rate your agreement (1-10):

  • My workload is manageable: [1-10]
  • I have enough time to do quality work: [1-10]
  • I can maintain work-life boundaries: [1-10]
  • I feel supported when overwhelmed: [1-10]

TEAM DYNAMICS

Rate your experience (1-10):

  • I feel psychologically safe on this team: [1-10]
  • Communication is clear and timely: [1-10]
  • I feel valued for my contributions: [1-10]
  • Conflicts are handled constructively: [1-10]

REMOTE WORK EXPERIENCE

Rate your satisfaction (1-10):

  • My home workspace supports productivity: [1-10]
  • I feel connected despite being remote: [1-10]
  • I have the tools/tech I need: [1-10]
  • I can focus without interruption: [1-10]

GROWTH & ENGAGEMENT

Rate your agreement (1-10):

  • I'm learning and growing in my role: [1-10]
  • My work feels meaningful: [1-10]
  • I see a clear path forward: [1-10]
  • I'm excited about upcoming projects: [1-10]

What's working well for you right now?

[OPEN RESPONSE]

What's your biggest concern or frustration?

[OPEN RESPONSE]

What would improve your experience next week?

[OPEN RESPONSE]

```
