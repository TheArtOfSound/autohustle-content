Template 1: Welcome Email

When to use: Immediately after a client signs a contract to introduce yourself and set expectations.

Subject: Welcome to [PROJECT NAME]! Let's Get Started

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

Welcome to [PROJECT NAME]! I'm thrilled to be working with you and can't wait to create something amazing together.

First, I want to thank you for choosing me for this project. I'm confident that we'll create something truly special.

To get started, I've outlined a few next steps and some information I'll need from you:

  1. Project Timeline: Our project is scheduled to kick off on [START DATE] and will run until [END DATE]. Please let me know if these dates work for you or if we need to adjust them.
  1. Initial Information: To ensure we're on the same page, I've prepared a questionnaire for you to fill out. You can find it here: [QUESTIONNAIRE LINK]. Please complete it at your earliest convenience.
  1. Communication: I'll be using [COMMUNICATION TOOL, e.g., Slack, Email] for day-to-day communication. I'll also send weekly progress updates via email.
  1. Feedback: I value your input and will be seeking your feedback throughout the project. I'll provide clear instructions on how and when to provide feedback as we progress.

If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm here to help!

Looking forward to an exciting journey together.

Best,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 4: Onboarding Workflow

When to use: To outline the onboarding process and set clear expectations for both parties.

Onboarding Workflow: [PROJECT NAME]

1. Contract Signing

  • Client signs the contract and returns it to me.

2. Welcome Email

  • I send a welcome email to the client outlining next steps.

3. Client Questionnaire

  • Client completes and returns the questionnaire.

4. Project Brief

  • I create a project brief based on the questionnaire and send it to the client for review and approval.

5. Kickoff Meeting

  • We schedule and hold a kickoff meeting to discuss the project in detail and answer any questions.

6. Design Process

  • I begin the design process, providing regular updates and seeking feedback as we go.

7. Revisions

  • Based on client feedback, I make revisions to the design.

8. Final Approval

  • Client provides final approval of the design.

9. Delivery

  • I deliver the final files to the client.

10. Project Completion

  • We review the project, gather feedback, and discuss any potential next steps.

Template 5: Kickoff Meeting Agenda

When to use: To structure the kickoff meeting and ensure all important topics are covered.

Kickoff Meeting Agenda: [PROJECT NAME]

1. Welcome and Introductions (5 minutes)

  • Brief introductions and icebreaker.

2. Project Overview (10 minutes)

  • Review of project goals, scope, and deliverables.

3. Timeline and Milestones (10 minutes)

  • Review of project timeline and key milestones.

4. Roles and Responsibilities (5 minutes)

  • Clear definition of roles and responsibilities for both parties.

5. Communication Plan (5 minutes)

  • Discussion of communication tools, frequency, and protocols.

6. Design Process (10 minutes)

  • Explanation of the design process, including how feedback will be incorporated.

7. Questions and Clarifications (10 minutes)

  • Open forum for any questions or clarifications.

8. Next Steps (5 minutes)

  • Summary of next steps and action items.

9. Wrap-up (5 minutes)

  • Thank you and adjournment.
