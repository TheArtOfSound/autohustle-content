6. Compliance & Legal Documentation Checklist

Purpose: Ensure all legal requirements and compliance standards are met for contractor engagement.

Instructions: Complete this checklist before contractor start date. Consult legal counsel for jurisdiction-specific requirements.

```markdown

Compliance & Legal Documentation Checklist

Contractor: [CONTRACTOR_NAME]

Company: [COMPANY_NAME]

Engagement Start: [START_DATE]

Jurisdiction: [LEGAL_JURISDICTION]

Contract Documentation

  • [ ] Independent Contractor Agreement signed and dated
  • Scope of work clearly defined
  • Payment terms specified: [PAYMENT_TERMS]
  • Intellectual property rights addressed
  • Termination clauses included
  • [ ] Statement of Work (SOW) attached
  • Deliverables outlined: [DELIVERABLE_LIST]
  • Timeline specified: [PROJECT_TIMELINE]
  • Acceptance criteria defined
  • [ ] Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) executed
  • Confidentiality terms agreed upon
  • Data protection requirements specified
  • Duration: [NDA_DURATION]

Tax & Financial Compliance

  • [ ] Form W-9 completed (US contractors)
  • [ ] Tax ID/SSN verified: [TAX_ID]
  • [ ] 1099 Reporting setup initiated
  • [ ] Payment Method established: [PAYMENT_METHOD]
  • [ ] Invoice Requirements communicated: [INVOICE_INSTRUCTIONS]

Data Protection & Security

  • [ ] Data Processing Agreement signed (if handling personal data)
  • [ ] Security Protocols acknowledged
  • [ ] Access Permissions documented: [ACCESS_LEVELS]
  • [ ] Device Security requirements communicated
  • [ ] VPN/Security Software installed (if required)

Industry-Specific Compliance

  • [ ] Professional Certifications verified: [CERTIFICATIONS]
  • [ ] Background Check completed (if required)
  • [ ] Industry Regulations compliance confirmed: [SPECIFIC_REGULATIONS]
  • [ ] Insurance Requirements met: [INSURANCE_TYPE]

International Contractors (if applicable)

  • [ ] Work Authorization verified
  • [ ] Tax Treaty benefits documented
  • [ ] Currency/Exchange considerations addressed
  • [ ] Local Labor Laws compliance confirmed
  • [ ] Time Zone documentation: [CONTRACTOR_TIMEZONE]

Record Keeping

  • [ ] Digital Copies stored in: [STORAGE_LOCATION]
  • [ ] Physical Copies filed in: [FILING_LOCATION]
  • [ ] Access Permissions set for HR team
  • [ ] Retention Schedule applied: [RETENTION_PERIOD]
  • [ ] Backup Copies created and secured

Ongoing Compliance Monitoring

  • [ ] Performance Reviews scheduled: [REVIEW_FREQUENCY]
  • [ ] Contract Renewals calendar reminder set
  • [ ] Compliance Training completed: [TRAINING_MODULES]
  • [ ] Regular Audits planned: [AUDIT_SCHEDULE]

Approval Sign-offs

  • [ ] Legal Review: [LEGAL_REVIEWER] - Date: [DATE]
  • [ ] HR Approval: [HR_REPRESENTATIVE] - Date: [DATE]
  • [ ] Finance Approval: [FINANCE_REPRESENTATIVE] - Date: [DATE]

Compliance Officer: [COMPLIANCE_OFFICER_NAME]
```
