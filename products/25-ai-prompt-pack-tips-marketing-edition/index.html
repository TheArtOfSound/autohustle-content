25 AI Prompt Pack Tips: Marketing Edition

Updated: March 10, 2026

25 practical prompt pack items for marketing professionals.

What's Inside

  • practical tips
  • ready to use
  • expert curated
  1. Generate a high-converting email subject line for a limited-time discount on our premium skincare line. Include urgency and emotional appeal while keeping it under 50 characters.
  1. Write a persuasive product description for a new wireless earbud launch, focusing on sound quality, comfort, and battery life. Use benefit-driven language tailored to tech-savvy millennials.
  1. Create three engaging social media captions for a fitness brand’s new activewear collection. Each caption should target a different platform: Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn, using appropriate tone and hashtags.
  1. Develop a content calendar for a 7-day email nurture sequence promoting a free marketing webinar. Include email subject lines, primary CTA, and key messaging for each stage of the buyer’s journey.
  1. Suggest 10 blog topic ideas targeting small business owners interested in improving their digital marketing. Focus on SEO, social media, and email marketing, with long-tail keywords included.
  1. Rewrite this marketing copy to be more concise and action-oriented: “Our platform helps businesses grow by providing tools that improve efficiency.” Make it suitable for a homepage hero section.
  1. Generate a list of 15 high-impact keywords for a PPC campaign promoting online MBA programs. Prioritize commercial intent and include a mix of short-tail and long-tail variations.
  1. Craft a compelling value proposition for a SaaS company offering AI-powered customer support software. Highlight speed, cost savings, and integration ease in under 30 words.
  1. Write a follow-up email to be sent 48 hours after a user downloads a lead magnet. Include a personalized tone, one key insight from the resource, and a soft upsell to a paid service.

10. Analyze this customer persona: “Sarah, 34, marketing manager at a mid-sized tech firm, busy, values time-saving tools, seeks career growth.” Recommend three content types and distribution channels to engage her.

11. Create a script for a 60-second explainer video introducing a meal kit delivery service. Focus on convenience, health benefits, and family appeal. Include visual cues and voiceover narration.

12. Develop a crisis communication response to a negative viral social media post about delayed product deliveries. Maintain brand empathy, transparency, and a clear resolution path.

13. Generate five attention-grabbing headlines for a landing page promoting a free SEO audit tool. Use power words and psychological triggers like curiosity and scarcity.

14. Write a customer testimonial template for B2B clients to fill out after completing a project. Include prompts for results, experience, and recommendation likelihood.

15. Optimize this Google Ads headline: “Get Marketing Help Today” — make it more specific, benefit-focused, and aligned with high-intent search queries.

16. Design a referral program framework for a subscription-based fitness app. Include incentive structure, messaging, and onboarding steps for both referrer and referee.

17. Create a competitive analysis matrix comparing our brand’s social media presence to three top competitors. Evaluate content themes, engagement rates, posting frequency, and follower growth.

18. Write a press release announcing a strategic partnership between our fintech startup and a major banking institution. Include quotes, company backgrounds, and mutual benefits.

19. Generate a set of 10 interactive poll questions for Instagram Stories targeting eco-conscious shoppers. Focus on sustainability habits, brand values, and product preferences.

20. Develop a lead scoring model based on these behaviors: website visits, content downloads, email opens, and demo requests. Assign point values and define MQL thresholds.

21. Craft a personalized LinkedIn outreach message to connect with HR directors for a B2B HR software demo. Emphasize efficiency gains and employee retention improvements.

22. Rewrite a stale product page for a cloud storage solution by incorporating storytelling elements, customer pain points, and clear differentiators from competitors.

23. Create a quarterly content strategy outline for a travel blog transitioning into affiliate marketing. Include content pillars, monetization tactics, and KPIs for each quarter.

24. Generate a customer journey map for a first-time buyer of organic coffee pods. Identify touchpoints, emotional states, potential obstacles, and optimization opportunities.

25. Write a performance review template for a digital marketing specialist. Include sections for campaign results, skill development, goal achievement, and growth recommendations.
