  • Lighting: ☐ Natural ☐ Artificial ☐ Mixed – Quality: [BRIGHT / DIM / HARSH]
  • Noise Level: ☐ Quiet ☐ Moderate ☐ Loud – Background Noise: [e.g., Traffic, TV]
  • Distraction Rating (1–5): ☐1 ☐2 ☐3 ☐4 ☐5

🪑 Ergonomics Check:

  • Chair Support: [YES / NO] – Do I feel back pain? ☐ Yes ☐ No
  • Desk Height: ☐ Correct ☐ Too High ☐ Too Low
  • Monitor Position: ☐ Eye Level ☐ Below ☐ Above – Distance: [APPROX. INCHES]
  • Keyboard/Mouse: ☐ Comfortable ☐ Causes strain

🔌 Tech & Tools Review:

  • Internet Speed (Tested): Download – [MBPS], Upload – [MBPS]
  • Backup System: ☐ Cloud ☐ External Drive ☐ None
  • Charging Stations: [LOCATION – e.g., Desk, kitchen]
  • Devices Used Daily: [LIST – e.g., Laptop, iPad, headphones]

⚡ Energy & Focus Factors:

  • Ventilation: ☐ Good ☐ Poor – Air quality feels: [FRESH / STALE]
  • Personal Items for Motivation: [e.g., Plants, photos, vision board] – Present? ☐ Yes ☐ No
  • Clutter Level: ☐ Minimal ☐ Moderate ☐ High

🔧 Improvement Plan:

  • [SHORT-TERM ACTION – e.g., Buy cable organizer]
  • [MEDIUM-TERM ACTION – e.g., Upgrade chair]
  • [LONG-TERM ACTION – e.g., Design dedicated workspace]
