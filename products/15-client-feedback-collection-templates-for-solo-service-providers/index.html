Template 4: Service Review Request for Testimonials

When to use: When you’re ready to request a public testimonial or case study contribution

Hi [Client Name],

It was such a pleasure helping you [achieve specific outcome, e.g., launch your new brand, streamline your email workflow, etc.] through [Project Name]. I’m thrilled with how it turned out — hope you are too!

If you’re happy with the results, I’d be honored if you could share a few sentences about your experience. Your words might help other [ideal client type] find the right support.

Here are a few prompts to get you started (no need to answer all):

  • What challenge were you facing before we started?
  • What did you appreciate about working with me?
  • What changed or improved after the project?

Example:

“Working with [Your Name] transformed my [specific area]. They were [adjective], [adjective], and delivered exactly what I needed — on time and with clarity. I’d recommend them to any [ideal client] looking for [specific benefit].”

Feel free to reply directly — I’ll never edit your words without permission.

Gratefully,

[Your Name]

Template 8: Post-Project Survey

When to use: After completing a project to gather general feedback from the client.

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

We hope you're enjoying the final result of our recent project together. As a solo service provider, your feedback is invaluable in helping us improve our services. Please take a few minutes to complete this survey:

  1. What were your initial expectations from our project, and did we meet them? [EXPECTATIONS]
  2. How would you rate the quality of work delivered? [RATING SCALE, e.g., 1-5]
  3. Were there any challenges or issues during the project? If yes, how were they resolved? [CHALLENGES AND RESOLUTION]
  4. How likely are you to recommend our services to others? [RATING SCALE, e.g., 1-5]
  5. Do you have any suggestions for how we can improve our services? [SUGGESTIONS]

Thank you for your time, and we look forward to the possibility of working together again in the future.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 10: Service Satisfaction Questionnaire

As we strive to continuously improve our services, your honest feedback is essential. Please take a moment to share your thoughts:

  1. Which of our services have you used? (Check all that apply) [SERVICE OPTIONS, e.g., web design, content creation]
  2. How would you rate the overall quality of these services? [RATING SCALE, e.g., 1-5]
  3. What did you like most about our services? [POSITIVE ASPECTS]
  4. Were there any aspects of our services that did not meet your expectations? If yes, please describe. [AREAS FOR IMPROVEMENT]
  5. How do our services compare to your initial expectations? [COMPARISON TO EXPECTATIONS]

Thank you for helping us refine our offerings.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 12: Net Promoter Score (NPS) Survey

When to use: To measure client loyalty and satisfaction with a single, straightforward question.

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

We're committed to delivering exceptional service and value to our clients. To help us gauge how we're doing, could you please answer the following question:

On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend our services to a friend or colleague? [NPS RATING]

Follow-up question for those who didn't give a perfect score: What could we do to improve our services and reach a perfect score for you? [IMPROVEMENT SUGGESTIONS]

Thank you for your honesty. Your feedback is invaluable.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]
