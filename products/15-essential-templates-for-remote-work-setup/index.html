Updated: March 05, 2026

15 essential templates to help remote workers and freelancers set up and organize their workspace, including templates for time tracking, project management, and goal setting.

Template 1: Remote Work Agreement

When to use: When starting a new remote work arrangement with a client or team to outline expectations and responsibilities.

This Remote Work Agreement ("Agreement") is made on [DATE] by and between [CLIENT/COMPANY NAME] (the "Client") and [REMOTE WORKER/FREELANCER NAME] (the "Remote Worker").

  1. Scope of Work: The Remote Worker will perform the following services: [DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES].
  2. Work Schedule: The Remote Worker will work [NUMBER] hours per week, with a schedule of [DAYS AND HOURS].
  3. Communication: The Remote Worker will be available for communication through [METHODS OF COMMUNICATION, e.g. email, phone, Slack].
  4. Confidentiality: The Remote Worker will maintain the confidentiality of all Client information and materials.
  5. Payment: The Client will pay the Remote Worker [RATE] per [TIME PERIOD] for services rendered.

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to the terms of this Agreement.
