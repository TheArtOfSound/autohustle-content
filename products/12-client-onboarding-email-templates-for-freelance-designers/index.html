12 Client Onboarding Email Templates for Freelance Designers

Updated: March 04, 2026

12 client onboarding email templates for freelance designers — ready to use for project start, information gathering, and initial feedback cycles.

What's Inside

  • 12 customizable email templates
  • covers project kickoff, brief collection, and feedback requests
  • designed for clear, professional client interactions

Template 1: Welcome & Project Kickoff

When to use: When a client signs a contract or agrees to start a project.

Subject: 🎉 Welcome Aboard! Let's Kickstart [Project Name]

Hi [Client's Name],

Welcome! I'm thrilled to work with you on [Project Name]. I'm confident that together, we'll create something amazing.

To get started, I've outlined a few next steps:

  1. Project Timeline: Our project timeline is [timeline]. I'll send over a detailed schedule shortly.
  2. Communication: I'll be sending regular updates via email. For urgent matters, you can reach me at [phone number].
  3. Feedback: I'll be seeking your feedback at key milestones. Your input is invaluable to me.

Please review our contract and let me know if you have any questions. I'm here to make this process as smooth as possible.

Looking forward to an amazing collaboration!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 2: Information Gathering

When to use: When you need to gather initial information from the client.

Subject: 📝 A Few Questions to Get Us Started

Hi [Client's Name],

To ensure I create a design that truly reflects your vision, I need to gather some information. Please take a moment to answer the following questions:

  1. What are the primary goals of [Project Name]?
  2. Who is the target audience for this project?
  3. Are there any specific design styles, colors, or fonts you prefer?
  4. Do you have any inspiration or reference materials you'd like to share?
  5. Are there any specific functionalities or features you require?

Your answers will help me tailor the design to your needs. Looking forward to your response!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 3: Design Brief Approval

When to use: After sending the design brief and waiting for client approval.

Subject: 📄 Design Brief: Your Review & Approval Needed

Hi [Client's Name],

I've prepared the design brief for [Project Name] based on our initial discussion. Please review the attached document at your earliest convenience.

The design brief outlines the project scope, objectives, target audience, and design requirements. Your feedback is crucial to ensure we're on the same page.

Please let me know if you approve the brief or if there are any changes you'd like to discuss. I'm here to make sure this project meets your expectations.

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 4: Initial Concept Sharing

When to use: When you're ready to share the first draft or concept with the client.

Subject: 🎨 Initial Concepts for [Project Name]

Hi [Client's Name],

I'm excited to share the initial concepts for [Project Name]. Please find the designs attached.

These are rough drafts and meant to give you an idea of the direction I'm taking. I've included [number] concepts to provide a range of options.

Please review the designs and let me know your thoughts. I'm particularly interested in:

  1. Which concepts resonate with you the most?
  2. Are there any elements you'd like to see more or less of?
  3. Any specific changes or adjustments you'd like to discuss?

Looking forward to your feedback!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 5: Feedback Request

When to use: When you need to prompt the client for feedback on a design.

Subject: 💡 Your Feedback Needed: [Project Name] Designs

Hi [Client's Name],

I hope this email finds you well. I wanted to follow up regarding the [Project Name] designs I sent earlier. Your feedback is essential to ensure we're moving in the right direction.

Could you please share your thoughts on the designs? Here are a few questions to guide your feedback:

  1. Which design concepts do you prefer and why?
  2. Are there any elements you'd like to see changed or improved?
  3. Do the designs align with your vision for [Project Name]?

Please take your time to review the designs and share your feedback. I'm here to make any necessary revisions based on your input.

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 6: Revisions & Next Steps

When to use: After receiving client feedback and making revisions.

Subject: 🔄 Revisions Complete: Next Steps for [Project Name]

Hi [Client's Name],

Thank you for your detailed feedback on the [Project Name] designs. I've made the necessary revisions based on your input, and I'm excited to share the updated designs with you.

Please find the revised designs attached. I've also included a summary of the changes made for your reference.

Before we move forward, I'd like to know:

  1. Are you satisfied with the revisions, or would you like further adjustments?
  2. Are there any additional changes or new elements you'd like to incorporate?
  3. Do you have any questions or concerns about the design process?

Once you're happy with the designs, we can discuss the next steps, such as finalizing the design, preparing assets for development, or planning the launch.

Looking forward to your thoughts!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 7: Final Design Approval

When to use: When you're ready to get the client's approval on the final design.

Subject: 🎉 Final Design Approval: [Project Name]

Hi [Client's Name],

I'm thrilled to share the final design for [Project Name]. After incorporating your feedback and making the necessary revisions, I believe we've created something truly special.

Please find the final design attached. I've also included a summary of the changes made since the last revision.

Before we proceed with the next steps, I need your official approval of the final design. Please let me know:

  1. Do you approve the final design as is?
  2. Are there any last-minute changes you'd like to discuss?

Once I have your approval, we can move forward with [next steps, e.g., preparing assets for development, planning the launch, etc.].

Thank you for your collaboration throughout this process. I'm excited to see [Project Name] come to life!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 8: Project Handoff

When to use: When the project is complete and you're handing off the final files to the client.

Subject: 📦 Project Handoff: [Project Name] Files & Next Steps

Hi [Client's Name],

I'm delighted to inform you that [Project Name] is now complete! I've compiled all the final files and assets for your review.

Please find the following attached:

  1. Final design files in [file format, e.g., PSD, AI, etc.]
  2. Optimized assets for web/development in [file format, e.g., PNG, JPG, SVG, etc.]
  3. A style guide outlining the design system, including colors, typography, and spacing

Before we wrap up, I'd like to schedule a quick call to walk you through the files and answer any questions you might have. Please let me know your availability over the next [timeframe, e.g., week].

Additionally, I'd appreciate it if you could provide any final feedback or leave a testimonial about your experience working with me. Your input is invaluable and helps me improve my services.

Thank you for the opportunity to work on [Project Name]. I'm proud of what we've accomplished together and hope to collaborate again in the future.

Best,

[Your Name]

I hope this email finds you well. As we discussed, I'm excited to start working on your [PROJECT TYPE] project. I wanted to take a moment to introduce you to the project timeline and milestones that we'll be following.

Below, you'll find a high-level overview of the project schedule, including key deadlines and deliverables:

  • Project kick-off: [START DATE]
  • Design concept presentation: [CONCEPT PRESENTATION DATE]
  • Revisions and feedback: [REVISION WINDOW]
  • Final design delivery: [FINAL DELIVERY DATE]

Please let me know if you have any questions or concerns about the project timeline. I'm always here to help and want to ensure that you're comfortable with the pace of the project.

If you'd like to discuss any aspects of the project, I'm available for a call at your convenience. Just let me know a time that works for you.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]
