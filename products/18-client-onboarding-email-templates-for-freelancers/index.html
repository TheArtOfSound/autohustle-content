18 Client Onboarding Email Templates for Freelancers

Updated: March 04, 2026

18 ready-to-use email templates to automate client onboarding — from welcome to project kickoff.

What's Inside

  • 18 customizable email templates
  • covers welcome, brief intake, and kickoff messages
  • designed for fast client setup

Template 1: Welcome and Introduction

When to use: Immediately after a client books your services to establish a positive tone.

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

A warm welcome to [YOUR COMPANY NAME]! We're thrilled to have you on board and excited to start working together. My name is [YOUR NAME], and I'll be your primary point of contact throughout our project. I've included some important details below to ensure a smooth onboarding process.

  • Project Start Date: [PROJECT START DATE]
  • Project Overview: [BRIEF PROJECT DESCRIPTION]

If you have any questions or need assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm looking forward to our collaboration and to helping you achieve your goals.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 2: Request for Project Information

When to use: After the initial welcome to gather necessary project details.

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

Hope you're doing well. As we prepare to kick off your project, I need some additional information to ensure everything goes as planned. Could you please provide the following:

  • Project objectives: [PROJECT OBJECTIVES PLACEHOLDER]
  • Target audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE PLACEHOLDER]
  • Any specific requirements or preferences: [SPECIAL REQUIREMENTS PLACEHOLDER]

Your prompt response will help us get started sooner. If you have any questions, feel free to ask.

Thank you,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 3: Onboarding Process Explanation

When to use: To inform clients about the steps involved in the onboarding process.

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

As we move forward with your project, I want to take a moment to outline the onboarding process. This typically involves the following steps:

  1. Introduction and Project Overview: We've already completed this step.
  2. Information Gathering: This is where we collect all necessary details to tailor our services to your needs.
  3. Project Planning: We create a tailored plan based on your objectives and preferences.
  4. Kickoff Meeting: A meeting to discuss the project plan, timelines, and any questions you may have.

If you have any questions about these steps, please let me know. Your understanding and cooperation are invaluable to the success of your project.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

  • Meeting Link: [MEETING LINK]
  • Agenda: [BRIEF AGENDA OVERVIEW]

If this time doesn't work for you, please let me know as soon as possible so we can find an alternative.

Looking forward to speaking with you then.

Best,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 7: Request for Feedback

When to use: To solicit client feedback on the work completed or the onboarding process.

  • Were the project objectives clearly outlined and met?
