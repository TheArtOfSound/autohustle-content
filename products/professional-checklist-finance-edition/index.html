Professional Checklist: Finance Edition

Getting Started with Your Finance Checklist

Step 1: Gather Essential Information

  • Check your current financial situation:
  • Income
  • Expenses (housing, food, transportation, entertainment, etc.)
  • Debt obligations (credit cards, loans, mortgages)
  • Credit report and score
  • Determine your financial goals:
  • Short-term (less than 1 year): emergency fund, paying off high-interest debt, saving for a specific purpose
  • Long-term (1-5 years): retirement savings, buying a home, funding education expenses

Step 2: Choose Your Finance Tools and Software

  • Select reliable budgeting apps:
  • Mint
  • Personal Capital
  • YNAB (You Need a Budget)
  • Excel or Google Sheets for tracking expenses
  • Consider investing platforms:
  • Vanguard
  • Fidelity
  • Robinhood
