Template 5: Skill Tip / Micro-Advice

When to use: To position yourself as an expert by sharing quick, valuable tips

Quick tip for [TARGET AUDIENCE, e.g., entrepreneurs building their first website]:

Your homepage should answer ONE question within 3 seconds:

👉 “What do you do, and how do you help me?”

Too many sites lead with fluff. Instead, try this formula:

  1. Clear headline: “[WHAT YOU DO] for [WHO YOU HELP]”
  2. Subheadline: “So you can [KEY BENEFIT]”
  3. Strong CTA: “Get Started” or “Learn More”

Example:

“Beautiful websites for coaches

So you can attract more clients online

👉 Book Your Free Consult”

Simple = effective. Try it!

🔁 Share this with a friend who needs it!

#FreelanceTips #[YourIndustry]Advice #WebsiteTips
