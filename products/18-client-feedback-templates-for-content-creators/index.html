18 Client Feedback Templates for Content Creators

Updated: March 04, 2026

18 ready-to-use client feedback templates for content creators — streamline reviews and get clearer input on your work.

What's Inside

  • 18 customizable feedback request templates
  • designed for video, blog, and social content
  • saves time and reduces revision loops

Template 1: General Content Review

When to use: When you need a comprehensive review of any type of content.

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

Thank you for taking the time to review my work. I've included the [CONTENT TYPE] below for your feedback. Please consider the following aspects:

  1. Content Quality: Does the content meet your expectations in terms of quality and depth?
  2. Relevance: Is the content relevant to your target audience and aligned with your brand voice?
  3. Structure: Is the content well-structured and easy to follow?
  4. Engagement: Does the content engage and resonate with your audience?
  5. Call-to-Action: Is the call-to-action clear and compelling?

Please provide your feedback on the following scale:

  • Excellent
  • Good
  • Fair
  • Poor

Additionally, please share any specific comments or suggestions you have to improve the content.

Looking forward to your feedback!

Best,

[YOUR NAME]
