Problem & Solution

Template 2: Customer Discovery Interview Script

Purpose: Conduct structured interviews to validate your assumptions about customer problems and solutions.

Instructions: Use this script for 10-15 customer interviews. Take detailed notes and look for patterns in responses.

Pre-Interview Setup

  • Interviewee Name: [Customer name]
  • Date/Time: [Interview date and time]
  • Background: [Brief description of interviewee's relevant background]
  • Interview Method: [Phone, video call, in-person]

Opening (5 minutes)

"Hi [Name], thank you for taking time to speak with me. I'm researching [PROBLEM AREA] and would love to learn about your experience. This isn't a sales call - I'm genuinely trying to understand how people like you handle [SITUATION]. The interview should take about 20 minutes."

Problem Discovery (10 minutes)

  1. "Tell me about the last time you encountered [PROBLEM/SITUATION]?"
  • Response: [Record their answer]
  • Follow-up questions asked: [Note any clarifying questions]
  1. "How do you currently handle [PROBLEM]?"
  • Current Solution: [How they solve it now]
  • Satisfaction Level: [Rate 1-10 how happy they are with current solution]
  1. "What's most frustrating about [CURRENT SOLUTION]?"
  • Pain Points: [List specific frustrations]
  1. "How much time/money does this problem cost you?"
  • Time Cost: [Hours per week/month]
  • Financial Cost: [Dollar amount if applicable]

Solution Validation (5 minutes)

  1. "If there was a solution that [BRIEF SOLUTION DESCRIPTION], how interested would you be?"
  • Interest Level: [Rate 1-10]
  • Concerns: [Any hesitations or objections]
  1. "What would make this solution irresistible to you?"
  • Must-Have Features: [Essential features they mention]
  • Nice-to-Have Features: [Desired but not essential features]

Closing Questions (3 minutes)

  1. "Who else do you know who has this same problem?"
  • Referrals: [Names and contact info if they're willing to share]
  1. "Would you be interested in being notified when this solution is available?"
  • Contact Permission: [Yes/No and preferred contact method]

Post-Interview Notes

  • Key Insights: [Most important takeaways]
  • Surprising Responses: [Anything unexpected]
  • Validation Score: [Rate 1-10 how well this validates your idea]
