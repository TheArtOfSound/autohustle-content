17 Client Onboarding Templates for Solo Content Creators

Updated: March 10, 2026

17 client onboarding templates for solo content creators — streamline your workflow with ready-to-use forms and messages for every step of the onboarding process.

What's Inside

  • 17 plug-and-play templates

Template 1: Welcome Email to New Client

When to use: Immediately after a client agrees to work with you, to confirm the partnership and set expectations.

Hi [Client Name],

Thank you so much for choosing me to help with [project/service name]! I’m excited to collaborate and support your goals.

This email confirms that we’re officially moving forward. Next, I’ll send over a brief onboarding form so I can learn more about your brand, goals, and expectations.

Here’s what to expect in the next few days:

  • A quick onboarding questionnaire to gather key details
  • A timeline and deliverables overview
  • Scheduling for any necessary kickoff calls

If you have any immediate questions, just reply to this email. Otherwise, look out for the next step in your inbox within 24 hours.

Warmly,

[Your Full Name]

[Your Business Name, if applicable]

[Website URL]

[Email Address] | [Phone Number]

When to use: After the welcome email, to collect essential information about the client, project, and brand.

Please answer the following questions and reply directly to this email:

  1. Project Overview:

What is the main goal of this project? (e.g., grow audience, promote a product, improve SEO)

[Client to fill]

  1. Target Audience:

Who are we creating content for? (e.g., busy moms aged 30–45, SaaS founders)

[Client to fill]

  1. Brand Voice & Style:

[Client to fill]

Any brands or content you admire as inspiration?

[Client to fill]

[Client to fill]

When do you need the first draft? Final delivery date?

[Client to fill]

  1. Additional Assets:

Do you have brand guidelines, visuals, keywords, or reference materials to share? If so, please attach or link them.

[Client to fill]

  1. Point of Contact:

Who should I direct questions to during the project?

[Client to fill]

Looking forward to your responses so we can kick things off smoothly!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 6: Client Kickoff Call Agenda

When to use: When scheduling a call to align on project details and build rapport.

Hi [Client Name],

Great to have you on board! I’d love to schedule a quick 20–30 minute kickoff call to align on goals, timeline, and any questions you have.

Here’s what we’ll cover:

Kickoff Call Agenda

  1. Welcome & Introductions (2 min)
  • Quick intro and connection
  1. Project Goals Recap (5 min)
  • Confirm: What success looks like for you
  1. Deliverables & Timeline (5 min)
  • Review what’s being created and key dates
  1. Brand Voice & Audience (5 min)
  • Discuss tone, style, and who we’re speaking to
  1. Feedback & Revisions Process (3 min)
  • How to share feedback, turnaround times
  1. Communication Plan (3 min)
  • Best ways to reach each other, response time expectations
  1. Your Questions (5–7 min)
  • Anything else you’d like to clarify?

Please suggest 2–3 times that work for you this week, and I’ll send a calendar invite with a Zoom/Google Meet link.

Looking forward to getting started!

Best,

[Your Name]
