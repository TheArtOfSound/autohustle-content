Communication & Feedback Framework Tools

Template 5: Emotional Intelligence Feedback Framework

Purpose: Provide structured, empathetic feedback that considers emotional impact.

Instructions: Use this framework when giving performance feedback or addressing behavioral issues. Complete all sections before the conversation.

```markdown

Emotional Intelligence Feedback Framework

Team Member: [NAME]

Manager: [MANAGER NAME]

Feedback Session Date: [DATE]

Topic: [SPECIFIC BEHAVIOR/PERFORMANCE AREA]

Pre-Feedback Preparation

Emotional Context Assessment

Team member's current emotional state: [OBSERVATIONS]

Recent stressors affecting them: [KNOWN FACTORS]

Their preferred communication style: [STYLE PREFERENCES]

Timing considerations: [OPTIMAL TIMING NOTES]

Feedback Framing

Primary goal of this feedback: [DEVELOPMENT/CORRECTION/RECOGNITION]

Desired outcome: [SPECIFIC BEHAVIORAL CHANGE OR ACKNOWLEDGMENT]

Potential emotional reactions: [ANTICIPATED RESPONSES]

Feedback Structure

Opening - Emotional Safety (2-3 minutes)

Setting the tone:

"I wanted to talk with you about [TOPIC] because I care about your success and our team's effectiveness. This conversation comes from a place of support, and I'm here to listen as much as to share observations."

Check-in question:

"Before we dive in, how are you feeling today? Is this a good time for you to have this conversation?"

Observation Sharing (5-7 minutes)

Specific behavior observed:

"I've noticed [SPECIFIC BEHAVIOR/PATTERN]. For example, [CONCRETE EXAMPLE WITH DATE/CONTEXT]."

Impact statement:

"The impact I've observed is [SPECIFIC IMPACT ON TEAM/WORK/RELATIONSHIPS]."

Avoiding assumptions:

"I'm curious about your perspective on this. What's your experience been?"

Collaborative Exploration (10-15 minutes)

Understanding their viewpoint:

  • "What's your take on the situation?"
  • "What factors might be contributing to this pattern?"
  • "How are you feeling about [SPECIFIC ASPECT]?"

Emotional validation:

"I can understand how [THEIR PERSPECTIVE] would be [FRUSTRATING/CHALLENGING/OVERWHELMING]."

Shared problem-solving:

"Let's think together about how we can [IMPROVE/ADJUST/SUPPORT] this situation."

Development Planning

Collaborative Solutions

Ideas generated together:

1. [SOLUTION/STRATEGY 1]

2. [SOLUTION/STRATEGY 2]

3. [SOLUTION/STRATEGY 3]

Selected approach:

[CHOSEN STRATEGY WITH RATIONALE]

Support Commitments

What I will do to support you:

  • [MANAGER COMMITMENT 1]
  • [MANAGER COMMITMENT 2]

What you've committed to:

  • [TEAM MEMBER COMMITMENT 1]
  • [TEAM MEMBER COMMITMENT 2]

Success Metrics

How we'll measure progress:

  • [MEASURABLE INDICATOR 1]
  • [MEASURABLE INDICATOR 2]

Timeline for review: [FOLLOW-UP DATE]

Closing & Emotional Check-in

Ending questions:

  • "How are you feeling about our conversation?"
  • "Is there anything else you'd like to discuss or clarify?"
  • "What support do you need from me going forward?"

Final affirmation:

"I want to emphasize that I believe in your capabilities and I'm committed to your success. We're in this together."

Post-Conversation Follow-up

Immediate actions: [WHAT TO DO IN NEXT 24-48 HOURS]

Documentation notes: [KEY POINTS FOR RECORD]

Emotional tone of conversation: [POSITIVE/NEUTRAL/CHALLENGING]

Follow-up needed: [SPECIFIC FOLLOW-UP ACTIONS]

```
