Template 1: Patient Intake Form

When to use: During the initial consultation with a new patient to gather medical history and contact information.

Thank you for choosing [HEALTH_CARE_PROVIDER]. To ensure we provide the best care possible, please fill out this form completely.

  • Name: [PATIENT_NAME]
  • Date of Birth: [DATE_OF_BIRTH]
  • Contact Information: [ADDRESS], [PHONE_NUMBER], [EMAIL]
  • Medical History: [MEDICAL_HISTORY]
  • Current Medications: [CURRENT_MEDICATIONS]
  • Allergies: [ALLERGIES]
  • Emergency Contact: [EMERGENCY_CONTACT_NAME], [EMERGENCY_CONTACT_PHONE]

Please return this form to our office by [FORM_DEADLINE].

Sincerely,

[HEALTH_CARE_PROVIDER]

Template 2: Chronic Disease Management Plan

When to use: For patients with ongoing conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, or asthma to clarify management goals and responsibilities.

Dear [Patient Name],

Managing [Chronic Condition, e.g., Type 2 Diabetes] is a shared effort, and we’re committed to supporting you every step of the way. Below is your personalized management plan:

  • Condition Overview: [Brief explanation of the condition and its impact on health]
  • Treatment Goals:
  • [Goal 1: e.g., Maintain HbA1c below 7%]
  • [Goal 2: e.g., Reduce systolic BP to <130 mmHg]
  • [Goal 3: e.g., Exercise 150 minutes per week]
  • Medications:
  • [Medication Name]: [Dosage], [Frequency]
  • [Medication Name]: [Dosage], [Frequency]
  • Monitoring Plan: [e.g., Check blood glucose twice daily, log readings weekly]
  • Lifestyle Recommendations: [Diet, physical activity, stress management tips]
  • Emergency Signs to Watch For: [e.g., Chest pain, severe shortness of breath, confusion]

Please bring this plan to all medical visits and share it with other providers when needed. We’ll review and adjust it during your next appointment on [Next Appointment Date].

Sincerely,

[Your Name], [Credentials]

[Title]

[Clinic Name]

[Date]

Dear Dr. [Specialist Last Name],

I am referring [Patient Name], a [Age]-year-old [Gender], for your expert evaluation regarding [Reason for Referral, e.g., persistent knee pain unresponsive to conservative treatment].

[Patient Name] has been under my care for [Duration] and presents with [Brief history of present illness, relevant medical history, prior treatments, and response].

Relevant Findings:

  • [Test Result or Physical Exam Finding, e.g., MRI shows meniscal tear]
  • [Comorbidities, e.g., Osteoarthritis, diabetes]
  • Current medications: [List relevant medications]

Reason for Referral:

[Specific reason, e.g., Assessment for surgical intervention, diagnostic clarification, management optimization]

Thank you for your collaboration in [Patient Name]’s care.

Sincerely,

[Employee Name] is currently [Age] years old and under routine primary care. Their health assessment on [Visit Date] indicates:

  • Overall Health Status: [e.g., Stable, managing chronic condition, no acute issues]

  • Recommended Workplace Supports: [e.g., Access to ergonomic evaluation, flexible break schedule for walking]

Identified Health Risks:

  1. [Risk 1: e.g., Elevated LDL Cholesterol – Level: [Value]]
  2. [Risk 2: e.g., Sedentary Lifestyle – <30 min activity/week]
  3. [Risk 3: e.g., Family History of [Condition]]

Current Protective Factors:

  • [e.g., Non-smoker, regular screenings up to date, strong social support]

Recommended Preventive Actions:

  • [Action 1: e.g., Start statin therapy to reduce cardiovascular risk]
  • [Action 2: e.g., Increase physical activity to 150 min/week of moderate exercise]
  • [Action 3: e.g., Schedule colonoscopy by [Date]]

Next Steps:

We recommend reviewing this assessment together at your next visit on [Appointment Date]. In the meantime, feel free to discuss any concerns with our care team via [Portal Link or Phone Number].
