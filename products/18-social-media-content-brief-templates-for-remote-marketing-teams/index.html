18 Social Media Content Brief Templates for Remote Marketing Teams

Updated: March 06, 2026

18 social media content brief templates for remote marketing teams — streamlines collaboration and maintains content quality across distributed teams.

What's Inside

  • 18 customizable content brief templates
  • designed for video, blog, and carousel posts
  • ensures clarity and brand alignment across time zones

Template 1: Campaign Launch Announcement

When to use: When announcing the official launch of a new marketing campaign, product, or service across social platforms. Ideal for aligning remote teams on messaging, visuals, and timing.

Platform: [PLATFORM: e.g., Instagram, LinkedIn, Twitter, etc.]

Objective: Announce the launch of [CAMPAIGN/PRODUCT NAME] and drive initial engagement and awareness.

Target Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE: e.g., female entrepreneurs aged 28–40, SaaS decision-makers]

Tone & Voice: [TONE: e.g., Excited, Professional, Playful, Bold]

Content Type: [CONTENT TYPE: e.g., Carousel, Single Image, Short Video, Story]

Headline (Max 150 characters):

[HEADLINE: e.g., “It’s here! Meet [PRODUCT NAME] — designed to help you [BENEFIT]”]

Primary Caption:

[CAPTION: Craft 2–3 engaging sentences introducing the launch. Highlight key benefits and include a CTA. Example: “We’ve been working hard behind the scenes, and we’re thrilled to finally share [PRODUCT NAME] with you! Whether you’re [AUDIENCE PAIN POINT], this is your solution. Tap the link in bio to learn more →”]

Call to Action (CTA): [CTA: e.g., “Learn more”, “Shop now”, “Join the waitlist”, “Tag a friend”]

Link (if applicable): [LINK: Insert URL or “N/A”]

Visual Requirements:

  • [VISUAL DESCRIPTION: e.g., High-res product shot with lifestyle background]
  • Include [BRAND ELEMENT: e.g., logo watermark, brand colors]
  • Preferred dimensions: [DIMENSIONS: e.g., 1080x1350px for Instagram carousel]

Hashtags (3–5):

#[HASHTAG1] #[HASHTAG2] #[HASHTAG3]

Mention Tags (if applicable): [@HANDLE1, @HANDLE2]

Publish Date & Time: [DATE & TIME: e.g., March 15, 2024 | 10:00 AM EST]

Notes for Creator:

  • Ensure all brand guidelines are followed.
  • Avoid stock imagery unless approved.
  • Tag [TEAM MEMBER/AGENCY] in draft for review before scheduling.

When to use: To share and celebrate content created by customers or followers. Builds trust and community engagement.

Platform: [PLATFORM: e.g., Instagram Feed, TikTok, Facebook]

Objective: Highlight authentic customer experiences with [PRODUCT/SERVICE NAME] and encourage more submissions.

Target Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE: e.g., Existing customers, brand advocates]

Content Type: [CONTENT TYPE: e.g., Reel, Image Post, Story Highlight]

[HEADLINE: e.g., “You’re doing amazing things with [PRODUCT] — here’s proof!”]

Primary Caption:

[CAPTION: e.g., “We love seeing how you use [PRODUCT] in your everyday life! Shoutout to [@CUSTOMER_HANDLE] for this incredible [USE CASE]. Want to be featured? Share your story with #[CAMPAIGN_HASHTAG] for a chance to be spotlighted!”]

Call to Action (CTA): [CTA: e.g., “Share your story”, “Use our hashtag”, “Tag us”]

Visual Requirements:

  • Use original content from [@CUSTOMER_HANDLE]
  • Credit creator in image/text overlay: “Photo by @CUSTOMER_HANDLE”
  • Crop/resize to fit [PLATFORM] specs: [DIMENSIONS]

Hashtags (3–5):

#[BRANDED_HASHTAG] #[UGC] #[INDUSTRY_HASHTAG]

Template 4: Educational Tip Post

When to use: To provide value-driven content that positions your brand as an industry expert.

Platform: [PLATFORM: e.g., LinkedIn, Instagram Carousel, Twitter Thread]

Objective: Educate audience on [TOPIC] and increase engagement through actionable advice.

Target Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE: e.g., Small business owners, new marketers]

Content Type: [CONTENT TYPE: e.g., Carousel, Infographic, Tweet Thread]

Headline (Max 150 characters):

[HEADLINE: e.g., “3 quick tips to boost your [METRIC] in under a week”]

Primary Caption:

[CAPTION: e.g., “Struggling with [PROBLEM]? You’re not alone. Here are 3 proven strategies our team uses daily to [ACHIEVE RESULT]. Save this post and try them this week!”]

Call to Action (CTA): [CTA: e.g., “Save for later”, “Try this tip”, “Share with a colleague”]

Link (if applicable): [LINK: e.g., Blog post or N/A]

Visual Requirements:

  • Design 3–5 slide carousel with one tip per slide
  • Use brand fonts and colors: [COLOR PALETTE CODES]
  • Include icons or illustrations for visual clarity
  • Final slide: “Follow for more [INDUSTRY] tips” + CTA

Hashtags (3–5):

#[EDUCATION] #[TIP] #[INDUSTRY] #[BRANDNAME]

Mention Tags (if applicable): [@SME_HANDLE, @CONTENT_TEAM]

  • Export as JPG or MP4 (for animated versions).
  • Tag subject matter expert for review.

Platform: [PLATFORM: e.g., YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, LinkedIn Video]

Objective: Demonstrate the value and functionality of [FEATURE NAME] to current and potential users.

Target Audience: [TARGET AUDIENCE: e.g., Active users, free-tier customers]

Tone & Voice: [TONE: e.g., Clear, Enthusiastic, Instructional]

Content Type: [CONTENT TYPE: e.g., 30–60s video, animated explainer, screen recording]

Headline (Max 150 characters):

[HEADLINE: e.g., “Did you know [FEATURE] can save you 2 hours a week?”]

Primary Caption:

[CAPTION: e.g., “Unlock the full power of [PRODUCT] with [FEATURE NAME]. Whether you’re [USE CASE 1] or [USE CASE 2], this tool is a game-changer. Watch to see how it works →”]

Call to Action (CTA): [CTA: e.g., “Try it now”, “Watch the full tutorial”, “Upgrade to access”]

Link (if applicable): [LINK: Feature page or in-app prompt]

Visual Requirements:

  • Screen recording of [FEATURE] in action (10–30 seconds)
  • Add text overlays: “Step 1: [ACTION]”, “Result: [BENEFIT]”
  • Include voiceover or subtitles
  • End screen: “Learn more at [WEBSITE]” + [BRAND LOGO]
  • Format: MP4, 9:16 or 1:1, under 100MB

Hashtags (3–5):

#[PRODUCTTIP] #[FEATURE] #[HOWTO]

Mention Tags (if applicable): [@PRODUCT_TEAM, @SUPPORT_HANDLE]

Publish Date & Time: [DATE & TIME]

Notes for Creator:

  • Use real (non-sensitive) demo data.
  • Keep audio clear and background noise-free.
  • Tag product manager for technical accuracy review.
