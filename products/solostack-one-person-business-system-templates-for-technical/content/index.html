Client Management & Sales Systems

4. Discovery Call Framework Template

Purpose: Standardize your sales conversations to close more qualified prospects.

Instructions: Customize questions based on your service type. Practice this framework until it feels natural.

```

PRE-CALL PREPARATION

□ Research [COMPANY NAME] website and recent news

□ Review their [INDUSTRY] challenges and trends

□ Prepare 3 relevant case studies

□ Set up screen sharing and recording

□ Send calendar invite with agenda

OPENING (5 minutes)

"Hi [NAME], thanks for taking the time to speak with me today. I've done some research on [COMPANY]

and I'm excited to learn more about [SPECIFIC CHALLENGE/GOAL].

Before we dive in, I'd like to understand your situation better so I can determine if there's a good

fit between what you need and what I provide. Does that sound good?"

DISCOVERY QUESTIONS (20 minutes)

Current Situation:

  • "Tell me about your current [RELEVANT AREA] setup"
  • "What's working well right now?"
  • "What's not working as well as you'd like?"

Problem Identification:

  • "What prompted you to look for help with this?"
  • "How is this challenge impacting [BUSINESS AREA]?"
  • "What have you tried to solve this already?"

Goals & Outcomes:

  • "What would success look like for you?"
  • "How would solving this impact your [REVENUE/EFFICIENCY/GOALS]?"
  • "What's your timeline for addressing this?"

Decision Process:

  • "Who else is involved in this decision?"
  • "What's your budget range for solving this?"
  • "What questions do they have that I should address?"

SOLUTION PRESENTATION (10 minutes)

"Based on what you've shared, it sounds like you need [SOLUTION SUMMARY].

I've helped [SIMILAR COMPANY] achieve [SPECIFIC RESULT] by [APPROACH].

Here's how I'd approach your situation:

[STEP 1]: [DESCRIPTION]

[STEP 2]: [DESCRIPTION]

[STEP 3]: [DESCRIPTION]

The investment for this would be [PRICE RANGE], and we could have this completed by [DATE]."

CLOSING (5 minutes)

"How does this approach sound to you?"

[LISTEN TO RESPONSE]

Next Steps:

□ "I'll send you a detailed proposal by [DATE]"

□ "Let's schedule a follow-up call for [DATE]"

□ "What questions can I answer for [DECISION MAKER]?"

POST-CALL ACTIONS

□ Send thank you email within 2 hours

□ Update CRM with notes and next steps

□ Prepare and send proposal within [TIMEFRAME]

□ Schedule follow-up reminder

```
