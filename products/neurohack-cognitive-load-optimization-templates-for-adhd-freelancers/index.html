Introduction

As an ADHD freelancer, you face unique challenges that traditional productivity advice simply doesn't address. Your brilliant, creative mind works differently – and that's your superpower. These templates are specifically designed for neurodivergent brains, helping you optimize cognitive load, manage energy fluctuations, and build sustainable systems that actually stick.

Template 2: Hyperfocus Session Planner

Purpose: Harness your hyperfocus superpowers while building in necessary breaks and transitions.

Instructions: Use this before entering a planned hyperfocus session. Set up your environment and safeguards first.

```

HYPERFOCUS SESSION PLAN

PROJECT/TASK: [SPECIFIC PROJECT OR TASK NAME]

PLANNED DURATION: [TIME ESTIMATE - START CONSERVATIVELY]

START TIME: [SPECIFIC TIME]

END TIME: [SPECIFIC TIME + BUFFER]

PRE-SESSION SETUP:

□ Phone on airplane mode or in another room

□ Water bottle filled: [LOCATION]

□ Snacks prepared: [WHAT AND WHERE]

□ Bathroom break completed

□ Workspace organized and distractions removed

□ Background music/white noise set up: [TYPE/PLAYLIST]

□ Timer set for: [WORK PERIOD] + [BREAK REMINDER]

SESSION GOALS:

Primary goal (must achieve): [MAIN OBJECTIVE]

Secondary goal (if time permits): [ADDITIONAL OBJECTIVE]

Bonus goal (if in flow state): [STRETCH OBJECTIVE]

TRANSITION PLAN (WHEN SESSION ENDS):

Next activity: [WHAT YOU'LL DO AFTER]

Transition time needed: [BUFFER TIME]

Transition ritual: [SPECIFIC ACTIONS TO SHIFT GEARS]

BREAK REMINDERS (SET ALARMS):

  • 90 minutes: Stand and stretch
  • 2 hours: Hydrate and snack
  • 3 hours: Take a 15-minute walk
  • 4 hours: MANDATORY longer break

POST-SESSION REFLECTION:

Actual duration: [TIME WORKED]

Goals achieved: [CHECKBOXES FOR EACH GOAL]

Energy level after: [1-10 RATING]

What worked well: [OBSERVATIONS]

What to adjust next time: [IMPROVEMENTS]

```
