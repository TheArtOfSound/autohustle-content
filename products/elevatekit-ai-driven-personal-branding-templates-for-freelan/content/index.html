AI prompts are instructions you provide to the AI to customize your templates. By using specific keywords and phrases, you can guide the AI to generate content that aligns with your personal brand.

1. Be Specific: Clearly define what you want the AI to generate.

2. Use Keywords: Include relevant keywords related to your industry and expertise.

3. Provide Examples: Share examples of content or design elements you like.
