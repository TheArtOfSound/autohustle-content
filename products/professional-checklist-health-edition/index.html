A comprehensive guide to maintaining optimal health and well-being, covering daily, weekly, monthly, and annual habits, as well as emergency preparedness.

Daily Health Habits

Developing consistent daily habits is crucial for overall health. Ensure you incorporate the following into your daily routine:

  • [ ] Drink at least 8 glasses of water throughout the day
  • [ ] Eat 5 servings of fruits and vegetables
  • [ ] Include a source of protein in every meal
  • [ ] Exercise for at least 30 minutes (brisk walking, jogging, yoga, etc.)
  • [ ] Practice good sleep hygiene (7-9 hours of sleep, avoid screens before bedtime)
  • [ ] Take a short break every hour to stretch and move around
  • [ ] Limit intake of sugary drinks and fast food
  • [ ] Meditate or practice deep breathing exercises for 10 minutes
  • [ ] Review and plan your meals for the next day
