25 Health & Fitness Checklists for Remote Workers

Updated: March 04, 2026

25 checklists designed to help remote workers maintain a healthy lifestyle, covering exercise routines, meal planning, and mental well-being.

What's Inside

  • 25 comprehensive checklists
  • Covers exercise, nutrition, and mental health
  • Easy-to-follow format
  • [ ] Item 1: Start the day with 8 oz of water before drinking coffee or tea
  • [ ] Item 2: Perform 5 minutes of morning stretching or light yoga upon waking
  • [ ] Item 3: Expose yourself to natural sunlight within 30 minutes of getting up
  • [ ] Item 4: Eat a protein-rich breakfast within one hour of waking
  • [ ] Item 5: Set up your workstation with proper ergonomics (laptop at eye level, feet flat on floor)
  • [ ] Item 6: Use a standing desk or elevate your laptop for at least 30 minutes per work hour
  • [ ] Item 7: Schedule a 5-minute movement break every hour (walk, stretch, or climb stairs)
  • [ ] Item 8: Drink one full glass of water every 90 minutes during work hours
  • [ ] Item 9: Silence non-essential notifications to minimize distractions and stress
  • [ ] Item 10: Practice the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds
  • [ ] Item 11: Take a 10-15 minute walk outside during your lunch break
  • [ ] Item 12: Eat lunch away from your desk—no screens or work during this time
  • [ ] Item 13: Include at least two servings of vegetables in your daily meals
  • [ ] Item 14: Prep healthy snacks (e.g., nuts, fruit, yogurt) at the start of the week
  • [ ] Item 15: Limit caffeine intake after 2 PM to improve sleep quality
  • [ ] Item 16: Schedule a 20-minute workout session (bodyweight, resistance bands, or cardio) during the day
  • [ ] Item 17: Do 10 minutes of deep breathing or guided meditation before lunch or after work
  • [ ] Item 18: Log off all work devices at a consistent time each day
  • [ ] Item 19: Write down three things you accomplished at work before ending your day
  • [ ] Item 20: Change out of work clothes to signal the end of the workday
  • [ ] Item 21: Spend 15 minutes tidying your workspace before going to bed
  • [ ] Item 22: Avoid screens for at least 30 minutes before bedtime
  • [ ] Item 23: Read a physical book or journal for 10–15 minutes before sleep
  • [ ] Item 24: Keep your bedroom cool, dark, and quiet to optimize sleep conditions
  • [ ] Item 25: Aim for 7–8 hours of uninterrupted sleep each night
