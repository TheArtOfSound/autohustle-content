Template 8: Workout Plan – Beginner Full-Body (4 Weeks)

When to use: For new clients starting strength training, 2–3 days per week

Beginner Full-Body Workout Plan

Coach: [Your Name]

Client: [Client Name]

Duration: Weeks 1–4

Frequency: [2 or 3] days per week (e.g., Mon / Wed / Fri)

Workout Structure

  • Warm-up: 5–7 minutes
  • Strength: 3 rounds of each circuit
  • Rest: 30–60 sec between sets
  • Cool-down: 5 minutes stretching

Week 1–2: Foundation Phase

Complete 2x per week

Circuit A (Repeat 3x)

  • Bodyweight Squats: 10 reps
  • Push-Ups (on knees or wall if needed): 8 reps
  • Bent-Over Dumbbell Rows: 10 reps (use [Weight] lbs)
  • Glute Bridges: 12 reps
  • Plank: Hold 20 seconds

Circuit B (Repeat 3x)

  • Step-Ups (use stairs or bench): 8 per leg
  • Dumbbell Overhead Press: 10 reps ([Weight] lbs)
  • Bird-Dog: 10 reps per side
  • Standing Side Bends: 12 per side
  • Seated Breathing (1 min): Focus on deep breaths

Week 3–4: Progression Phase

Complete 3x per week

Circuit (Repeat 3x)

  • Goblet Squats: 10 reps ([Weight] lb dumbbell)
  • Push-Ups: 10 reps (progress as able)
  • Dumbbell Rows: 10 reps per arm ([Weight] lbs)
  • Romanian Deadlifts: 10 reps ([Light Weight] lbs)
  • Plank: Hold 30 seconds
  • Standing Marches: 30 seconds

Notes:

  • Increase weight slightly when 10 reps feel easy
  • Record workouts in your journal or app
  • Rest at least 1 day between sessions

You’re building strength and confidence—keep going, [Client Name]!
