20 Checklists for Remote Work Productivity

Updated: March 04, 2026

20 comprehensive checklists designed to boost productivity for remote workers and freelancers.

What's Inside

  • 20 detailed checklists
  • Covers daily, weekly, and monthly tasks
  • Easy to follow format
  • [ ] Item 1: Set a consistent wake-up time and stick to it, even on weekends
  • [ ] Item 2: Begin the day with a 5-minute mindfulness or breathing exercise
  • [ ] Item 3: Make your bed immediately after waking up
  • [ ] Item 4: Get dressed in work-appropriate attire (no pajamas)
  • [ ] Item 5: Review your calendar and task list for the day within the first 30 minutes
  • [ ] Item 6: Prioritize your top 3 most important tasks (MITs) for the day
  • [ ] Item 7: Break large tasks into smaller, time-blocked subtasks
  • [ ] Item 8: Schedule focused work blocks of 60–90 minutes with no distractions
  • [ ] Item 9: Turn off non-essential notifications on all devices during deep work
  • [ ] Item 10: Use a task management tool (e.g., Todoist, Trello) to log all assignments
  • [ ] Item 11: Send a clear morning check-in message to your team or client (if applicable)
  • [ ] Item 12: Respond to urgent emails and messages only during designated times
  • [ ] Item 13: Use status indicators (e.g., Slack status) to communicate availability
  • [ ] Item 14: Schedule all meetings with clear agendas and time limits
  • [ ] Item 15: Take a 5–10 minute break every 60 minutes to stretch or walk
  • [ ] Item 16: Use the Pomodoro technique (25-min work, 5-min break) for sustained focus
  • [ ] Item 17: Hydrate with a full glass of water and eat a nutritious meal every 4 hours
  • [ ] Item 18: Step outside for at least 10 minutes of fresh air and sunlight
  • [ ] Item 19: End your workday by updating task lists and noting unfinished items
  • [ ] Item 20: Perform a 5-minute evening reflection on accomplishments and blockers
