Introduction

Welcome to ShadowOps, the ultimate collection of stealth productivity templates designed specifically for ambitious corporate employees who are building side businesses on the down-low. These templates will help you maintain the perfect balance between your day job responsibilities and your entrepreneurial dreams, all while keeping your side hustle completely under the radar.

Each template has been carefully crafted to maximize efficiency, minimize risk, and ensure you can build your empire without jeopardizing your current position. Whether you're consulting, creating digital products, or running an e-commerce business, these tools will keep you organized, compliant, and profitable.

Purpose: Define your side business goals while maintaining alignment with your career trajectory.

  1. [SPECIFIC MEASURABLE GOAL #1]

  • I will never use company time for [SPECIFIC RESTRICTIONS]
  • I will never use company resources for [SPECIFIC RESTRICTIONS]
  • I will maintain [PERFORMANCE STANDARDS] at my day job
  • I will avoid clients in [INDUSTRY/SECTOR RESTRICTIONS]
