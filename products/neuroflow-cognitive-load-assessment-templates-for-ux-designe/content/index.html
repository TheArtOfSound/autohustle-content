NeuroFlow: Cognitive Load Assessment Templates for UX Designers

Introduction

Cognitive load theory is fundamental to creating user-centered digital experiences. These templates help UX/UI designers and product managers systematically evaluate and optimize the mental effort required for users to interact with digital interfaces.

Use these templates to identify cognitive bottlenecks, improve usability, and create more intuitive user experiences across mobile apps, complex interfaces, and accessible digital products.
