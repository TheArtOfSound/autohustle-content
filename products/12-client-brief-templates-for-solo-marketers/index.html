Template 1: The Quick-Start Marketing Brief

When to use: When onboarding new clients with minimal time for discovery—ideal for social media, content, or ad campaigns with clear goals

Client Name: [CLIENT NAME]

Project Title: [PROJECT TITLE]

Date: [DATE]

1. Primary Goal

What is the #1 outcome you want from this project?

[PRIMARY GOAL – e.g., “Generate 50 qualified leads in 30 days”]

2. Target Audience

Who are we speaking to?

  • Demographics: [AGE, LOCATION, JOB TITLE, INCOME LEVEL, etc.]
  • Pain Points: [TOP 3 CHALLENGES YOUR AUDIENCE FACES]
  • Desired Outcome: [WHAT THEY WANT TO ACHIEVE]

3. Project Scope

What exactly do you need from me?

[LIST SPECIFIC DELIVERABLES – e.g., “3 LinkedIn posts per week, 1 lead magnet, monthly ad campaign setup”]

4. Brand Voice & Style

Describe your brand tone in 3 words: [WORD1, WORD2, WORD3]

Examples of brands you admire: [EXAMPLE BRANDS]

5. Competitors

Who are your top 3 competitors?

  1. [COMPETITOR 1]
  2. [COMPETITOR 2]
  3. [COMPETITOR 3]

6. Success Metrics

How will we measure success?

[KPIs – e.g., “CTR > 3%, 20% conversion rate on landing page”]

7. Timeline & Deadlines

  • Project Start: [START DATE]
  • First Deliverable Due: [DATE]
  • Final Delivery: [DATE]

8. Additional Notes

[ANY OTHER INFO – e.g., “Avoid political references,” “All visuals must be mobile-first”]
