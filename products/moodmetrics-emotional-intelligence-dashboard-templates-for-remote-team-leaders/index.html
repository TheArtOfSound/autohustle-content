Team Mood Assessment Templates

Template 1: Weekly Team Pulse Survey

Purpose: Capture team-wide emotional trends and identify areas requiring attention

Instructions: Customize questions for your team culture, send weekly via your preferred survey platform, and track responses over time to identify patterns.

```markdown

Weekly Team Pulse - Week of [DATE]

Overall Team Energy

Rate your team's overall energy level this week:

  • [ ] High Energy (4) - Team feels motivated and engaged
  • [ ] Good Energy (3) - Team is functioning well with minor challenges
  • [ ] Moderate Energy (2) - Team feels neutral, some ups and downs
  • [ ] Low Energy (1) - Team feels drained or disconnected

Collaboration Quality

How would you rate team collaboration this week?

  • [ ] Excellent (4) - Seamless communication and cooperation
  • [ ] Good (3) - Generally positive with minor friction
  • [ ] Fair (2) - Some communication challenges present
  • [ ] Poor (1) - Significant collaboration barriers

Workload & Stress Indicators

Current workload feels:

  • [ ] Manageable and engaging
  • [ ] Busy but sustainable
  • [ ] Overwhelming but temporary
  • [ ] Unsustainable and concerning

Support Needs

What type of support would benefit the team most right now?

  • [ ] Technical resources and tools
  • [ ] Process clarification and structure
  • [ ] Social connection and team building
  • [ ] Individual coaching and development
  • [ ] Workload redistribution
  • [ ] Other: [SPECIFY]

Open Feedback

What's working well for our team this week?

[OPEN TEXT RESPONSE]

What could we improve or address?

[OPEN TEXT RESPONSE]

Survey administered by: [MANAGER NAME]

Team: [TEAM NAME]

Response deadline: [DATE]

```

Template 2: Monthly Team Climate Assessment

Purpose: Deep dive into team dynamics, psychological safety, and long-term trends

Instructions: Use monthly for comprehensive team health evaluation. Combine quantitative ratings with qualitative insights for action planning.

```markdown

Monthly Team Climate Assessment - [MONTH/YEAR]

Psychological Safety Index

Rate your agreement with these statements (1-5 scale):

Communication Openness

  • Team members feel safe expressing different opinions: [1-5]
  • We can discuss mistakes without blame or punishment: [1-5]
  • People ask for help when needed without fear: [1-5]

Trust & Respect

  • Team members trust each other's intentions: [1-5]
  • We respect diverse working styles and perspectives: [1-5]
  • Conflicts are addressed constructively: [1-5]

Innovation & Risk-Taking

  • Team feels safe to experiment and try new approaches: [1-5]
  • We learn from failures rather than avoiding them: [1-5]
  • Creative ideas are welcomed and considered: [1-5]

Team Cohesion Metrics

Connection Strength (1-5 scale)

  • How connected does the team feel despite being remote? [1-5]
  • How well do team members know each other personally? [1-5]
  • How effectively do we celebrate successes together? [1-5]

Shared Purpose

  • Team understands and aligns with our common goals: [1-5]
  • Everyone feels their role contributes meaningfully: [1-5]
  • We have clarity on team priorities and direction: [1-5]

Growth & Development Climate

Rate the team's current state:

  • Learning and skill development opportunities: [1-5]
  • Feedback quality and frequency: [1-5]
  • Career growth support and mentorship: [1-5]

Trend Analysis

Compared to last month, our team's overall wellbeing is:

  • [ ] Significantly improved
  • [ ] Somewhat improved
  • [ ] About the same
  • [ ] Somewhat declined
  • [ ] Significantly declined

Key factors influencing this trend:

[OPEN RESPONSE]

Action Planning

Top 3 strengths to maintain:

  1. [STRENGTH]
  2. [STRENGTH]
  3. [STRENGTH]

Top 3 areas for improvement:

  1. [IMPROVEMENT AREA]
  2. [IMPROVEMENT AREA]
  3. [IMPROVEMENT AREA]

Assessment period: [START DATE] to [END DATE]

Team: [TEAM NAME]

Completed by: [MANAGER NAME]

Next assessment date: [DATE]

```
