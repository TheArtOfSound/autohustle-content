Template 1: General Freelance Services Agreement

When to use: For freelancers offering a wide range of services (e.g., writing, design, consulting) on a non-project-specific basis where scope may evolve over time.

[CLIENT NAME], hereinafter referred to as "Client," agrees to engage [FREELANCER NAME], hereinafter referred to as "Freelancer," for the provision of freelance services under the following terms:

  1. Services Provided

The Freelancer agrees to perform the following services: [DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES]. These services may be adjusted with mutual written agreement.

  1. Compensation

Client agrees to pay Freelancer [AMOUNT] per [HOUR/PROJECT/MONTH], invoiced [WEEKLY/MONTHLY/UPON COMPLETION]. Payment is due within [NUMBER] days of invoice receipt via [PAYMENT METHOD].

  1. Project Timeline

Services will begin on [START DATE] and continue until terminated by either party with [NUMBER]-day written notice.

  1. Revisions & Edits

Freelancer includes [NUMBER] rounds of revisions per deliverable. Additional revisions billed at [RATE] per hour or [FLAT FEE].

  1. Ownership & Rights

Upon full payment, Freelancer transfers all rights, title, and interest in the final deliverables to Client. Preliminary concepts, drafts, and tools remain Freelancer’s property.

  1. Confidentiality

Both parties agree not to disclose confidential information received during the engagement without prior written consent.

  1. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement with [NUMBER] days’ written notice. Client will pay for all services rendered and expenses incurred up to termination date.

  1. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE/COUNTRY]. Any disputes will be resolved in [CITY, STATE/COUNTRY].

Signatures:

_________________________

[CLIENT NAME] – Date: [DATE]

_________________________

[FREELANCER NAME] – Date: [DATE]

Template 2: Website Design Contract

When to use: For freelance web designers creating custom websites for clients, including design, development, and launch.

This Website Design Contract ("Agreement") is made between [CLIENT NAME] ("Client") and [FREELANCER NAME] ("Designer") as of [DATE].

  1. Project Scope

Designer will create a custom website for Client including:

  • [NUMBER] responsive web pages (e.g., Home, About, Contact)
  • [CMS PLATFORM, e.g., WordPress integration]
  • [MOBILE OPTIMIZATION]
  • [BASIC SEO SETUP]
  • Final delivery of fully functional website hosted on [CLIENT’S HOSTING OR FREELANCER’S TEMPORARY SERVER]
  1. Project Timeline
  • Initial mockups delivered by: [DATE]
  • Client feedback due within: [NUMBER] days
  • Development phase: [START DATE] to [END DATE]
  • Launch date: [DATE]
  1. Compensation

Total project fee: [AMOUNT].

Payment schedule:

  • [PERCENTAGE]% ($[AMOUNT]) due upon signing
  • [PERCENTAGE]% ($[AMOUNT]) due upon approval of mockups
  • [PERCENTAGE]% ($[AMOUNT]) due upon final delivery
  1. Revisions

[NUMBER] rounds of revisions included for design mockups. Additional changes billed at [RATE] per hour.

  1. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to provide:

  • Content (text, images, logos) by [DATE]
  • Access to hosting and domain (if applicable)
  • Timely feedback at each stage
  1. Ownership

Upon final payment, all website files and content created by Designer are transferred to Client. Third-party assets (e.g., stock images) subject to their respective licenses.

  1. Launch & Maintenance

Website launch included. Ongoing maintenance available under a separate agreement at [RATE].

  1. Termination

If Client terminates early, non-refundable deposit of [AMOUNT] applies. Freelancer retains rights to all work until full payment.

Signatures:

_________________________

[CLIENT NAME] – Date: [DATE]

_________________________

[FREELANCER NAME] – Date: [DATE]

  • Each approximately [WORD COUNT] words
  • Topics: [TOPICS OR BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
  • Style/tone: [TONE, e.g., professional, conversational]
  • Delivery format: [e.g., Google Docs, Word]
  1. Timeline

Revisions completed by: [DATE]

Final delivery date: [DATE]

  1. Compensation

Total fee: $[AMOUNT]

Payment due within [NUMBER] days of invoice via [PAYMENT METHOD]

[Optional: Rate of $[RATE] per word/article]

[NUMBER] rounds of revisions included. Additional edits billed at $[RATE] per hour.

  1. Client Responsibilities

Client will provide:

  • Prompt feedback within [NUMBER] business days
  1. Termination

Either party may terminate with [NUMBER] days’ notice. Writer will be paid for work completed up to termination.

Template 4: Social Media Management Contract

When to use: For freelancers managing a client’s social media accounts, including content creation and scheduling.

This Social Media Management Contract ("Agreement") is made between [CLIENT NAME] ("Client") and [FREELANCER NAME] ("Manager") effective [DATE].

  1. Services

Manager will provide:

  • Creation of [NUMBER] social media posts per [WEEK/MONTH] for [PLATFORMS, e.g., Instagram, Facebook]
  • Content scheduling via [TOOL, e.g., Buffer, Hootsuite]
  • [BASIC ENGAGEMENT, e.g., responding to comments]
  • [MONTHLY PERFORMANCE REPORT]
  1. Account Access

Client will provide necessary login credentials or grant access via platform tools. Manager agrees to maintain account security and not share credentials.

  1. Content Approval

Client may require approval of all content prior to posting. Manager will submit drafts [NUMBER] days in advance.

  1. Compensation

Monthly fee: $[AMOUNT], due on the [DAY] of each month (e.g., 1st). First payment due upon signing.

  1. Term

This Agreement begins on [START DATE] and continues month-to-month until terminated with [NUMBER] days’ written notice.

  1. Content Ownership

All original content created by Manager is owned by Client upon payment. Manager may showcase work in portfolio unless otherwise agreed.

  1. Reporting

Manager will provide a monthly report by [DAY] of the following month, including:

  • Engagement metrics
  • Follower growth
  • Top-performing content
  1. Termination & Handover

Upon termination, Manager will deliver all content files and account access information within [NUMBER] days.

Template 9: Freelance Service Agreement

When to use: For general freelance services that require a broad agreement covering various aspects of the project.

This Freelance Service Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on [DATE] ("Effective Date") by and between [FREELANCER NAME] ("Freelancer") with a principal place of business at [FREELANCER ADDRESS] and [CLIENT NAME] ("Client") with a principal place of business at [CLIENT ADDRESS].

WHEREAS, Freelancer is a professional [FREELANCER PROFESSION] with expertise in [SPECIFIC SKILLS]; and

WHEREAS, Client desires to engage Freelancer to perform certain services as outlined in this Agreement.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

  1. Services: Freelancer agrees to perform the following services for Client: [DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES].
  2. Term and Termination: The term of this Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until [PROJECT COMPLETION DATE] unless terminated earlier by either party with [NUMBER] days' written notice.
  3. Compensation: Client shall pay Freelancer a total of $[TOTAL COMPENSATION] for the services performed under this Agreement, payable in [NUMBER] installments of $[INSTALLMENT AMOUNT] each, due on [PAYMENT DUE DATES].

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

Freelancer Signature: _____________________________

Client Signature: _____________________________

Date: _______________________________________

Template 10: Non-Disclosure Agreement for Freelancers

When to use: To protect confidential information shared between the freelancer and the client.

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made and entered into on [DATE] by and between [FREELANCER NAME] ("Freelancer") and [CLIENT NAME] ("Client").

  1. Purpose: The Client is willing to disclose certain confidential and proprietary information to the Freelancer for the purpose of [PROJECT PURPOSE]. This Agreement is intended to protect the confidentiality of such information.
  1. Confidential Information: For the purposes of this Agreement, "Confidential Information" means all information, whether written or oral, that is disclosed by the Client to the Freelancer, including but not limited to [TYPES OF CONFIDENTIAL INFORMATION].
  1. Obligations: The Freelancer agrees:
  • To hold the Confidential Information in confidence and not to disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the Client.
  • To use the Confidential Information solely for the purpose of performing the services under this Agreement.
  1. Term: This Agreement shall commence on the date first above written and continue for a period of [LENGTH OF TIME] from the date of disclosure of the Confidential Information.
  1. Remedies: The Freelancer acknowledges that a breach of this Agreement may cause irreparable harm to the Client, and therefore agrees that the Client shall be entitled to seek equitable relief, including injunctions, in addition to such other remedies as may be available at law.

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

Freelancer Signature: _____________________________

Client Signature: _____________________________

Date: _______________________________________

Template 14: Cancellation of Freelance Contract

When to use: To formally cancel a freelance contract.

Cancellation of Freelance Contract

Date: [DATE]

To [CLIENT NAME]

[CLIENT ADDRESS]

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

Re: Cancellation of Freelance Service Agreement dated [ORIGINAL CONTRACT DATE]

This letter serves as formal notice of the cancellation of the Freelance Service Agreement ("Agreement") between [FREELANCER NAME] ("Freelancer") and [CLIENT NAME] ("Client"), effective [CANCELLATION DATE].

The reason for this cancellation is [REASON FOR CANCELLATION, IF DESIRED].

As of the cancellation date, all obligations under the Agreement shall cease, except for those that by their nature are intended to survive cancellation, including but not limited to payment for services rendered prior to cancellation and confidentiality obligations.

Please confirm in writing your acceptance of this cancellation. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us at [FREELANCER CONTACT INFORMATION].

Sincerely,

[FREELANCER NAME]
