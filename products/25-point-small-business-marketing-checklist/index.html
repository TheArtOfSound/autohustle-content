25-Point Small Business Marketing Checklist

Updated: February 26, 2026

25-point checklist to streamline your small business marketing efforts, covering social media, email marketing, and SEO.

What's Inside

  • 25 actionable tasks
  • organized by priority
  • clear instructions

1. Review your business mission statement to ensure it remains relevant and concise.

[ ] Write down your current mission statement.

[ ] Revise and refine it if necessary.

[ ] Implement changes into marketing materials.

[ ] Update website content accordingly.

[ ] Share revised mission statement on social media.

2. Conduct an online audit of competitors' websites, focusing on branding, navigation, and user experience.

[ ] Research competitor brand identities.

[ ] Analyze competitor website navigation and user experience.

[ ] Identify gaps in the market for your business.

[ ] Develop a plan to address these gaps.

[ ] Create a competitor analysis report.

3. Optimize your social media profiles with high-quality, consistent branding across all platforms.

[ ] Review and update profile images.

[ ] Ensure consistent color schemes and typography.

[ ] Write compelling bio descriptions for each platform.

[ ] Set up profile links to other business sites.

[ ] Engage with followers on each platform.

4. Create a content calendar to plan, organize, and schedule blog posts, emails, and social media content.

[ ] Determine content themes and topics.

[ ] Plan out posting schedules for the next month.

[ ] Source potential content ideas.

[ ] Develop a list of relevant keywords.

[ ] Review and adjust the calendar regularly.

5. Set up email marketing automation tools to streamline newsletters and promotional emails.

[ ] Choose an email service provider.

[ ] Create and customize email templates.

[ ] Define lead magnets and opt-in offers.

[ ] Design and schedule email sequences.

[ ] Test and refine automation workflows.

6. Develop a comprehensive SEO strategy, focusing on keyword research, on-page optimization, and link building.

[ ] Conduct thorough keyword research.

[ ] Optimize website structure and content for keywords.

[ ] Build high-quality backlinks from authoritative sources.

[ ] Monitor and adjust the SEO report regularly.

[ ] Analyze competitors' SEO efforts.

7. Create a lead magnet to capture email addresses and start nurturing leads through the sales funnel.

[ ] Develop a valuable resource (e.g., eBook, webinar).

[ ] Design an appealing landing page.

[ ] Optimize for conversions using A/B testing.

[ ] Write persuasive copy and headlines.

[ ] Promote the lead magnet on social media.

8. Host a free webinar or workshop to showcase expertise, build trust with potential customers, and generate leads.

[ ] Choose a topic and date.

[ ] Create engaging content (e.g., slides, video).

[ ] Develop an effective call-to-action (CTA).

[ ] Promote the event on social media.

[ ] Invite speakers and attendees.

9. Develop a referral program to encourage customer loyalty, word-of-mouth marketing, and new leads.

[ ] Determine program goals and target audience.

[ ] Design appealing referral incentives (e.g., discounts, rewards).

[ ] Create a referral tracking system.

[ ] Promote the program on social media.

[ ] Distribute program materials.

10. Establish relationships with influencers in your niche to collaborate, promote content, and expand your reach.

[ ] Research potential influencers.

[ ] Craft compelling pitch messages.

[ ] Share influencer content on social media.

[ ] Engage with influencers' audiences.

[ ] Collaborate on joint projects or content.

11. Optimize website pages for user experience, conversion rates, and search engine rankings.

[ ] Conduct A/B testing to improve page performance.

[ ] Simplify navigation and content organization.

[ ] Ensure mobile-friendliness and responsiveness.

[ ] Write concise, scannable content.

[ ] Use clear calls-to-action.

12. Create a loyalty program to reward repeat customers, retain existing leads, and build brand advocacy.

[ ] Determine customer retention goals.

[ ] Design appealing rewards (e.g., discounts, freebies).

[ ] Develop a points or tiered system.

[ ] Promote the program on social media.

[ ] Track and analyze customer behavior.

13. Leverage user-generated content to showcase success stories, build trust, and create social proof.

[ ] Encourage customers to share their experiences.

[ ] Display user-generated content prominently.

[ ] Share customer testimonials on social media.

[ ] Utilize customer reviews as SEO keywords.

[ ] Create a dedicated section for customer success stories.

14. Develop a retargeting campaign to reach users who have abandoned their shopping carts or visited the website without converting.

[ ] Identify high-value users (e.g., based on purchase history).

[ ] Craft targeted retargeting ads.

[ ] Set up retargeting pixels and tracking codes.

[ ] Optimize ad creative for better performance.

[ ] Monitor and analyze campaign results.

15. Create a social media contest or giveaway to engage with followers, generate buzz, and attract new followers.

[ ] Choose a theme and rules (e.g., photo submission).

[ ] Promote the contest on social media.

[ ] Offer incentives (e.g., discounts, freebies).

[ ] Encourage user-generated content.

[ ] Measure and analyze campaign performance.

16. Develop a customer advocacy program to encourage loyal customers to share their experiences with others.

[ ] Identify key advocates (e.g., based on customer feedback).

[ ] Craft persuasive content (e.g., blog posts, videos).

[ ] Share customer success stories on social media.

[ ] Utilize customer testimonials as social proof.

[ ] Recognize and reward advocates.

17. Optimize video content for YouTube and other platforms to increase engagement, views, and conversions.

[ ] Determine optimal video length and format.

[ ] Write compelling titles and descriptions.

[ ] Develop engaging thumbnails and visuals.

[ ] Optimize video tags for search engines.

[ ] Create a consistent branding across all videos.

18. Leverage email marketing automation tools to personalize and optimize email sequences, improving conversion rates.

[ ] Choose an email service provider with automation capabilities.

[ ] Set up email templates and workflows.

[ ] Define lead magnets and opt-in offers.

[ ] Optimize email subject lines and CTAs.

[ ] Analyze campaign performance regularly.

19. Create a referral program for employees to encourage internal referrals, build brand loyalty, and expand your network.

[ ] Determine program goals and target audience (employees).

[ ] Develop appealing incentives (e.g., discounts, rewards).

[ ] Design an employee referral tracking system.

[ ] Promote the program on company intranet or social media.

[ ] Distribute program materials.

20. Develop a podcast or video series to establish thought leadership, build brand awareness, and attract new audiences.

[ ] Determine target audience and content themes.

[ ] Research potential guests (e.g., influencers, industry experts).

[ ] Create engaging content (e.g., script, audio files).

[ ] Promote the podcast on social media and company channels.

[ ] Distribute podcast episodes.

21. Utilize customer reviews to improve search engine rankings, increase credibility, and attract new customers.

[ ] Identify high-quality reviews from multiple sources.

[ ] Create a dedicated review section on website.

[ ] Encourage customers to leave reviews.

[ ] Respond promptly to all reviews (positive or negative).

[ ] Use customer feedback as SEO keywords.

22. Develop a mobile app or native experience to enhance user experience, increase engagement, and expand revenue streams.

[ ] Determine target audience and use cases.

[ ] Design appealing UI/UX.

[ ] Build engaging content (e.g., tutorials, tips).

[ ] Optimize for mobile performance.

[ ] Launch the app or native experience.

23. Create an e-commerce platform to streamline online sales, improve customer satisfaction, and increase revenue.

[ ] Determine target audience and product offerings.

[ ] Design appealing UI/UX.

[ ] Build engaging content (e.g., tutorials, tips).

[ ] Optimize for user experience.

[ ] Launch the e-commerce platform.

24. Utilize SEO best practices to optimize website content, improve crawlability, and increase organic traffic.

[ ] Conduct thorough keyword research.

[ ] Optimize website structure and content for keywords.

[ ] Build high-quality backlinks from authoritative sources.

[ ] Monitor and analyze search engine rankings regularly.

[ ] Adjust keyword strategy accordingly.

25. Review and refine your overall marketing strategy to ensure alignment with business goals, stay up-to-date with industry trends, and adapt to changing customer needs.
