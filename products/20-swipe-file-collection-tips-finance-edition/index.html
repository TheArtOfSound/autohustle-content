11. Financial Dashboard KPI Definitions

Use case: Providing clarity on metrics used in executive dashboards

Dashboard: Executive Finance Dashboard – [Company Name]

| KPI | Definition | Source | Frequency |

|-----|-----------|--------|---------|

| Revenue | GAAP revenue recognized, net of discounts and returns | General Ledger | Monthly |

| Gross Margin | (Revenue – COGS) / Revenue | P&L | Monthly |

| Operating Cash Flow | Net cash from operating activities | Cash Flow Stmt | Quarterly |

| DSO | (A/R Balance / Revenue) × Days in Period | A/R Subledger | Monthly |

| Burn Rate | Net cash outflow per month (pre-funding) | Cash Rollforward | Weekly |

| CapEx as % of Rev | Total capital expenditures / Revenue | GL & CapEx Register | Quarterly |

Note: All figures subject to month-end close adjustments.
