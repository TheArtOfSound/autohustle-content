Template 1: Brand Voice & Tone Guide

When to use: When establishing or refining your brand’s communication style to ensure consistency across all marketing channels

[BRAND NAME]’s voice reflects who we are and how we connect with our audience. Use this guide to maintain a consistent tone in every message, post, email, and ad.

Brand Personality Traits:

[Choose 3–4 adjectives, e.g., Friendly, Professional, Playful, Trustworthy]

We are [TRAIT 1], [TRAIT 2], and [TRAIT 3]—never [OPPOSITE TRAIT, e.g., robotic, overly formal, or salesy].

Voice Characteristics:

  • Word Choice: [e.g., Simple, conversational, jargon-free]
  • Punctuation & Grammar: [e.g., Use contractions, short sentences, exclamation points sparingly]
  • Emojis: [e.g., Use 1–2 per social post when appropriate]

Tone by Situation:

  • Happy News: [e.g., Warm and celebratory – “We did it! Thanks to YOU, we’ve hit 1,000 customers!”]
  • Customer Issue: [e.g., Empathetic and helpful – “We’re so sorry this happened. Let’s fix it right away.”]
  • Educational Content: [e.g., Clear and encouraging – “Here’s how you can get started in just 3 steps.”]

Use this guide for all customer-facing content, including social media, emails, ads, and website copy.
