Template 5: Kickoff Call Agenda

When to use: To structure the first official meeting with a new client and ensure all key topics are covered.

[Full template body with [PLACEHOLDER] tags for customizable parts]

Kickoff Call Agenda

Client: [CLIENT BUSINESS NAME]

Project: [PROJECT NAME]

Date: [MEETING DATE]

Time: [START TIME] – [END TIME]

Location: [ZOOM LINK / MEETING ROOM / OTHER]

Objective

Align on project goals, timeline, communication, and expectations to ensure a smooth collaboration.

Agenda Items (30–60 mins)

  1. Welcome & Introductions (5 mins)
  • Brief personal & professional background
  • Why we’re working together
  1. Review Project Scope (10 mins)
  • Confirm deliverables: [LIST KEY ITEMS]
  • Clarify what’s included vs. out of scope
  1. Timeline & Milestones (10 mins)
  • Key dates: [START DATE, DRAFT DATE, FINAL DATE]
  • Dependencies: What you need to provide and when
  1. Communication Plan (5 mins)
  • Best way to reach each other
  • Response time expectations
  • Tools we’ll use (e.g., email, Slack, Trello)
  1. Client Input & Assets (10 mins)
  • Brand guidelines, logos, content, references
  • Who will provide feedback/approvals?
  1. Q&A (10 mins)
  • Open floor for any questions or concerns
  1. Next Steps (5 mins)
  • What happens after this call
  • When to expect first draft

Preparation Required:

Please bring:

  • Examples of work you love (websites, designs, copy, etc.)
  • Any brand assets (logos, fonts, color codes)
  • List of must-haves for the project

Looking forward to our conversation!

— [YOUR NAME]
