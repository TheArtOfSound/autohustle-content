Template 3: Onboarding Process

When to use: To outline the steps a client will take during the onboarding process.

Onboarding Process

  1. Welcome Email: You'll receive a welcome email with an overview of our onboarding process and a link to our client questionnaire.
  1. Questionnaire Completion: Please fill out the questionnaire to help us understand your project better.
  1. Project Proposal: We'll review your questionnaire and send you a project proposal outlining the scope, timeline, and pricing.
  1. Contract Signing: Upon agreement on the proposal, we'll send you a contract for your signature.
  1. Invoice Payment: You'll receive an invoice for the project deposit. Upon payment, we'll officially kick off the project.
  1. Project Kickoff: We'll schedule a kickoff meeting to discuss the project in detail and answer any questions you may have.

Template 10: Freelance Designer Contract

When to use: Before starting work on a project to establish a legally binding agreement between the freelancer and the client.

FREELANCE DESIGNER CONTRACT

This Contract is made on [CONTRACT DATE] by and between [FREELANCER NAME] (the "Freelancer") and [CLIENT COMPANY NAME] (the "Client").

  1. Scope of Work:

The Freelancer agrees to provide [PROJECT TYPE] services as described in the project proposal dated [PROPOSAL DATE].

  1. Payment Terms:
  • The Client shall pay the Freelancer a total of [PROJECT COST] for the services outlined in the scope of work.
  • Payment shall be made according to the following schedule: [PAYMENT SCHEDULE].
  1. Intellectual Property:

The Freelancer retains the copyright to all design work produced during the project. Upon full payment, the Client is granted [LICENSE TYPE] rights to use the design work for [SPECIFIED USE].

  1. Confidentiality:

Both parties agree to keep confidential any information marked as confidential or which, under the circumstances, ought to be treated as confidential.

  1. Termination:

Either party may terminate this Contract upon [NUMBER] days written notice to the other. Upon termination, the Freelancer shall deliver all completed work and the Client shall pay for all services rendered up to the date of termination.

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Contract.

Freelancer Signature: [FREELANCER SIGNATURE]

Client Signature: [CLIENT SIGNATURE]

Date: [CONTRACT SIGNING DATE]
