12 Client Onboarding Templates for Freelance Copywriters

Updated: March 07, 2026

12 client onboarding templates for freelance copywriters — includes project briefs, welcome sequences, and scope agreements.

What's Inside

  • 12 plug-and-play templates
  • covers briefs, welcome emails, and project agreements
  • designed for clarity and professionalism

Template 1: Client Onboarding Welcome Email

When to use: Immediately after a client signs your contract and pays a deposit — this email kicks off the working relationship warmly and professionally.

Subject: Welcome, [Client Name]! Let’s Get Started on [Project Name]

Hi [Client Name],

Welcome — I’m so excited to be working with you on [Project Name]!

Your enthusiasm for [brief mention of client goal, e.g., “growing your online course business”] is inspiring, and I can’t wait to help you achieve those goals with powerful, results-driven copy.

Here’s what happens next:

✅ I’ve received your signed agreement and [deposit amount] deposit — thank you!

📅 We’ve scheduled our kickoff call for [date] at [time] [time zone] — you’ll find the calendar invite attached.

📝 Before we meet, I’d appreciate it if you could complete the brief questionnaire (linked here) to help me understand your brand voice, audience, and goals.

In the meantime, feel free to reply with any initial thoughts or materials (like website links, brand guidelines, or competitor examples).

Looking forward to getting started!

Warmly,

[Your Name]

[Your Business Name]

[Email Address] | [Phone Number]

[Website URL]

P.S. Got questions before our call? Just hit reply — I’m happy to help!

What’s the main goal of this project? (e.g., boost conversions, launch a new product, refresh messaging)

→ [Client answers here]

→ [Client answers here]

  1. Brand Voice & Tone:

→ [Client answers here]

  1. Competitors:

Who are 2–3 competitors you admire (or want to stand out from)?

→ [Client answers here]

  1. Examples You Love:

Share links to copy (emails, websites, ads) that inspire you — and tell me why.

→ [Client answers here]

→ [Client answers here]

  1. Timeline:

  1. Additional Assets:

Template 3: Kickoff Call Agenda

When to use: Shared 24 hours before the first strategy call to set expectations and structure the conversation.

Subject: Agenda for Our [Project Name] Kickoff Call – [Date] at [Time]

Hi [Client Name],

Looking forward to our call tomorrow! To make the most of our time, here’s the agenda:

[Project Name] Kickoff Call – [Date] at [Time] [Time Zone]

Duration: [30/45/60] minutes

Location: [Zoom/Google Meet/Phone Call Link]

Agenda:

  1. 🎯 Project Goals & Success Metrics (10 min)
  • Reaffirm primary objectives
  • Define what “success” looks like
  1. 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Audience Deep Dive (10 min)
  • Review ideal customer profile
  • Discuss pain points and motivations
  1. 🎙️ Brand Voice & Messaging (10 min)
  • Finalize tone and style
  • Review competitor examples
  1. 📦 Deliverables & Timeline (10 min)
  • Confirm exact deliverables and formats
  • Review milestones and deadlines
  1. 🔄 Feedback & Revisions Process (5 min)
  • How and when to share feedback
  • Number of revision rounds included
  1. ❓ Open Q&A (5 min)

Prep Needed:

  • Please bring any final thoughts or reference materials
  • Have access to your website, product, or relevant tools if possible

Looking forward to aligning and getting creative!

Warmly,

[Your Name]

[Your Business Name]

Template 4: Freelance Copywriting Contract (Basic)

When to use: For straightforward copywriting projects under $2,000 — sent after initial discussions and before work begins.

[Your Business Name] Copywriting Services Agreement

This agreement is made between:

[Your Full Name], operating as [Your Business Name], (“Copywriter”)

and

[Client Full Name or Company Name], (“Client”)

on this [Date] day of [Month], [Year].

1. Scope of Work

The Copywriter agrees to provide the following services:

  • [Deliverable 1, e.g., 1 high-converting sales page]
  • [Deliverable 2, e.g., 3 email sequences for launch]
  • [Deliverable 3, e.g., 5 ad variations for Facebook/Google]

All deliverables will be created based on brand guidelines and input provided by the Client.

2. Project Timeline

  • First draft delivery: [Date]
  • Revision period: [Number] business days
  • Final delivery: [Date]

Delays caused by late feedback may adjust deadlines accordingly.

3. Payment Terms

  • Total project fee: [Amount]
  • Deposit due upon signing: [Amount or %] (non-refundable)
  • Final payment due upon delivery of final copy: [Amount]
  • Payment method: [Bank Transfer, PayPal, Stripe, etc.]

4. Revisions

  • [Number, e.g., 2] rounds of revisions included per deliverable
  • Additional revisions: [Rate, e.g., $75/hour or $50 per round]

5. Ownership & Usage

Final copy becomes the property of the Client upon full payment. The Copywriter retains the right to display the work in their portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

6. Cancellation Policy

  • If Client cancels after deposit: Deposit is non-refundable
  • If Client cancels after work begins: Invoice for hours worked at [Hourly Rate]

7. Signatures

By signing below, both parties agree to the terms of this agreement.

___________________________

[Your Full Name]

[Your Business Name]

Date: _______________

___________________________

[Client Full Name]

[Client Company Name]

Date: _______________

  • Email: [Your Email] – I respond within 24 business hours (Mon–Fri)
  • Phone/Text: [Your Phone] – Only for urgent issues (please include “URGENT” in the message)
  • Scheduled Calls: Use [Calendly Link] for non-kickoff meetings

🕐 Response Times

  • Emails: Within 24 business hours (not including weekends)
  • Draft Feedback: Please respond within [Number] business days to keep us on schedule
  • Revisions: I’ll return edits within [Number] days of receiving your notes

📁 File Sharing

Please send all assets (logos, brand guides, product details) via:

🔗 [Google Drive Folder Link] or reply to this email (under 10MB)

🚫 What I Don’t Offer

  • 24/7 availability
  • Same-day revisions (unless rush fee is paid)
  • Real-time edits during weekends (unless pre-arranged)

Sticking to this rhythm keeps your project on time and high quality. Thanks for respecting my workflow — I’ll do the same for you!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 11: Client Onboarding Questionnaire

When to use: Send this template to new clients at the beginning of the onboarding process to gather essential information about their project.

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

I'm excited to start working on your project. To ensure I deliver high-quality content that meets your expectations, I've put together a brief questionnaire to gather some essential information. Please take a few minutes to complete the following:

  1. What is the primary goal of this project? [PROJECT GOAL]
  2. Who is your target audience? [TARGET AUDIENCE]
  3. What sets your product/service apart from the competition? [UNIQUE SELLING POINT]
  4. What tone do you want to convey in the content? (e.g., formal, friendly, humorous) [TONE]
  5. Are there any specific keywords or phrases you'd like me to include? [KEYWORDS]
  6. Do you have any existing brand guidelines (e.g., logos, color schemes, fonts) that I should follow? [BRAND GUIDELINES]
  7. What is the expected word count or content length for this project? [WORD COUNT]
  8. Are there any specific deadlines or milestones I should be aware of? [DEADLINES]
  9. How would you like to communicate throughout the project (e.g., email, phone, project management tool)? [COMMUNICATION PREFERENCE]

10. Is there anything else you'd like to share about your project or expectations? [ADDITIONAL COMMENTS]

Thank you for taking the time to complete this questionnaire. I'll review your responses and use them to create high-quality content that meets your needs.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

Subject: Welcome to [YOUR BUSINESS NAME] - Onboarding Next Steps

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

A warm welcome to [YOUR BUSINESS NAME]! I'm thrilled to have you on board, and I'm excited to start working on your project. Thank you for taking the time to complete the onboarding questionnaire. I've reviewed your responses, and I'm looking forward to creating high-quality content that meets your expectations.

Here's a brief overview of what you can expect from our collaboration:

  • I'll review your project details and create a customized content plan tailored to your needs.
  • You'll receive regular updates on my progress, and I'll be in touch if I have any questions or need further clarification.
  • Once the content is complete, I'll deliver it to you in [DELIVERY FORMAT, e.g., Word document, Google Doc] for your review and feedback.
  • We'll work together to revise and refine the content until you're satisfied with the final result.

If you have any questions or concerns, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm always here to help. You can expect to hear from me within [RESPONSE TIME, e.g., 24 hours] if you have any questions or need support.

Thank you again for choosing [YOUR BUSINESS NAME]. I'm excited to work with you and deliver exceptional content that helps you achieve your goals.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]
