Project Details:

  • Project name: [PROJECT NAME]
  • Project timeline: [PROJECT TIMELINE]
  • Deliverables: [DELIVERABLES]

Design Requirements:

  • Color scheme: [COLOR SCHEME]
  • Font styles: [FONT STYLES]
  • Imagery style: [IMAGERY STYLE]
