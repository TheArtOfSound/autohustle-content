When to use: After initial contact, to gather detailed project and brand information from the client.

Subject: Let’s Get to Know Your Project, [Client Name]!

Hi [Client Name],

To ensure I deliver exactly what you’re looking for, I’d love to learn more about your project, goals, and brand. Please take 10–15 minutes to fill out the questions below.

You can reply directly to this email or copy these questions into a document — whatever’s easiest for you.

Project Overview

  • What is the name of your project or business? [Project/Business Name]
  • What type of project are we working on? (e.g., logo, website, branding, packaging) [Project Type]
  • What are your top 3 goals for this project? [Goal 1], [Goal 2], [Goal 3]
  • Who is your target audience? [Target Audience Description]
  • Who are your top 3 competitors? [Competitor 1], [Competitor 2], [Competitor 3]

  • How would you describe your brand’s personality? (e.g., modern, playful, luxurious) [Brand Personality]
  • Do you have brand guidelines? If yes, please attach or share a link. [Link or “None”]
  • Preferred colors (if any): [Color 1], [Color 2]
  • Preferred fonts or typography styles: [Font Preferences]
  • Design styles you love (attach examples if possible): [Inspiration Notes]
  • Design styles you dislike: [Avoidance Notes]

Timeline & Deliverables

  • What is your ideal project completion date? [Target Deadline]
  • Do you have any key milestones or launch dates? [Key Dates]
  • What final deliverables do you expect? (e.g., logo files, style guide, website) [Deliverables List]

Additional Notes

  • Anything else I should know before we begin? [Open Response]

Template 4: Freelance Design Contract

When to use: Before starting work, to legally define project terms, payments, and ownership.

Freelance Design Contract

This Agreement is made between:

Designer: [Your Full Name], operating as [Your Business Name], hereinafter referred to as “Designer”

Client: [Client Full Name or Company], hereinafter referred to as “Client”

Effective Date: [Contract Start Date]

Project: [Project Name]

1. Scope of Work

The Designer agrees to provide the following services:

  • [Service 1, e.g., Logo design and variations]
  • [Service 2, e.g., Brand color and typography system]
  • [Service 3, e.g., Social media kit]

Additional requests outside this scope may be billed separately or require a change order.

2. Project Timeline

  • Project start date: [Start Date]
  • Estimated completion date: [End Date]

Delays caused by Client feedback or material delivery may adjust the timeline.

3. Payment Terms

  • Total Fee: [Total Amount]
  • Payment 1 (50%): [Amount] due on signing
  • Payment 2 (50%): [Amount] due before final delivery

Late payments are subject to a [X]% monthly fee.

4. Revisions

The Client is entitled to [Number, e.g., 2] rounds of revisions per deliverable. Additional revisions: [Rate, e.g., $50/hour].

5. Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, the Designer transfers final design files and full commercial usage rights to the Client.

The Designer retains the right to display the work in their portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

6. Cancellations

If the Client cancels:

  • Before work begins: Full refund of deposit
  • After work begins: Deposit is non-refundable; Client pays for time spent

7. Governing Law

This contract is governed by the laws of [State/Country].

Signatures

_________________________

[Your Full Name]

Designer

Date: [Date Signed]

_________________________

[Client Full Name]

Client

Date: [Date Signed]

Date: [Call Date] | Time: [Start Time] – [End Time]

Duration: [e.g., 30 minutes]

Location: [Zoom/Google Meet/Phone] – [Meeting Link]

Align on project goals, timelines, and communication preferences.

📋 Agenda

  1. Welcome & Introductions (5 min)
  • Brief personal intro
  • Confirm roles and contact preferences
  1. Project Goals Review (10 min)
  • Recap of client’s goals from questionnaire
  • Clarify top priorities and success metrics
  1. Scope & Deliverables (5 min)
  • Walk through final deliverables
  • Confirm any changes or additions
  1. Timeline & Milestones (5 min)
  • Review key dates and delivery schedule
  • Confirm client availability for feedback
  1. Feedback Process (3 min)
  • Explain revision policy
  • Preferred feedback format (e.g., written notes, video)
  1. Q&A (7 min)
  • Answer client questions
  • Address concerns or blockers
  1. Next Steps (2 min)
  • Confirm when first draft is due
  • Share how updates will be communicated

Notes:

[Leave space for notes during call]
