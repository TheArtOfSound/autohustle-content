Streamline your executive function and optimize your entrepreneurial workflow with neurodivergent-friendly templates

Introduction

Welcome to NeuroFlow, a comprehensive collection of cognitive load management templates specifically designed for ADHD entrepreneurs and small business owners. These templates acknowledge the unique challenges of executive dysfunction while leveraging the creative strengths and hyperfocus abilities common in neurodivergent minds.

Each template in this pack is built with ADHD-friendly principles:

  • Visual clarity with clear sections and minimal cognitive load
  • Flexible structure that adapts to your changing needs
  • Dopamine-friendly progress tracking and reward systems
  • Executive function support through external scaffolding
  • Energy-aware planning that respects your natural rhythms

How to Use This Template Pack

  1. Start small - Choose one template that addresses your biggest current challenge
  2. Customize freely - Adapt the language and structure to match your thinking style
  3. Make it visual - Use colors, symbols, or drawings to enhance engagement
  4. Build gradually - Add new templates as you establish routines with existing ones
  5. Review regularly - These templates work best with consistent use and periodic adjustment
