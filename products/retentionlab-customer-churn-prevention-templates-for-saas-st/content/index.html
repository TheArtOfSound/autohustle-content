Template 2: Churn Exit Interview Script

Purpose: Gather structured feedback from churned customers to identify improvement opportunities.

Instructions: Customize questions based on your product and common churn reasons. Use this within 24-48 hours of churn notification.

```markdown

Customer Exit Interview: [CUSTOMER_NAME]

Pre-Interview Preparation

  • Interviewer: [INTERVIEWER_NAME]
  • Date/Time: [INTERVIEW_DATETIME]
  • Customer Contact: [CONTACT_NAME_AND_TITLE]
  • Relationship Duration: [MONTHS_AS_CUSTOMER]
  • Final MRR: $[MONTHLY_VALUE]
  • Cancellation Reason Given: [INITIAL_REASON_STATED]

Opening (2-3 minutes)

"Hi [CONTACT_NAME], thank you for taking the time to speak with us. I know you've decided to move on from [PRODUCT_NAME], and while we're disappointed to see you go, we'd really value your feedback to help us improve. This should take about [15-20] minutes. Everything you share will help us serve future customers better."

Core Questions

1. Decision Process

  • What initially led you to consider leaving [PRODUCT_NAME]?
  • How long were you thinking about this decision before acting?
  • Who else was involved in this decision?
  • What was the final trigger that made you cancel?

2. Product Experience

  • What did [PRODUCT_NAME] do well for your business?
  • Where did we fall short of your expectations?
  • Were there specific features or capabilities you needed that we didn't provide?
  • How would you rate the overall user experience? [1-10 SCALE]

3. Value & Pricing

  • Did you feel you were getting good value for the price you were paying?
  • How did our pricing compare to alternatives you considered?
  • If we had offered [DISCOUNT_PERCENTAGE]% discount, would that have changed your decision?

4. Support & Success

  • How would you rate the support you received from our team? [1-10 SCALE]
  • Did you feel we understood your business goals and helped you achieve them?
  • What could our customer success team have done differently?

5. Competitive Landscape

  • Are you moving to a competitor? If so, which one?
  • What made that solution more attractive?
  • How did you evaluate different options?

6. Future Relationship

  • Would you consider [PRODUCT_NAME] again in the future?
  • Would you recommend us to others despite your decision to leave?
  • Can we stay in touch for potential future opportunities?

Closing

"Thank you so much for this feedback. It's incredibly valuable. Is there anything else you'd like to share that might help us improve?"

Post-Interview Analysis

  • Primary Churn Reason: [MAIN_REASON_CATEGORY]
  • Secondary Factors: [CONTRIBUTING_FACTORS]
  • Preventable? [YES/NO] - [EXPLANATION]
  • Product Improvement Opportunities: [SPECIFIC_SUGGESTIONS]
  • Process Improvement Opportunities: [INTERNAL_CHANGES_NEEDED]
  • Follow-up Actions: [NEXT_STEPS]

Internal Distribution

Share insights with:

  • [ ] Product Team
  • [ ] Customer Success Team
  • [ ] Sales Team
  • [ ] Leadership Team

```

Template 3: Customer Health Score Calculator

Purpose: Create a systematic approach to monitor customer health and predict churn risk.

Instructions: Adapt the metrics and weights based on your product's key success indicators. Update thresholds monthly based on data analysis.

```markdown

Customer Health Score System for [COMPANY_NAME]

Scoring Framework Overview

Total Score: 100 points across 5 categories

  • Product Usage (30 points)
  • Engagement (20 points)
  • Support Health (15 points)
  • Business Outcomes (20 points)
  • Relationship Strength (15 points)

Category Breakdowns

1. Product Usage (30 points)

Login Frequency (10 points)

  • Daily login: 10 points
  • 3-6 times/week: 8 points
  • 1-2 times/week: 5 points
  • Less than weekly: 0 points

Feature Adoption (10 points)

  • Uses [CORE_FEATURE_COUNT]+ core features: 10 points
  • Uses [MODERATE_FEATURE_COUNT] core features: 7 points
  • Uses [MINIMAL_FEATURE_COUNT] core features: 4 points
  • Uses fewer than [MINIMAL_FEATURE_COUNT]: 0 points

Data/Content Volume (10 points)

  • Above [HIGH_USAGE_THRESHOLD]: 10 points
  • [MEDIUM_USAGE_RANGE]: 7 points
  • [LOW_USAGE_RANGE]: 4 points
  • Below [MINIMUM_USAGE_THRESHOLD]: 0 points

2. Engagement (20 points)

Email Engagement (10 points)

  • Opens >50% + clicks regularly: 10 points
  • Opens 25-50%: 7 points
  • Opens <25%: 3 points
  • No engagement: 0 points

In-App Engagement (10 points)

  • Completes onboarding + explores features: 10 points
  • Completes onboarding only: 7 points
  • Partial onboarding: 3 points
  • No onboarding progress: 0 points

3. Support Health (15 points)

Ticket Volume & Sentiment (15 points)

  • 0-1 tickets, positive sentiment: 15 points
  • 2-3 tickets, neutral sentiment: 10 points
  • 4+ tickets or negative sentiment: 5 points
  • Escalated issues or angry feedback: 0 points

4. Business Outcomes (20 points)

Goal Achievement (20 points)

  • Exceeding stated goals: 20 points
  • Meeting stated goals: 15 points
  • Progressing toward goals: 10 points
  • No progress on goals: 0 points

5. Relationship Strength (15 points)

Champion Presence (8 points)

  • Strong internal champion: 8 points
  • Moderate champion: 5 points
  • Weak/no champion: 0 points

Expansion Signals (7 points)

  • Asking about upgrades/add-ons: 7 points
  • Adding users: 5 points
  • No expansion interest: 0 points

Health Score Ranges

  • 90-100: Advocate (Green)
  • 75-89: Healthy (Green)
  • 60-74: Stable (Yellow)
  • 45-59: At Risk (Orange)
  • 0-44: Critical (Red)

Automated Triggers

Score 90-100 (Advocate)

  • [ ] Tag for expansion opportunities
  • [ ] Request case study/testimonial
  • [ ] Invite to reference calls
  • [ ] Consider for advisory board

Score 75-89 (Healthy)

  • [ ] Continue regular check-ins
  • [ ] Share relevant resources
  • [ ] Monitor for expansion opportunities

Score 60-74 (Stable)

  • [ ] Increase check-in frequency
  • [ ] Provide additional training
  • [ ] Identify improvement opportunities

Score 45-59 (At Risk)

  • [ ] Schedule immediate call with CSM
  • [ ] Executive outreach within 48 hours
  • [ ] Create improvement plan
  • [ ] Weekly monitoring

Score 0-44 (Critical)

  • [ ] Immediate escalation to leadership
  • [ ] Emergency intervention plan
  • [ ] Daily monitoring
  • [ ] Consider retention offers

Monthly Review Process

1. Calculate scores for all customers

2. Identify score trends (improving/declining)

3. Review threshold effectiveness

4. Update scoring weights based on churn data

5. Share insights with team

Implementation Notes

  • Update scores [FREQUENCY - WEEKLY/DAILY]
  • Integrate with [CRM_SYSTEM_NAME]
  • Alert CSM when score drops [THRESHOLD_POINTS] points
  • Review scoring model [REVIEW_FREQUENCY]

Template 4: Early Warning System Setup

Purpose: Create automated alerts for potential churn signals to enable proactive intervention.

Instructions: Configure these triggers in your analytics platform or CRM. Adjust thresholds based on your baseline customer behavior data.

```markdown

Early Warning System: Churn Prevention Alerts

Alert Configuration for [PRODUCT_NAME]

Tier 1: Immediate Action Required (24-hour response)

Usage Drop Alert

  • Trigger: [USAGE_METRIC] drops by [PERCENTAGE]% compared to [TIME_PERIOD] average
  • Threshold: [SPECIFIC_THRESHOLD_VALUE]
  • Alert Recipients: [PRIMARY_CSM], [BACKUP_CSM], [MANAGER_EMAIL]
  • Auto-actions: Create high-priority task in [TASK_SYSTEM]

Login Frequency Alert

  • Trigger: No login for [NUMBER_OF_DAYS] consecutive days
  • Exceptions: Exclude customers with [EXCEPTION_CRITERIA]
  • Alert Recipients: [CSM_EMAIL_LIST]
  • Auto-actions: Send [AUTOMATED_EMAIL_TEMPLATE_NAME]

Support Escalation Alert

  • Trigger: Support ticket marked as [ESCALATION_LEVEL] or contains keywords: [NEGATIVE_KEYWORDS_LIST]
  • Alert Recipients: [CSM_EMAIL], [SUPPORT_MANAGER], [EXECUTIVE_EMAIL]
  • Auto-actions: Flag account in CRM, schedule follow-up call

Tier 2: Monitor Closely (48-hour response)

Engagement Decline Alert

  • Trigger: Email open rate below [PERCENTAGE]% for [TIME_PERIOD]
  • Additional factors: Combined with [SECONDARY_METRIC] decline
  • Alert Recipients: [CSM_EMAIL]
  • Auto-actions: Add to weekly review list

Feature Abandonment Alert

  • Trigger: Core feature [FEATURE_NAME] unused for [TIME_PERIOD]
  • Context: Customer previously active with this feature
  • Alert Recipients: [CSM_EMAIL]
  • Auto-actions: Trigger educational email sequence

Payment Issues Alert

  • Trigger: Payment failure or [DAYS_OVERDUE] days overdue
  • Alert Recipients: [CSM_EMAIL], [BILLING_TEAM]
  • Auto-actions: Pause churn risk other alerts until resolved

Tier 3: Track & Trend (Weekly review)

Health Score Decline

  • Trigger: Health score drops [POINTS] points in [TIME_PERIOD]
  • Threshold: Only for scores above [MINIMUM_SCORE]
  • Review frequency: Weekly batch report
  • Recipients: [CSM_TEAM_EMAIL]

Seat Reduction

  • Trigger: User count decreases by [NUMBER/PERCENTAGE]
  • Context: Track for [MONITORING_PERIOD] before alerting
  • Recipients: [CSM_EMAIL], [SALES_REP]

Alert Response Playbooks

For Usage Drop Alerts

1. Immediate (Same day)

  • Review recent support tickets
  • Check for product issues/outages
  • Send personalized outreach: [EMAIL_TEMPLATE_LINK]

2. Within 24 hours

  • Schedule call with primary contact
  • Prepare usage report and recommendations
  • Involve technical team if needed

3. Follow-up (48-72 hours)

  • Document findings and action plan
  • Set monitoring schedule
  • Update customer health score

For Support Escalation Alerts

1. Immediate (Within 2 hours)

  • Contact customer directly
  • Loop in relevant technical resources
  • Escalate internally if needed

2. Within 24 hours

  • Provide resolution timeline
  • Schedule follow-up call
  • Document impact on relationship

3. Post-resolution

  • Conduct satisfaction check
  • Identify process improvements
  • Update customer success plan

Alert Effectiveness Tracking

Monthly Review Metrics

  • Alert accuracy: [TARGET_PERCENTAGE]% of alerts lead to actual risk
  • Response time: Average [TARGET_HOURS] hours from alert to action
  • Prevention success: [TARGET_PERCENTAGE]% of alerted customers retained
  • False positive rate: Keep below [MAXIMUM_PERCENTAGE]%

Optimization Process

1. Review alert performance monthly

2. Adjust thresholds based on false positive/negative rates

3. Update playbooks based on successful interventions

4. Train team on new alert types and responses
