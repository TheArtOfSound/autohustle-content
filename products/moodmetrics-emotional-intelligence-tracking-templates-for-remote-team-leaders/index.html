MoodMetrics: Emotional Intelligence Tracking Templates for Remote Team Leaders

Overview

Remote team leadership requires a new approach to understanding and supporting your team's emotional wellbeing. MoodMetrics provides comprehensive templates and frameworks to help you monitor, analyze, and improve the emotional health of your distributed workforce.

This template pack includes scientifically-backed assessment tools, communication frameworks, and intervention strategies specifically designed for remote work environments. Each template is customizable and can be implemented immediately to strengthen team cohesion and individual performance.

Team Emotional Health Assessment Templates

Template 1: Weekly Team Pulse Survey

Purpose: Capture team-wide emotional trends and identify potential issues before they escalate.

Instructions: Send this survey to your entire team every Friday. Analyze trends over time to identify patterns and seasonal variations in team morale.
