Welcome & Project Overview Templates

Template 1: Welcome Email to New Client

Instructions: Send this email immediately after a client accepts your proposal to set a professional tone and outline next steps.

Subject: Welcome to [YOUR BUSINESS NAME] - Let's Get Started on [PROJECT NAME]!

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

Thank you for choosing [YOUR BUSINESS NAME] for your [PROJECT TYPE] needs! I'm excited to work with you on [PROJECT NAME] and help you achieve [CLIENT'S MAIN GOAL].

What happens next:

  1. I'll send you a detailed project brief within [TIMEFRAME] to confirm all requirements
  2. You'll receive our service agreement for review and signature
  3. We'll schedule a kickoff call for [PROPOSED DATE/TIME]
  4. Project work begins on [START DATE]

Project Overview:

  • Estimated Timeline: [DURATION]
  • Deliverables: [LIST MAIN DELIVERABLES]
  • Investment: [TOTAL COST]
  • Payment Schedule: [PAYMENT TERMS]

I've attached a client welcome packet with important information about our working relationship, including communication preferences and project milestones.

If you have any questions before we begin, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm here to make this process as smooth as possible for you.

Looking forward to creating something amazing together!

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

[YOUR TITLE]

[CONTACT INFORMATION]
