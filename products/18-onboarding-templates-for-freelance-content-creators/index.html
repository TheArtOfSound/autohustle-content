Template 1: Welcome Email to New Clients

When to use: Immediately after a client signs your contract or makes a deposit

Welcome! I’m so excited to be working with you on [Project Name or Type of Content]. I’m committed to making this process smooth, collaborative, and productive.

Here’s what you can expect next:

  • I’ll send over a brief to gather all the details needed to get started.
  • We’ll schedule a quick 15-minute kickoff call to align on goals, tone, and timeline.
  • You’ll receive the first draft by [Estimated Delivery Date].

If you have any immediate questions or materials you’d like to share (brand guidelines, past content, inspiration), feel free to reply to this email.

Looking forward to creating something great together!

Best,

[Your Name]

[Your Business Name, if applicable]

[Email Address] | [Website URL]

Template 2: Client Onboarding Questionnaire

When to use: After initial contact, to gather project-specific details

Hi [Client Name],

Thanks again for choosing me to create content for [Company/Project Name]. To ensure I deliver exactly what you’re looking for, I’d appreciate your input on the following:

1. Project Overview

  • What is the main goal of this content? (e.g., drive traffic, generate leads, boost engagement)
  • Who is the target audience? (Please describe age, interests, pain points, etc.)
  • Where will this content be published? (e.g., blog, LinkedIn, email newsletter)

2. Brand Voice & Style

  • How would you describe your brand voice? (e.g., professional, conversational, witty)
  • Are there any brand guidelines or style documents you can share?
  • Share 2–3 examples of content you love (from your site or others).

3. Content Details

  • Preferred word count or length?
  • Any keywords or topics that must be included?
  • Do you need visuals, CTAs, or links included?

4. Timeline & Deliverables

  • What is your ideal deadline for the first draft?
  • How many rounds of revisions do you expect?
  • Who is the final decision-maker for approvals?

Please reply with your answers or fill out this document and send it back. I’ll get started as soon as I have your input!

Warmly,

[Your Name]

Template 4: Project Kickoff Call Agenda

When to use: Before starting work, to align expectations via a short call

Project Kickoff Call – [Client Name] – [Project Title]

Duration: 15–20 minutes

Date: [Date & Time]

Platform: [e.g., Zoom, Google Meet – include link]

Agenda:

  1. Welcome & Introductions (2 min)
  2. Project Goals & Success Metrics (5 min)
  • What does success look like?
  • How will we measure it?
  1. Audience & Messaging (3 min)
  • Any nuances about the audience I should know?
  • Key messages to emphasize or avoid?
  1. Brand Voice & Examples (3 min)
  • Review tone preferences and sample content
  1. Logistics (4 min)
  • Finalize deadlines and deliverables
  • Clarify revision process and approval chain
  • Confirm file formats and submission method
  1. Q&A (3 min)
  2. Next Steps (1 min)
  • I’ll send a summary and first draft by [Date]

Please come ready to share any last-minute insights or materials. Looking forward to it!

Best,

[Your Name]

Template 6: Content Style Guide (Client-Specific)

When to use: After learning a client’s preferences, to document their unique voice and rules

[Client Company Name] Content Style Guide

Last Updated: [Date]

Brand Voice:

[Description: e.g., “Approachable expert—clear, confident, and conversational without being casual.”]

Do’s and Don’ts:

✅ Do: Use contractions, address readers as “you,” focus on benefits

❌ Don’t: Use jargon, passive voice, or exclamation points (!)

Tone by Context:

  • Blog Posts: Informative and engaging
  • Social Media: Friendly and concise
  • Email Copy: Urgent but polite

Grammar & Formatting Rules:

  • Spelling: [e.g., US English]
  • Punctuation: [e.g., Oxford comma: yes/no]
  • Heading Case: [e.g., Title Case for H1/H2]
  • Numbers: [e.g., spell out one to nine]

Preferred Words:

Use: [e.g., “platform,” “solution,” “grow”]

Avoid: [e.g., “tool,” “fix,” “explode”]

CTA Examples:

  • “Start your free trial”
  • “Get the full report”
  • “See how it works”

Approved by: [Client Name]

For use on all future content projects.

  • The Recipient shall maintain the confidentiality of the Confidential Information and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the prior written consent of the Discloser.
  1. Exclusions:

  • Publicly available
  • Already known to the Recipient
  • Obtained from a third party having the right to disclose such information
  • Required to be disclosed by law
  1. Term and Termination:
  • This Agreement shall commence on the Effective Date and continue until [TERM LENGTH] from the date of disclosure of the Confidential Information.
  • Upon termination, the Recipient shall return or destroy all Confidential Information.
