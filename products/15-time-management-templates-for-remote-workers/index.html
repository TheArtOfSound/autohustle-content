Template 6: Daily Time Blocking Planner

When to use: When you need to structure your entire remote workday into focused blocks of time to minimize distractions and increase productivity.

[TIME BLOCK] | [TASK/ACTIVITY] | [PRIORITY (High/Med/Low)] | [NOTES/OUTCOMES]

---|---|---|---

[7:00 AM - 8:00 AM] | [Morning Routine & Planning] | [High] | [Prepare for the day, review goals]

[8:00 AM - 10:00 AM] | [[TASK: e.g., Client Project Draft]] | [High] | [Focus work – no distractions]

[10:00 AM - 10:15 AM] | [Short Break + Stretch] | [Low] | [Hydrate, move around]

[10:15 AM - 12:00 PM] | [[TASK: e.g., Email & Communication]] | [Medium] | [Respond to messages, check updates]

[12:00 PM - 1:00 PM] | [Lunch Break] | [Low] | [Step away from desk]

[1:00 PM - 2:30 PM] | [[TASK: e.g., Creative Work or Deep Focus]] | [High] | [Use Pomodoro if needed]

[2:30 PM - 2:45 PM] | [Break + Walk] | [Low] | [Refresh mind]

[2:45 PM - 4:00 PM] | [[TASK: e.g., Admin & Follow-ups]] | [Medium] | [Invoices, scheduling, etc.]

[4:00 PM - 4:30 PM] | [Review & Plan Tomorrow] | [High] | [Update task list, reflect]

Notes: Adjust time blocks based on your energy levels. Label each task with a project name ([PROJECT NAME]) for better tracking.
