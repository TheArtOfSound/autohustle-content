Instructions: Every morning, list all tasks for the day and categorize them. Focus on Quadrant 1 first, then Quadrant 2. Delegate or eliminate Quadrants 3 and 4 when possible.

| [TASK_DESCRIPTION] | [CLIENT_NAME] | [XX minutes] | [TIME/DATE] | [NOT_STARTED/IN_PROGRESS/COMPLETE] |

| [TASK_DESCRIPTION] | [CLIENT_NAME] | [XX minutes] | [TIME/DATE] | [NOT_STARTED/IN_PROGRESS/COMPLETE] |

| Task | Client | Estimated Time | Can be delegated to | Status |
