25 Health & Fitness Checklist for Remote Workers

Updated: March 04, 2026

25 checklist items designed to help remote workers maintain a healthy lifestyle, covering exercise routines, nutrition tips, and mental health practices.

What's Inside

  • 25 actionable checklist items
  • Covers exercise, nutrition, and mental health
  • Easy to follow and implement
  • [ ] Item 1: Start your day with 10 minutes of stretching or light yoga to activate your muscles and improve circulation.
  • [ ] Item 2: Drink a full glass of water immediately after waking up to rehydrate your body.
  • [ ] Item 3: Avoid checking work emails or messages during the first 30 minutes of your morning.
  • [ ] Item 4: Take a 5-minute walk outside within the first hour of waking to boost vitamin D and mental clarity.
  • [ ] Item 5: Set your workspace up so your computer screen is at eye level to maintain proper posture.
  • [ ] Item 6: Use a standing desk or elevate your laptop to avoid prolonged sitting during work hours.
  • [ ] Item 7: Schedule a 2-minute micro-break every 30 minutes to stand, stretch, or walk in place.
  • [ ] Item 8: Place a water bottle on your desk and refill it twice during your workday to meet hydration goals.
  • [ ] Item 9: Block out a 20-minute window for a mid-morning walk or bodyweight exercise routine.
  • [ ] Item 10: Practice the 20-20-20 rule: every 20 minutes, look at something 20 feet away for 20 seconds to reduce eye strain.
  • [ ] Item 11: Eat a protein-rich lunch away from your desk to support energy and focus.
  • [ ] Item 12: Take a 10-minute mindfulness break using a guided meditation app to reduce stress.
  • [ ] Item 13: Walk around your home or block during phone calls or virtual meetings when possible.
  • [ ] Item 14: Set a daily step goal (e.g., 7,000–10,000) and track it with a wearable or smartphone.
  • [ ] Item 15: Limit caffeine intake after 2 PM to avoid sleep disruption.
  • [ ] Item 16: Prepare and pack healthy snacks (e.g., nuts, fruit, yogurt) the night before to avoid junk food cravings.
  • [ ] Item 17: Do a 5-minute desk workout (e.g., seated leg lifts, shoulder rolls, or resistance band pulls) mid-afternoon.
  • [ ] Item 18: Close all work-related apps and browser tabs at the end of your scheduled workday.
  • [ ] Item 19: Change out of work clothes to signal the brain that the workday is over.
  • [ ] Item 20: Spend 15 minutes doing a low-intensity activity like walking, stretching, or foam rolling after work.
  • [ ] Item 21: Write down three things you’re grateful for to promote positive mental health.
  • [ ] Item 22: Prepare your workout clothes or gear the night before to increase follow-through.
  • [ ] Item 23: Include at least two servings of vegetables in your dinner to support overall nutrition.
  • [ ] Item 24: Complete a full 30-minute workout (e.g., strength training, cardio, or yoga) at least three times this week.
  • [ ] Item 25: Set a consistent bedtime and follow a 10-minute pre-sleep routine (e.g., reading, deep breathing) to improve sleep quality.
