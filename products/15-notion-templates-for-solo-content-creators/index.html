Template 1: Content Calendar Dashboard

When to use: When planning and scheduling content across multiple platforms (blog, social media, email) for consistent publishing.

```notion

🗓️ [Content Calendar Dashboard]

  • Month: [Month, Year]
  • Content Themes: [Theme 1], [Theme 2], [Theme 3]
  • Top Priority Platforms: [e.g., Instagram, YouTube, Newsletter]

📅 Weekly Overview

| Date | Content Type | Topic | Platform | Status | Notes |

|------|--------------|-------|--------|--------|-------|

| [MM/DD] | [e.g., Reel, Blog] | [Topic Title] | [Platform] | [Draft / Scheduled / Published] | [e.g., Need graphics] |

| [MM/DD] | [e.g., Newsletter] | [Topic Title] | [Platform] | [Draft / Scheduled / Published] | [e.g., Link to draft] |

🔁 Repurpose Pipeline

  • Main Content (Pillar): [e.g., "10 Productivity Tips" blog post]
  • Repurposed Into:
  • [✓] [e.g., Instagram carousel]
  • [ ] [e.g., 1 TikTok script]
  • [ ] [e.g., Newsletter snippet]

📣 Publishing Schedule

  • Blog Posts: [Day(s) of week, e.g., Every Tuesday]
  • Instagram: [e.g., 3x/week: Mon, Wed, Fri]
  • Newsletter: [e.g., Every other Friday]
  • Video Uploads: [e.g., Every Thursday at 6 PM]

> 💡 Tip: Sync this with your calendar app for automated reminders.

```

  • Keywords/Tags: #[Keyword1] #[Keyword2] #[Keyword3]
  • Status: [Idea / In Progress / Published]
  • Related Content: [[Link to related idea or published post]]

🔍 Idea Filters

  • Category: [Education / Entertainment / Behind-the-Scenes / Promo]
  • Difficulty Level: [Beginner / Intermediate / Advanced]
  • Estimated Time to Create: [e.g., 1 hour / 3 hours / Full day]

🗂️ Idea Archive

> Move older ideas here when they're used or outdated.

> 📌 Tip: Review this weekly to fill gaps in your content calendar.

Template 4: Daily Task Board

When to use: When organizing your daily workload to maintain focus and avoid burnout.

```notion

✅ [Daily Task Board]

🗓️ Date: [MM/DD/YYYY]

  • Today's Focus: [e.g., Finish YouTube script + schedule 3 posts]
  • Top 3 Priorities:
  1. [Task 1: e.g., Record podcast episode]
  2. [Task 2: e.g., Respond to client emails]
  3. [Task 3: e.g., Edit Instagram Reel]

📂 Task List

| Task | Category | Time Estimate | Status | Notes |

|------|--------|----------------|--------|-------|

| [e.g., Write blog intro] | Content | 45 min | [✓ Done / In Progress / Blocked] | [e.g., Use outline from Tuesday] |

| [e.g., Upload to YouTube] | Publishing | 15 min | [ ] | [e.g., Add hashtags] |

| [e.g., Review analytics] | Admin | 30 min | [ ] | [e.g., Track last week’s growth] |

🕐 Time Blocks

  • 9:00–10:30 AM: [Deep Work: e.g., Writing]
  • 10:30–11:00 AM: [Break / Admin]
  • 11:00–12:00 PM: [Creative: e.g., Editing]
  • 1:00–2:00 PM: [Client Work / Outreach]
  • 2:00–3:00 PM: [Review & Plan Tomorrow]

> 🔄 Carry unfinished tasks to tomorrow’s board with a 1-sentence reason.

```
