  • Operating Margin: [X]%, compared to [X]% last quarter

Hi [Recipient Name],

This memo outlines the core assumptions used in the [Model Name] prepared for [Use Case, e.g., "Board Presentation on Expansion"]. These inputs are critical for model integrity and should be reviewed quarterly.

Revenue Assumptions:

  • Revenue Growth: [X]% YoY for Year 1, [X]% for Year 2, [X]% for Year 3
  • Pricing: [e.g., "No increase planned"]
  • Volume Growth: Based on [e.g., "historical trends and market research from [Source]"]

Cost Assumptions:

  • COGS: [X]% of revenue (flat)
  • Operating Expenses: [X]% annual increase due to [e.g., "inflation and headcount"]
  • CapEx: $[Amount] in Year 1, $[Amount] in Year 2

Macro Assumptions:

  • Discount Rate: [X]% (WACC based on [Assumption Source])
  • Tax Rate: [X]% effective rate
  • Inflation: [X]% (aligned with [Index, e.g., CPI])

Sensitivity Notes:

  • NPV varies by ±[X]% with ±1% change in discount rate
  • Breakeven achieved in [Month/Year] under base case

Model file: [Link or File Name]

Last updated: [Date] by [Your Name]

Please reach out with questions or suggested adjustments.

Best,

[Your Name]

[Job Title]
