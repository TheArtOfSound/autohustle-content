Template 1: Client Onboarding Checklist

When to use: When a new client is onboarded to ensure a smooth transition and clear communication.

Welcome to [CLIENT COMPANY NAME]! We're excited to have you on board. This checklist outlines the necessary steps to ensure a successful onboarding process.

  1. Introduction and Welcome
  • Send a welcome email to [CLIENT EMAIL] with an introduction to our team and services.
  • Schedule a kick-off call with [CLIENT NAME] to discuss project goals and objectives.
  1. Project Overview
  • Review and discuss the project scope, timeline, and deliverables with [CLIENT NAME].
  • Ensure [CLIENT NAME] understands their roles and responsibilities.
  1. Communication Channels
  • Establish primary communication channels: [PREFERRED COMMUNICATION METHOD, e.g., email, phone, or Slack].
  • Set up regular check-ins: [FREQUENCY, e.g., weekly or bi-weekly].
  1. Payment and Invoicing
  • Discuss payment terms and schedules with [CLIENT NAME].
  • Send a contract outlining the scope, payment, and terms to [CLIENT EMAIL] for signature.
  1. Next Steps
  • Assign tasks and deadlines to team members.
  • Schedule a follow-up meeting to review progress and address any questions or concerns.
