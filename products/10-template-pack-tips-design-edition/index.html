Template 8: Design Critique Session Agenda

When to use: When facilitating a structured team critique to improve design quality and collaboration.

Design Critique: [Project Name]

Date: [Date]

Time: [Start Time] – [End Time]

Facilitator: [Your Name]

Location: [Room/Zoom Link]

Objective

Collect actionable feedback on [specific design element – e.g., checkout flow UX] to improve [goal – e.g., conversion rate].

Attendees

• [Name, Role]

• [Name, Role]

• [Note: Max 6 participants for effectiveness]

Agenda (60 mins)

1. Context (5 min)

Presenter shares:

  • Project goal
  • User problem being solved
  • Stage of design (e.g., wireframe, prototype)

2. Silent Review (10 min)

Team reviews designs independently and writes feedback on sticky notes or [Miro/Mural link].

3. Discussion (35 min)

Guided by facilitator:

• What’s working well?

• What’s confusing or missing?

• Suggestions for improvement

Rules:

  • Critique the work, not the person
  • Be specific and constructive
  • Use “I” statements: “I felt confused when…”

4. Summary & Next Steps (10 min)

Presenter notes key takeaways.

Action items assigned:

• [Task] – Owner: [Name] – Due: [Date]

Materials:

[Link to design file]

[User research summary – optional]
