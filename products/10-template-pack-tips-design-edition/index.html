Template 9: Design Handoff Checklist

When to use: To ensure a smooth handoff from design to development.

[PROJECT NAME] Design Handoff Checklist

Design Files

  • [ ] All design files have been exported in the correct format (e.g., PSD, AI, Sketch, Figma)
  • [ ] Design files are organized and named consistently
  • [ ] All layers and artboards are clearly labeled

Design Specifications

  • [ ] Design specifications have been documented (e.g., colors, typography, spacing)
  • [ ] Design specifications are easily accessible to the development team

Assets

  • [ ] All necessary assets (e.g., images, icons) have been exported and organized
  • [ ] Assets are optimized for web/mobile use

Interactive Elements

  • [ ] All interactive elements (e.g., buttons, links) have been clearly indicated
  • [ ] Interactive elements have been documented in the design specifications

Review and Approval

  • [ ] Designs have been reviewed and approved by the client/stakeholder
  • [ ] Any necessary revisions have been made and approved

Handoff Meeting

  • [ ] A handoff meeting has been scheduled with the development team
  • [ ] All necessary information and materials have been prepared for the handoff meeting

Completed by: [NAME]

Date: [DATE]
