35 High-Converting Email Swipes for Freelancers

Updated: March 09, 2026

35 high-converting email swipes for freelancers — ready to use for outreach, follow-ups, and client onboarding.

What's Inside

  • 35 battle-tested email templates
  • proven to generate replies and bookings
  • easy copy-paste format for immediate use

1. The Warm Intro Opener

Use case: When someone refers you to a potential client

Hi [First Name],

[Referrer’s Name] suggested I reach out — they mentioned you’re working on [specific project or challenge], and that my experience helping [similar type of business] achieve [specific outcome] might be useful.

I’ve helped companies like [Client Name] increase [metric] by [result] in [timeframe] — would you be open to a quick 15-minute chat this week to explore how I could support your goals?

Best,

[Your Name]

2. The Problem-Agitate Solution (PAS) Pitch

Use case: Cold outreach to leads with a clear pain point

Hi [First Name],

I noticed [specific observation about their website, content, or business], and it made me think — are you seeing the results you want from [specific area, e.g., lead generation, branding, etc.]?

Many [their industry] struggle with [specific pain point], which leads to [agitate the consequence — e.g., lost revenue, wasted time, poor engagement].

I specialize in helping [target audience] fix that — like when I helped [Client] increase [metric] by [result] in [timeframe].

Want me to show you how? I’d love to send over a quick 3-step strategy idea tailored to your business — no strings attached.

Best,

[Your Name]

3. The “I Fixed This Before” Case Study Hook

Use case: Follow-up or standalone outreach with social proof

Hi [First Name],

I recently helped [similar company] solve [specific problem] — they were struggling with [brief challenge], and within [timeframe], we achieved [specific result, e.g., 50% more leads].

Given that you’re in [their industry/niche], I’m confident I can deliver similar results for you.

Would you be open to a 15-minute call this week to explore how? Happy to share the full case study if you’re interested.

Best,

[Your Name]
