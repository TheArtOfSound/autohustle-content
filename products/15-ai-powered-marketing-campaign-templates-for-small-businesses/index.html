Template 1: New Product Launch Buzz Builder

When to use: To generate excitement and awareness for an upcoming product or service.

Campaign Goal: Drive pre-orders/sign-ups and build anticipation for [Product Name].

Target Audience: [Ideal Customer Persona for Product]

Key Message: Get ready for [Key Benefit 1] and [Key Benefit 2] with the all-new [Product Name]!

AI-Powered Suggestions:

  • Social Media: Analyze competitor launch campaigns for successful hashtags and engagement tactics. Suggest optimal posting times based on audience activity.
  • Email: Recommend subject line variations with high open-rate potential. Suggest segmenting your list based on past purchase behavior for targeted teasers.
  • Content: Propose blog post topics that address pain points your product solves. Suggest influencer collaboration ideas.

Campaign Steps:

  1. Teaser Social Media Posts (Week 1-2):
  • Platform: [Primary Social Media Platform 1], [Primary Social Media Platform 2]
  • Content: Intriguing visuals/short videos hinting at the product. Use questions to spark curiosity.
  • AI Prompt: "Generate 5 social media post ideas for a mysterious new [Product Category] launch, focusing on [Key Benefit 1]."
  • Example Post: "Something exciting is brewing...🤫 Can you guess what's coming to revolutionize your [Problem Area]? #ComingSoon #[YourBrandName]"
  1. Behind-the-Scenes Content (Week 2-3):
  • Platform: [Social Media Platform 1], [Blog/Website]
  • Content: Short videos or blog posts showing the development process, team excitement, or sneak peeks of features.
  • AI Prompt: "Suggest 3 'behind-the-scenes' content ideas for a product launch, highlighting innovation and team passion."
  1. Reveal Post & Landing Page (Week 3):
  • Platform: [All Social Media Platforms], [Email List], [Website Homepage]
  • Content: Official announcement of [Product Name], its key features, and benefits. Link to a dedicated landing page.
  • AI Prompt: "Draft a compelling social media announcement for the launch of [Product Name], emphasizing [Unique Selling Proposition]."
  • Landing Page Copy:
  • Headline: Introducing [Product Name]: The [Adjective] Solution for [Target Audience's Problem].
  • Subheadline: Experience [Key Benefit 1] and [Key Benefit 2] like never before.
  • Call to Action: [Pre-Order Now / Sign Up for Early Access]
  1. Early Bird Offer/Incentive (Week 3-4):
  • Platform: [Email List], [Social Media], [Landing Page]
  • Content: Announce a limited-time discount or bonus for early adopters.
  • AI Prompt: "Suggest 3 enticing early bird offers for a new [Product Category] launch to drive immediate sales."
  1. Customer Testimonial Teasers (Week 4):
  • Platform: [Social Media], [Email]
  • Content: Share quotes or short video clips from beta testers (if applicable) highlighting positive experiences.
  • AI Prompt: "Generate 5 engaging social media teaser quotes from hypothetical early adopters of a [Product Category] that solves [Problem Area]."
  1. Launch Day Countdown (Last Few Days):
  • Platform: [Social Media Stories], [Email]
  • Content: Daily reminders and countdowns to the official launch date.
  • AI Prompt: "Create a 3-day countdown social media content plan for a product launch, building excitement."
  1. Post-Launch Follow-up (Week 5+):
  • Platform: [Email List], [Social Media]
  • Content: Thank early adopters, share user-generated content, and address FAQs.
  • AI Prompt: "Draft a post-launch email to new customers of [Product Name], encouraging feedback and sharing usage tips."

Template 2: Lead Magnet & Nurture Sequence

When to use: To capture new leads and guide them towards becoming paying customers.

Campaign Goal: Generate qualified leads and move them down the sales funnel for [Your Core Service/Product].

Target Audience: [Ideal Customer Persona for Lead Magnet]

Lead Magnet Offer: [Free Guide/Checklist/Webinar Title]

AI-Powered Suggestions:

  • Content: Analyze top-performing lead magnets in your industry to identify popular topics and formats. Suggest lead magnet titles with high search volume potential.
  • Email: Recommend A/B testing subject lines for nurture emails. Suggest personalization tokens to increase engagement.
  • Social Media: Propose ad copy variations for promoting the lead magnet. Suggest targeting parameters for lead generation ads.

Campaign Steps:

  1. Create Lead Magnet:
  • Content: Develop [Free Guide/Checklist/Webinar] focused on solving [Specific Problem] for [Target Audience].
  • AI Prompt: "Generate 10 compelling titles for a free [Content Format] about [Specific Problem] for [Target Audience]."
  1. Landing Page for Lead Magnet:
  • Content: Clear headline, benefit-driven description, and simple opt-in form.
  • AI Prompt: "Write persuasive landing page copy for a free [Content Format] titled '[Lead Magnet Title]', emphasizing how it solves [Specific Problem]."
  • Example Headline: "Unlock [Desired Outcome] with Our Free [Lead Magnet Title]!"
  1. Social Media Promotion:
  • Platform: [Primary Social Media Platform 1], [Primary Social Media Platform 2]
  • Content: Posts highlighting the value of the lead magnet, using compelling visuals.
  • AI Prompt: "Create 5 social media posts promoting a free [Lead Magnet Title], highlighting its benefits for [Target Audience]."
  1. Paid Ad Campaign (Optional):
  • Platform: [Facebook Ads], [Google Ads]
  • Content: Targeted ads driving traffic to the lead magnet landing page.
  • AI Prompt: "Generate ad copy variations for a Facebook lead generation campaign promoting a free [Lead Magnet Title], targeting [Demographic/Interest]."
  1. Welcome Email (Immediate):
  • Subject Line: [AI-Suggested Subject Line 1]
  • Content: Deliver the lead magnet, thank them for opting in, and briefly introduce your brand.
  • AI Prompt: "Draft a welcome email delivering a free [Lead Magnet Title], thanking the subscriber and briefly introducing [Your Brand Name]."
  1. Nurture Email 1 (1-2 Days Later):
  • Subject Line: [AI-Suggested Subject Line 2]
  • Content: Provide additional value related to the lead magnet topic. Share a relevant blog post or tip.
  • AI Prompt: "Write a nurture email providing a valuable tip related to [Lead Magnet Topic], encouraging engagement with [Your Brand Name]."
  1. Nurture Email 2 (3-4 Days Later):
  • Subject Line: [AI-Suggested Subject Line 3]
  • Content: Introduce a problem that your core service/product solves, subtly hinting at a solution.
  • AI Prompt: "Craft a nurture email that highlights a common challenge faced by [Target Audience] that [Your Core Service/Product] addresses."
  1. Nurture Email 3 (5-7 Days Later):
  • Subject Line: [AI-Suggested Subject Line 4]
  • Content: Introduce your core service/product as the solution to the problem mentioned in the previous email. Include a soft call to action.
  • AI Prompt: "Write a nurture email that positions [Your Core Service/Product] as the solution to [Problem Mentioned in Nurture Email 2], with a call to action to [Learn More/Book a Consultation]."

Template 3: Customer Testimonial Showcase

When to use: To build trust and social proof by highlighting positive customer experiences.

Campaign Goal: Increase credibility and drive conversions by showcasing satisfied customers.

Target Audience: [Potential Customers who are considering your product/service]

Key Message: See how [Your Brand Name] has helped real people like you achieve [Key Outcome].

AI-Powered Suggestions:

  • Social Media: Identify trending testimonial formats (e.g., video clips, quote graphics). Suggest compelling calls to action for sharing their own experiences.
  • Content: Propose blog post ideas that delve deeper into customer success stories. Suggest ways to repurpose testimonials across different platforms.
  • Email: Recommend subject lines that pique curiosity for testimonial-focused emails. Suggest personalizing emails with industry-specific success stories.

Campaign Steps:

  1. Gather Testimonials:
  • Method: Request reviews via email, social media polls, or direct outreach.
  • AI Prompt: "Draft an email requesting customer testimonials for [Your Product/Service], emphasizing the value of their feedback."
  1. Select Best Testimonials:
  • Criteria: Focus on testimonials that highlight specific benefits, outcomes, and address common objections.
  • AI Prompt: "Identify 5 key elements of a powerful customer testimonial that builds trust and encourages conversion."
  1. Create Visual Assets:
  • Content: Design quote graphics, short video clips, or case study snippets.
  • AI Prompt: "Suggest 3 visually appealing formats for showcasing customer testimonials on social media."
  1. Social Media Posts:
  • Platform: [Primary Social Media Platform 1], [Primary Social Media Platform 2], [LinkedIn]
  • Content: Share individual testimonials with engaging visuals and a clear call to action.
  • AI Prompt: "Generate 5 social media captions for customer testimonials, each highlighting a different benefit of [Your Product/Service]."
  • Example Post: "Hear from [Customer Name]! 🎉 '[Quote from Testimonial]' Discover how [Your Brand Name] can help you achieve [Key Outcome] too! Link in bio. #CustomerSuccess #[YourIndustry]"
  1. Dedicated Landing Page/Section:
  • Content: Compile multiple testimonials, potentially organized by service or outcome. Include a prominent call to action.
  • AI Prompt: "Outline the key sections for a 'Customer Stories' page on a business website, designed to build trust."
  1. Email Campaign:
  • Subject Line: [AI-Suggested Subject Line 1]
  • Content: Feature a compelling testimonial in an email to your subscriber list.
  • AI Prompt: "Write an email featuring a customer testimonial that highlights [Specific Benefit], encouraging readers to [Learn More/Book a Call]."
  1. Blog Post/Case Study:
  • Content: Expand on a particularly strong testimonial with a mini-case study or blog post detailing the customer's journey and results.
  • AI Prompt: "Suggest a narrative structure for a customer success story blog post, starting with the problem and ending with the solution and results."
  1. Encourage More Reviews:
  • Content: Include a call to action in post-purchase emails or on your website encouraging customers to share their own experiences.
  • AI Prompt: "Draft a polite and effective call to action for customers to leave a review after a positive experience with [Your Product/Service]."

Template 4: Seasonal Promotion Blitz

When to use: To capitalize on holidays, events, or specific seasons to drive sales.

Campaign Goal: Increase sales and engagement during [Seasonal Event/Holiday].

Target Audience: [Customers interested in seasonal offers/products]

Offer: [Discount Percentage/Bundle Deal/Limited Edition Product] for [Seasonal Event/Holiday].

AI-Powered Suggestions:

  • Social Media: Identify trending seasonal hashtags and visual themes. Suggest ad targeting based on holiday shopping behavior.
  • Email: Recommend subject lines that create urgency for seasonal deals. Suggest segmenting lists based on past seasonal purchase history.
  • Content: Propose blog post or social media content ideas related to the season and your products.

Campaign Steps:

  1. Define Offer & Duration:
  • Offer: [Discount Percentage]% off [Specific Products/Services] or [Bundle Deal Name].
  • Duration: [Start Date] to [End Date].
  • AI Prompt: "Suggest 5 creative promotional offers for [Seasonal Event/Holiday] that align with a [Product/Service Category] business."
  1. Create Promotional Assets:
  • Content: Design banners, social media graphics, email headers, and ad creatives with a seasonal theme.
  • AI Prompt: "Generate visual theme ideas for a [Seasonal Event/Holiday] promotion for a [Your Industry] business."
  1. Announce Offer (Email & Social Media):
  • Platform: [Email List], [Primary Social Media Platform 1], [Primary Social Media Platform 2]
  • Content: Clearly state the offer, duration, and how to redeem it.
  • AI Prompt: "Draft an announcement email for a [Seasonal Event/Holiday] sale offering [Offer Details], creating a sense of excitement."
  • Example Social Post: "Get ready for [Seasonal Event/Holiday]! 🎁 For a limited time, enjoy [Offer Details]! Shop now: [Link] #SeasonalSale #[YourBrandName]"
  1. Social Media Countdown/Reminders:
  • Platform: [Social Media Stories], [Primary Social Media Platform 1]
  • Content: Post daily reminders, highlight specific products included in the sale, and build urgency.
  • AI Prompt: "Create a 5-day social media countdown plan for a [Seasonal Event/Holiday] sale, focusing on urgency and specific product highlights."
  1. Paid Advertising (Optional):
  • Platform: [Facebook Ads], [Instagram Ads]
  • Content: Target users likely to be shopping for [Seasonal Event/Holiday].
  • AI Prompt: "Generate ad copy variations for a [Seasonal Event/Holiday] promotion targeting [Demographic/Interest] on Facebook."
  1. Last Chance Email/Post:
  • Platform: [Email List], [Primary Social Media Platform 1]
  • Content: Final reminder before the offer expires, emphasizing the limited-time nature.
  • AI Prompt: "Write a 'Last Chance' email for a [Seasonal Event/Holiday] sale, creating urgency and encouraging immediate purchase."
  1. Post-Sale Follow-up (Optional):
  • Platform: [Email List]
  • Content: Thank customers for their purchases and offer a small thank-you discount for their next purchase.
  • AI Prompt: "Draft a thank-you email to customers who purchased during the [Seasonal Event/Holiday] sale, offering a small incentive for their next purchase."
  1. Analyze Performance:
  • Metrics: Track sales, website traffic, conversion rates, and engagement during the campaign.
  • AI Prompt: "Identify key metrics to track for a seasonal promotion to assess its success and inform future campaigns."
