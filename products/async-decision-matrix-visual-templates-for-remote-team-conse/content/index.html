Decision Framework Templates

Template 1: RACI Decision Matrix

Purpose: Clarify roles and responsibilities for decision-making processes

When to use: Before starting any major decision process to establish clear accountability

```

RACI Decision Matrix: [DECISION TITLE]

Decision Statement: [Clearly define the decision that needs to be made]

Timeline: [Start Date] to [End Date]

Team Roles

| Team Member | Role | RACI Code | Responsibilities |

|-------------|------|-----------|------------------|

| [Name] | [Title] | R/A/C/I | [Specific responsibilities] |

| [Name] | [Title] | R/A/C/I | [Specific responsibilities] |

| [Name] | [Title] | R/A/C/I | [Specific responsibilities] |

Legend:

  • R = Responsible (does the work)
  • A = Accountable (signs off)
  • C = Consulted (provides input)
  • I = Informed (kept updated)

Decision Criteria

1. [Criterion 1 - e.g., Budget impact]

2. [Criterion 2 - e.g., Technical feasibility]

3. [Criterion 3 - e.g., Timeline constraints]

Communication Plan

  • Updates: [Frequency and method]
  • Input deadline: [Date and time with timezone]
  • Final decision: [Date and method of announcement]

```

Instructions: Fill in team member names and assign appropriate RACI codes. Ensure only one person is "Accountable" for the final decision. Share this matrix before beginning the decision process to set clear expectations.

Consensus Building Workflows

Template 4: Async Consensus Timeline

Purpose: Structure a time-bound consensus-building process across multiple time zones

When to use: When you need input from globally distributed team members

```

Async Consensus Timeline: [DECISION TITLE]

Decision Deadline: [Final date and time with timezone]

Facilitator: [Name and contact information]

Phase 1: Information Sharing

Duration: [Start date] to [End date]

Objective: Ensure all team members have the same baseline information

Required Actions:

  • [ ] Facilitator: Post decision context and background materials
  • [ ] All Team Members: Review materials and confirm understanding
  • [ ] Subject Matter Experts: Provide additional context as needed

Materials Location: [Link to shared folder/document]

Questions/Clarifications Deadline: [Date and time]

Phase 2: Option Generation

Duration: [Start date] to [End date]

Objective: Collect diverse perspectives and potential solutions

Required Actions:

  • [ ] All Team Members: Submit at least [number] potential options
  • [ ] Facilitator: Compile and organize submitted options
  • [ ] Team: Review compiled options and suggest combinations/improvements

Submission Method: [How and where to submit ideas]

Criteria for Options:

1. [Criterion 1 - e.g., Must be implementable within 3 months]

2. [Criterion 2 - e.g., Budget impact under $50k]

3. [Criterion 3 - e.g., Aligns with company values]

Phase 3: Evaluation & Feedback

Duration: [Start date] to [End date]

Objective: Systematically evaluate options and gather structured feedback

Required Actions:

  • [ ] All Team Members: Score options using provided criteria
  • [ ] Team Members: Provide written feedback on top 3 options
  • [ ] Facilitator: Compile scores and feedback

Evaluation Framework: [Link to scoring template]

Feedback Format:

  • Option: [Option name]
  • Score: [1-10 rating]
  • Strengths: [What works well]
  • Concerns: [Potential issues]
  • Suggestions: [How to improve]

Phase 4: Consensus Building

Duration: [Start date] to [End date]

Objective: Address concerns and build agreement around preferred option

Consensus Levels:

  • Level 5: Fully support - "I love this idea"
  • Level 4: Support with minor concerns - "I can live with this"
  • Level 3: Neutral - "I have no strong feelings"
  • Level 2: Disagree but won't block - "I don't like it but won't stand in the way"
  • Level 1: Strong disagreement - "I cannot support this decision"

Required Actions:

  • [ ] All Team Members: Indicate consensus level for top option
  • [ ] Level 1-2 Members: Explain concerns and suggest modifications
  • [ ] Facilitator: Work with dissenting members to address concerns
  • [ ] Team: Revise option based on feedback if needed

Target Consensus: All members at Level 3 or higher

Phase 5: Decision Finalization

Duration: [Start date] to [End date]

Objective: Confirm final decision and implementation plan

Required Actions:

  • [ ] Decision Owner: Announce final decision with rationale
  • [ ] All Team Members: Acknowledge decision and commit to support
  • [ ] Implementation Team: Create detailed action plan
  • [ ] Facilitator: Document decision process and lessons learned

Decision Documentation: [Where final decision will be recorded]

```

Instructions: Adjust phase durations based on decision complexity and team size. Ensure adequate time for each time zone to participate. Send reminder notifications 24 hours before each phase deadline.
