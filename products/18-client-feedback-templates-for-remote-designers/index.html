Template 1: Project Discovery Kickoff Questionnaire

When to use: At the start of a new project to align on goals, audience, scope, and expectations

Thank you for taking the time to complete this project discovery form. Your responses will help me understand your vision, target users, and success criteria so I can deliver designs that meet your business goals.

Please answer the following questions as thoroughly as possible. If you’re unsure about any item, share your best thinking — we can refine it together.

  1. Project Overview
  • What is the name or working title of the project?

[PROJECT NAME]

  • What problem are we trying to solve with this project?

[PROBLEM STATEMENT]

  • How will success be measured? (e.g., increased conversions, improved user engagement)

[SUCCESS METRICS]

  1. Target Audience
  • Who is the primary user of this product or feature?

[PRIMARY USER DESCRIPTION]

  • Do you have user personas, research, or analytics we can reference?

[LINK TO RESEARCH OR “NOT AVAILABLE”]

  1. Scope & Deliverables
  • What specific deliverables do you expect from this project? (e.g., wireframes, high-fidelity mockups, prototype)

[EXPECTED DELIVERABLES]

  • Are there any out-of-scope items we should be aware of?

[OUT-OF-SCOPE ITEMS OR “NONE”]

  1. Stakeholders & Feedback Process
  • Who are the key decision-makers for design approval?

[STAKEHOLDER NAMES AND ROLES]

  • What is your preferred method for providing feedback? (e.g., email, Figma comments, video call)

[FEEDBACK METHOD]

  1. Timeline & Milestones
  • What is your ideal launch or review date?

[TARGET DATE]

  • Are there any critical deadlines or milestones we should plan around?

[KEY DATES OR “NONE”]

Please return this completed form by [DUE DATE] so we can proceed with the project kickoff meeting.

  • Vibe: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
  • Example reference: [LINK OR ATTACHMENT]

  • Vibe: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION]
  • Example reference: [LINK OR ATTACHMENT]

Template 8: Final Design Approval Request

When to use: Before handing off designs to development, to obtain formal sign-off

Hi [CLIENT NAME],

The final designs for [PROJECT NAME] are complete and ready for your review. These include all approved revisions and are prepared for handoff to developers.

📎 Final design file: [LINK TO FIGMA OR PDF]

📋 Included screens: [LIST OF SCREENS OR PAGES]

✅ Status: All feedback from previous rounds has been addressed

Please review by [REVIEW DEADLINE] and confirm approval by replying with:

✅ APPROVED – Ready for handoff

or

🔶 Revisions needed – See comments below

If revisions are needed, please:

  • Leave specific, actionable comments in the file
  • Limit changes to critical issues (e.g., functionality, brand errors)
  • Avoid new feature requests or scope changes at this stage

Once approved, I’ll prepare the design specifications, assets, and developer handoff package.

Thank you for your partnership — I’m excited to see this go live!
