Template 1: Video Script Feedback Response

When to use: When receiving written feedback on a video script draft and needing to acknowledge changes while maintaining creative boundaries

[Hi [Client Name],]

Thank you for reviewing the script draft for [Video Title/Project Name]. I appreciate your thoughtful feedback and the time you took to provide detailed notes.

Below is my response to each of your comments:

  • [Client Feedback Point 1]: [Your response – e.g., “I’ve revised the opening to better reflect your brand voice. See line 4.”]
  • [Client Feedback Point 2]: [Your response – e.g., “I understand the concern about pacing. I’ve tightened the middle section to reduce runtime by 15 seconds.”]
  • [Client Feedback Point 3]: [Your response – e.g., “I recommend keeping this line for emotional impact, but I’ve added an alternative version in brackets for your consideration.”]

The updated script now includes all agreed-upon changes and is attached/[linked here]. Let me know if you'd like a quick 5-minute voice memo walking through the revisions.

Next step: Approve this version by [Date] so we can move into storyboarding/filming on schedule.

Best,

[Your Name]
