Template 1: Welcome Email Template

When to use: When a new client signs up for your freelance writing services to introduce yourself and set the tone for the project.

A warm welcome to [YOUR COMPANY NAME]! We're thrilled to have you on board and excited to start working on your writing project. As discussed, we'll be delivering high-quality content tailored to your needs.

To ensure a smooth process, please find below some key information about our collaboration:

  • Project Start Date: [INSERT PROJECT START DATE]
  • Project Deadline: [INSERT PROJECT DEADLINE]
  • Primary Point of Contact: [YOUR NAME] at [YOUR EMAIL ADDRESS]

If you have any questions or need assistance, don't hesitate to reach out. We're looking forward to creating compelling content for you.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 5: Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

When to use: When you need to protect confidential information shared by the client or when working on sensitive projects.

NON-DISCLOSURE AGREEMENT

This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is made on [AGREEMENT DATE] by and between [YOUR COMPANY NAME] ("Recipient") and [CLIENT NAME] ("Discloser").

  1. Confidential Information: The Discloser may disclose certain confidential and proprietary information ("Confidential Information") to the Recipient for the purpose of [PROJECT/PURPOSE].
  2. Obligations: The Recipient agrees to hold the Confidential Information in confidence and not to disclose it to any third party without the prior written consent of the Discloser.
  3. Exclusions: The obligations of confidentiality shall not apply to any information which is:
  • Already in the public domain;
  • Already known to the Recipient;
  • Obtained from a third party who is not under any obligation of confidentiality;
  • Required to be disclosed by law.
  1. Term: This Agreement shall commence on the date first above written and continue for a period of [LENGTH OF TIME].

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

Recipient Signature: ______________________ Date: ______________________

Discloser Signature: ______________________ Date: ______________________

Template 7: Client Project Brief Questionnaire

When to use: After the welcome email, use this to gather detailed information about the project, tone, audience, and expectations.

Subject: Quick Questions to Kick Off [Project Name]

Hi [Client Name],

To ensure your [content/article/blog/social media campaign] hits the mark, I’d love to get a few details from you. Please reply with your answers to the questions below:

  1. What is the primary goal of this project? (e.g., generate leads, educate readers, boost engagement)
  2. Who is your target audience? (e.g., small business owners, fitness enthusiasts, B2B tech buyers)
  3. What tone or style should the content reflect? (e.g., professional, conversational, witty, authoritative)
  4. Do you have any brand guidelines, style guides, or examples of content you like?
  5. Are there specific keywords or topics that must be included?
  6. What format and length are you expecting? (e.g., 1,200-word blog post, 3 social media captions)
  7. Are there any points or messaging that should be avoided?
  8. Who will be reviewing/approving the content?

Let me know if you’d prefer to hop on a quick call instead!

Thanks,

[Your Name]
