40-Point Freelancer's Tax Checklist

Updated: February 26, 2026

40-point checklist to help freelancers stay on top of their tax obligations throughout the year.

What's Inside

  • 40 essential tax tasks
  • Organized by quarter

  • [ ] Item 31: Set up a system for tracking and paying business property taxes, if applicable

  • [ ] Item 33: Keep detailed records of business vehicle expenses, including fuel, maintenance, and insurance
  • [ ] Item 34: Understand the tax implications of business expansion or relocation
