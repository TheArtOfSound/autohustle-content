Template 1: Freelance Marketing Services Agreement

When to use: When onboarding new clients to outline the scope of work and terms of the freelance marketing services.

This Freelance Marketing Services Agreement ("Agreement") is made on [DATE] by and between [FREELANCER NAME] ("Freelancer") and [CLIENT NAME] ("Client") for the provision of marketing services.

  1. Scope of Work: The Freelancer agrees to provide [LIST OF SERVICES] to the Client.
  2. Term and Termination: The term of this Agreement shall commence on [START DATE] and continue until [END DATE], unless terminated earlier by either party with [NUMBER] days' written notice.
  3. Compensation: The Client shall pay the Freelancer a total of [TOTAL COMPENSATION] for the services provided, payable in [NUMBER] installments of [INSTALLMENT AMOUNT] each.

Template 2: Client Onboarding Questionnaire

When to use: To gather essential information about the client's business and marketing goals during the onboarding process.

Client Onboarding Questionnaire for [CLIENT NAME]

  1. Business Overview: Please provide a brief description of your business and its primary objectives.
  2. Marketing Goals: What are your top marketing goals for the next [TIMEFRAME]?
  3. Target Audience: Who is your ideal customer?
  4. Competitors: Who are your main competitors, and how do you differentiate your business from theirs?
  5. Current Marketing Efforts: What marketing strategies are you currently using, and what results have you seen so far?
