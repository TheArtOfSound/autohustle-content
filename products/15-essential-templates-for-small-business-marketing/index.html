Template 5: Product Launch Timeline (4-Week Plan)

When to use: To organize and execute a successful product or service launch

Product Name: [PRODUCT NAME]

Launch Date: [DATE]

Goal: [NUMBER] sales or [REVENUE TARGET] in first week

Week -4: Pre-Launch Prep

  • Finalize product/service deliverables
  • Create all marketing assets (graphics, emails, sales page)
  • Add [NUMBER] beta testers or launch team members

Week -3: Tease & Build Anticipation

  • Send teaser email: “Something big is coming…”
  • Post behind-the-scenes content on social media
  • Host a poll: “What’s your biggest struggle with [TOPIC]?”

Week -2: Early Access & Value

  • Share a free training or mini-challenge
  • Open early-bird pricing for subscribers
  • Post customer story or use case

Week -1: Countdown & Urgency

  • Email sequence: 3-day countdown
  • Post launch video teaser
  • Share FAQs and testimonials

Launch Week (Week 0)

  • Go live on [LAUNCH DATE]
  • Send launch announcement email
  • Post across all platforms with CTA
  • Monitor feedback and engage comments

Post-Launch (Week +1):

  • Thank customers with follow-up email
  • Request reviews or testimonials
  • Analyze results and document lessons
