Template 3: Async Sprint Planning Framework

Purpose: Conduct effective sprint planning sessions across time zones using asynchronous collaboration.

Instructions: Adapt the timeline based on your sprint length and team distribution. Share the template 48 hours before sprint planning begins.

```markdown

Async Sprint Planning Framework

Sprint [NUMBER] - [START DATE] to [END DATE]

Pre-Planning Phase (Days 1-2)

#### Day 1: Backlog Review

Responsible: [PRODUCT OWNER/MANAGER]

Deliverable: Updated and prioritized backlog

Tasks:

  • [ ] Review previous sprint outcomes
  • [ ] Update story priorities based on [CRITERIA]
  • [ ] Ensure stories have clear acceptance criteria
  • [ ] Post backlog in [LOCATION] for team review

#### Day 2: Individual Story Review

Responsible: All team members

Deliverable: Individual story estimates and questions

Tasks for Each Team Member:

  • [ ] Review top [NUMBER] stories in backlog
  • [ ] Add effort estimates using [ESTIMATION METHOD]
  • [ ] Flag stories needing clarification in [LOCATION]
  • [ ] Submit capacity for upcoming sprint: [X] hours/points

Planning Phase (Days 3-4)

#### Story Estimation Consolidation

Responsible: [SCRUM MASTER/TEAM LEAD]

Process:

1. Collect all individual estimates

2. Identify stories with high variance in estimates

3. Create async discussion threads for disputed estimates

4. Set 24-hour window for estimate convergence

#### Sprint Goal Definition

Collaborative Input Required From: [LIST TEAM MEMBERS]

Sprint Goal Draft: [PROPOSED GOAL]

Supporting Stories:

  • [STORY 1] - [ESTIMATE]
  • [STORY 2] - [ESTIMATE]
  • [STORY 3] - [ESTIMATE]

Team Feedback Required:

  • Does this goal align with [PROJECT OBJECTIVES]?
  • Are the supporting stories realistic for our capacity?
  • Any dependencies or risks not addressed?

Finalization Phase (Day 5)

#### Final Sprint Commitment

Team Capacity: [TOTAL POINTS/HOURS]

Committed Stories: [LIST WITH ESTIMATES]

Sprint Goal: [FINALIZED GOAL]

#### Success Metrics

  • [METRIC 1]: [TARGET]
  • [METRIC 2]: [TARGET]
  • [METRIC 3]: [TARGET]

#### Daily Standup Schedule

Format: Async updates in [LOCATION]

Posting Time: By [TIME] in [TIMEZONE]

Sync Meetings: [FREQUENCY] at [TIME] in [TIMEZONE]

```
