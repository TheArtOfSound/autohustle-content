Template 1: Freelance Writing Services Contract

When to use: For hiring a freelance writer to create blog posts, articles, website content, or other written materials.

[CLIENT NAME] ("Client") and [FREELANCER NAME] ("Freelancer") agree to the following terms for freelance writing services:

  1. Scope of Work: Freelancer agrees to write [NUMBER] pieces of content, including [DESCRIPTION OF CONTENT, e.g., blog posts, product descriptions], as outlined in [ATTACHMENT A or PROJECT BRIEF]. Topics, word count, tone, and deadlines will be specified per assignment.
  1. Compensation: Client agrees to pay Freelancer [AMOUNT] per [WORD COUNT or PIECE], or a flat fee of [TOTAL FEE] for the project. Payment will be made via [PAYMENT METHOD] within [NUMBER] days of invoice receipt.
  1. Revisions: Freelancer will provide up to [NUMBER] rounds of revisions per piece. Additional revisions will be billed at [HOURLY RATE or FEE].
  1. Deadlines: First draft(s) due by [DATE]. Final delivery due by [DATE]. Extensions require mutual written agreement.
  1. Ownership & Rights: Upon full payment, Client receives full copyright and publishing rights to the final content. Freelancer retains the right to display the work in their portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.
  1. Confidentiality: Freelancer agrees not to disclose any proprietary or confidential information received from Client.
  1. Termination: Either party may terminate this agreement with [NUMBER] days’ written notice. Client will pay for all completed work up to the termination date.
  1. Governing Law: This agreement is governed by the laws of [STATE/PROVINCE/COUNTRY].

Signatures:

___________________________

[FREELANCER NAME]

Date: [DATE]

___________________________

[CLIENT NAME]

Date: [DATE]

Template 2: Graphic Design Services Contract

  1. Confidentiality: Designer agrees to keep all Client materials and project details confidential.

Template 3: Website Development Contract

  1. Project Scope: Developer will design and develop a website for [BUSINESS NAME], including [PAGES TO BE BUILT, e.g., Home, About, Services, Contact], [FUNCTIONALITY, e.g., contact form, mobile responsiveness], and [PLATFORM, e.g., WordPress, custom HTML].

  1. Compensation: Total fee: [AMOUNT]. Payment schedule: [AMOUNT] due at signing, [AMOUNT] at design approval, [AMOUNT] at launch.
  1. Revisions: [NUMBER] rounds of design and functionality revisions included. Additional changes billed at [HOURLY RATE].

  1. Client Responsibilities: Client will provide content (text, images), feedback, and approvals in a timely manner.

Template 8: Virtual Assistant Services Contract

When to use: For hiring a freelance virtual assistant to handle administrative, scheduling, or customer support tasks.

  1. Communication: Assistant will report progress [FREQUENCY, e.g., weekly via email] and be available during [AVAILABILITY HOURS].
  1. Compensation: Assistant will be paid [AMOUNT] per [HOUR/WEEK/MONTH], payable via [PAYMENT METHOD] on [PAY DATE, e.g., the 1st of each month].

Template 10: Freelance Writing Contract

When to use: When hiring a freelance writer for content creation services

This Freelance Writing Contract ("Agreement") is made and entered into on [DATE] ("Effective Date") by and between [CLIENT COMPANY NAME] ("Client") with a principal place of business at [CLIENT ADDRESS] and [FREELANCE WRITER NAME] ("Freelancer") with a principal place of business at [FREELANCER ADDRESS].

WHEREAS, Client desires to retain Freelancer to create written content ("Content") as outlined in the scope of work below; and

WHEREAS, Freelancer agrees to create the Content according to the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants and promises contained herein, the parties agree as follows:

  1. Scope of Work: Freelancer shall create [NUMBER] of [TYPE] of content pieces, including but not limited to [LIST SPECIFIC TYPES OF CONTENT], as outlined in the project schedule below.
  2. Payment: Client shall pay Freelancer a total of $[AMOUNT] for the Content created, payable in [NUMBER] installments of $[AMOUNT] each, due on [DUE DATES].
  3. Ownership: Client retains all rights to the Content created by Freelancer, and Freelancer assigns all rights, title, and interest in the Content to Client.
  4. Confidentiality: Freelancer shall maintain the confidentiality of all Client information and shall not disclose any confidential information to any third party.
