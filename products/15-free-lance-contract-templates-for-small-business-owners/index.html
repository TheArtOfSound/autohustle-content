Template 1: Basic Freelance Agreement

When to use: For general freelance work, such as writing, designing, or consulting services.

This Basic Freelance Agreement ("Agreement") is made on [DATE] by and between [CLIENT NAME] ("Client") with a principal place of business at [CLIENT ADDRESS] and [FREELANCER NAME] ("Freelancer") with a principal place of business at [FREELANCER ADDRESS].

  1. Services: The Freelancer agrees to provide [DESCRIPTION OF SERVICES] to the Client.
  2. Payment: The Client shall pay the Freelancer a total of [TOTAL PAYMENT] for the Services, according to the following schedule: [PAYMENT SCHEDULE].
  3. Term and Termination: This Agreement shall commence on [START DATE] and continue until [END DATE], unless terminated earlier by either party with [NUMBER] days' written notice.
  4. Intellectual Property: The Freelancer retains ownership of all intellectual property rights in the work created under this Agreement, except as otherwise agreed in writing by the parties.
  5. Confidentiality: The Freelancer shall keep confidential all information and materials provided by the Client, and shall not disclose such information to any third party without the Client's prior written consent.

By signing below, the parties acknowledge that they have read, understand, and agree to be bound by the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

Freelancer Signature: _____________________________

Template 6: Freelance Web Development Contract

When to use: When hiring a freelancer to build or modify a website for your small business, this contract outlines project scope, deliverables, payment terms, and intellectual property rights.

This Freelance Web Development Contract ("Agreement") is made effective as of [DATE], by and between:

[CLIENT NAME], with a principal place of business at [CLIENT ADDRESS] ("Client"),

and

[FREELANCER NAME], with a principal place of business at [FREELANCER ADDRESS] ("Developer").

1. Services

Developer agrees to design, develop, and deliver a website according to the specifications outlined in Exhibit A – Project Scope and Deliverables, attached and incorporated into this Agreement.

2. Project Timeline

The project will begin on [START DATE] and milestones are as follows:

  • Initial design mockups delivered by: [DATE]
  • Development phase completed by: [DATE]
  • Final website launch by: [DATE]

Any delays caused by Client feedback or content delivery may adjust the timeline accordingly.

3. Compensation

Client agrees to pay Developer a total fee of [TOTAL AMOUNT] for the services rendered, structured as follows:

  • [AMOUNT] due upon signing (non-refundable deposit)
  • [AMOUNT] due upon approval of design mockups
  • [AMOUNT] due upon final website delivery and launch

Payments shall be made via [PAYMENT METHOD] within [NUMBER] days of invoice date.

4. Revisions & Feedback

Developer will provide up to [NUMBER] rounds of revisions at no additional cost. Additional revisions will be billed at [HOURLY RATE] per hour.

5. Intellectual Property

Upon full payment, Developer assigns all rights, title, and interest in the final website design, code, and custom assets to Client. Developer retains the right to display the work in their portfolio unless otherwise agreed in writing.

6. Client Responsibilities

Client agrees to provide:

  • All required content (text, images, logos) by [DATE]
  • Timely feedback within [NUMBER] business days of submission
  • Access to necessary accounts (domain, hosting, etc.) as needed

7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree not to disclose any proprietary or sensitive information received during the project without prior written consent.

8. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement with [NUMBER] days’ written notice. In the event of termination, Client will pay Developer for all work completed up to the termination date.

9. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [STATE].

10. Entire Agreement

This document, including Exhibit A, constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions.

Signed:

___________________________

[CLIENT NAME]

Date: [DATE]

___________________________

[FREELANCER NAME]

Date: [DATE]

Exhibit A – Project Scope and Deliverables

  • Website type: [e.g., 5-page responsive site, e-commerce, blog]
  • Key features: [e.g., contact form, SEO optimization, mobile compatibility]
  • Content management system: [e.g., WordPress, Shopify]
  • Final deliverables: [e.g., fully functional website, source files, training session]
