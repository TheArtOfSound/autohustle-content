FutureProof: AI-Driven Scenario Planning Prompts for Small Business Owners

==============================================

As a small business owner, staying ahead of the curve can be daunting. Market trends are constantly shifting, competition is fierce, and uncertainty looms around every corner. That's where scenario planning comes in – a powerful tool to anticipate, prepare for, and adapt to an uncertain future.

This prompt pack, FutureProof, provides you with 30 AI-driven scenario planning prompts tailored specifically for small business owners like you. Each prompt is designed to help you navigate the complex landscape of your industry and emerge stronger, more resilient, and better positioned for success.

Market Disruption & Competitive Intelligence

Scenario Planning Prompts

#### 1. Market Shift Analysis

What significant market shifts do you foresee in the next 2-3 years that will impact your business?

#### 2. Competitor Landscape

Who are the key players in your industry, and how can we analyze their strengths and weaknesses to gain a competitive edge?

#### 3. Emerging Trends Research

Which emerging trends or technologies are likely to disrupt our market, and how can we prepare for them?

#### 4. Customer Feedback Analysis

What customer feedback do we receive on our products/services, and how can we use this information to inform future product development?

#### 5. Industry Benchmarking

How does our business performance compare to industry benchmarks, and what areas require improvement?
