Pre-Launch Planning & Idea Validation

Template 1: Rapid Idea Validation Framework

Purpose: Quickly assess if your product idea has market potential before investing time in development.

Instructions: Fill in each section honestly. If you can't complete a section easily, it may indicate a weak point in your idea that needs addressing.

#### Product Concept Overview

  • Product Name: [YOUR PRODUCT NAME]
  • One-Line Description: [DESCRIBE YOUR PRODUCT IN 10 WORDS OR LESS]
  • Target Problem: [WHAT SPECIFIC PROBLEM DOES THIS SOLVE?]
  • Solution Summary: [HOW DOES YOUR PRODUCT SOLVE THIS PROBLEM?]

#### Market Validation Checklist

  • Target Audience: [WHO SPECIFICALLY NEEDS THIS SOLUTION?]
  • Audience Size: [HOW MANY PEOPLE HAVE THIS PROBLEM? (ROUGH ESTIMATE)]
  • Pain Level: [RATE THE PROBLEM SEVERITY: 1-10, WHERE 10 = EXTREMELY PAINFUL]
  • Current Solutions: [WHAT ARE PEOPLE USING NOW TO SOLVE THIS?]
  • Solution Gaps: [WHAT'S MISSING FROM EXISTING SOLUTIONS?]

#### Validation Evidence

  • Personal Experience: [DO YOU PERSONALLY HAVE THIS PROBLEM? Y/N]
  • Community Mentions: [WHERE HAVE YOU SEEN PEOPLE DISCUSSING THIS PROBLEM?]
  • Search Volume: [KEYWORD SEARCH VOLUME FOR RELATED TERMS]
  • Competitor Analysis: [LIST 3 DIRECT OR INDIRECT COMPETITORS]

#### Quick Feasibility Check

  • Technical Complexity: [RATE 1-10, WHERE 1 = VERY SIMPLE TO BUILD]
  • Time to MVP: [HOW LONG TO BUILD A BASIC VERSION?]
  • Required Skills: [WHAT SKILLS DO YOU NEED? WHICH DO YOU HAVE?]
  • Initial Investment: [ESTIMATED COST TO GET STARTED]

Template 2: 48-Hour Launch Strategy Canvas

Purpose: Plan your entire micro-launch strategy before execution begins.

Instructions: Complete this template 24-48 hours before your launch. Keep strategies simple and executable within your timeframe.

#### Launch Objectives

  • Primary Goal: [WHAT'S YOUR MAIN OBJECTIVE? (e.g., 50 signups, $500 revenue, 100 feedback responses)]
  • Secondary Goals: [LIST 2-3 ADDITIONAL MEASURABLE OUTCOMES]
  • Success Metrics: [HOW WILL YOU MEASURE SUCCESS?]
  • Minimum Viable Success: [WHAT'S THE LOWEST RESULT YOU'D CONSIDER WORTHWHILE?]

#### Target Audience Definition

  • Primary Persona: [DESCRIBE YOUR IDEAL CUSTOMER IN 2-3 SENTENCES]
  • Demographics: [AGE, LOCATION, INCOME, JOB TITLE, ETC.]
  • Psychographics: [VALUES, INTERESTS, PAIN POINTS, BEHAVIORS]
  • Where They Hang Out: [LIST 5-7 SPECIFIC PLACES YOUR AUDIENCE GATHERS ONLINE]

#### Product Positioning

  • Value Proposition: [COMPLETE: "Unlike [ALTERNATIVES], our [PRODUCT] helps [TARGET AUDIENCE] [ACHIEVE DESIRED OUTCOME] by [UNIQUE APPROACH]"]
  • Key Benefits: [LIST TOP 3 BENEFITS CUSTOMERS WILL EXPERIENCE]
  • Proof Points: [WHAT EVIDENCE SUPPORTS YOUR CLAIMS?]
  • Pricing Strategy: [FREE/PAID? IF PAID, WHAT PRICE AND WHY?]

#### Marketing Channel Strategy

  • Channel 1: [PRIMARY MARKETING CHANNEL] - [SPECIFIC TACTIC]
  • Channel 2: [SECONDARY MARKETING CHANNEL] - [SPECIFIC TACTIC]
  • Channel 3: [TERTIARY MARKETING CHANNEL] - [SPECIFIC TACTIC]
  • Content Assets Needed: [LIST ALL CONTENT YOU'LL NEED TO CREATE]
  • Timeline: [WHEN WILL YOU EXECUTE EACH CHANNEL?]
