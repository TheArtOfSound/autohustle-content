When to use: To greet and onboard new email subscribers.

Welcome to the [Your Business Name] family! We're so excited to have you join our community.

At [Your Business Name], we're passionate about [briefly describe your business mission or what you offer]. We believe in [core value or benefit].

As a new subscriber, you'll be the first to know about:

  • [Benefit 1 - e.g., Exclusive offers and discounts]
  • [Benefit 2 - e.g., New product launches]
  • [Benefit 3 - e.g., Helpful tips and industry insights]

To get started, we recommend:

  • Checking out our [Link to popular blog post or resource]
  • Exploring our [Link to product/service page]
  • Following us on social media: [Link to Facebook] | [Link to Instagram] | [Link to LinkedIn]

In the meantime, here's a special welcome gift just for you: [Discount Code or Offer]! Use it at checkout on your next purchase.

Thanks again for joining us! We can't wait to connect with you.

Sincerely,

The Team at [Your Business Name]
