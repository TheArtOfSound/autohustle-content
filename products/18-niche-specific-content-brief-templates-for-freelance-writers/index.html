[Describe the primary goal of this post — e.g., educate readers about gut health, promote a new wellness program, increase email sign-ups]

[Describe your ideal reader — e.g., women aged 30–45 managing stress and hormonal imbalances, new moms seeking postpartum fitness routines]

[Choose one or describe: conversational, clinical, empowering, nurturing, science-backed, motivational]

[Insert proposed title or topic idea — e.g., “5 Natural Ways to Boost Energy Without Caffeine”]

[Specify range — e.g., 800–1,000 words]

  • Primary keyword: [e.g., “natural energy boosters”]
  • Secondary keywords: [e.g., “adrenal fatigue remedies,” “holistic energy tips”]

  1. Engaging introduction with relatable pain point
  2. Brief explanation of the core issue
  3. Numbered or bulleted list of solutions/tips
  4. Actionable takeaway or invitation to learn more
  5. Optional: Call-to-action for [e.g., free guide, consultation, newsletter]

CTA (Call to Action):

[Specify desired reader action — e.g., “Download my free Energy Reset Checklist,” “Book a discovery call”]
