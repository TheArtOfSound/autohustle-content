45 Daily Productivity Checkpoints for Remote Developers

Updated: March 05, 2026

45 daily productivity checkpoints designed specifically for remote developers to enhance focus, track progress, and maintain work-life balance.

  • 45 actionable daily checkpoints
  • structured by time of day
  • optimized for deep work and task completion

  • [ ] Item 3: Review and update your project management tools (e.g., Jira, Trello) to reflect current priorities
  • [ ] Item 4: Set up your workspace, ensuring it's clean, organized, and conducive to focus
  • [ ] Item 5: Prepare a healthy breakfast and stay hydrated to fuel your body and mind
  • [ ] Item 6: Establish a morning exercise or meditation routine to boost energy and focus
