Template 1: Introduction to Project Feedback

When to use: At the beginning of a project to set expectations for the feedback process

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

I'm excited to start working on your [PROJECT TYPE] project. To ensure we're on the same page throughout the process, I wanted to take a moment to discuss how we'll handle feedback. Your input is invaluable in helping me create a design that meets your needs and exceeds your expectations.

Throughout the project, I'll be sending you regular updates and requesting your feedback at key milestones. Your prompt and detailed feedback will help me refine the design and deliver the best possible outcome.

If you have any questions or concerns about the feedback process, please don't hesitate to reach out. I'm always here to help.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 2: Project Kickoff Questionnaire

When to use: At the start of a project to gather essential information from the client

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

Thank you for choosing me to work on your [PROJECT TYPE] project. To get started, I have a few questions to help me better understand your vision and goals.

Please take a moment to answer the following questions:

  • What are your primary objectives for this project?
  • Who is your target audience?
  • What sets your [BUSINESS/PRODUCT] apart from the competition?
  • Are there any specific design preferences (e.g., color scheme, typography, imagery) you'd like me to incorporate?
  • Are there any existing brand guidelines (e.g., logos, style guides) I should be aware of?

Your input will help me create a design that accurately represents your brand and meets your needs.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]
