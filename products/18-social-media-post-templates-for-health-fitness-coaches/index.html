Template 5: Quick Tip Tuesday

When to use: To deliver value in under 60 seconds

🔥 Quick Tip Tuesday: Stop skipping warm-ups.

Your 5-minute warm-up isn’t “wasted time” — it’s what PREVENTS injury and boosts performance.

Try this quick sequence before ANY workout:

  1. Arm circles – 30 sec
  2. Bodyweight squats – 10 reps
  3. Leg swings – 10 per leg
  4. Cat-Cow – 30 sec
  5. Jumping jacks – 1 min

That’s it. You’ll move better, lift safer, and feel stronger from rep one.

Save this post for your next workout! ⬇️

#QuickTipTuesday #FitnessTips #WorkoutSafety #StrengthTraining #[NICHE]Coach
