Template 1: Welcome & Project Kickoff

When to use: Send immediately after signing a contract to officially welcome the client and initiate the project.

Subject: Welcome to [YOUR COMPANY NAME] – Let's Create Something Amazing!

Hi [CLIENT FIRST NAME],

Thank you so much for choosing me to work on your [PROJECT TYPE] project! I'm thrilled to be part of bringing your vision to life.

To kick things off, here's what you can expect from our collaboration:

• Project Scope: [BRIEF PROJECT DESCRIPTION]

• Timeline: We'll deliver [FINAL DELIVERABLES] by [DEADLINE]

• Investment: As discussed, the total project cost is [TOTAL AMOUNT]

I've attached a copy of our signed agreement for your records. Please don't hesitate to reach out if you have any questions about the scope or timeline.

Looking forward to creating something incredible together!

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]

Template 2: Client Onboarding Questionnaire

When to use: After initial welcome, to collect detailed project and brand information

Hi [Client Name],

To ensure your project reflects your brand perfectly, I’d love to gather a bit more detail. Please take 10–15 minutes to fill out the questions below — your answers will guide every design decision I make.

You can reply directly to this email with your responses:

  1. What is the primary goal of this project? (e.g., increase conversions, rebrand, improve usability)
  2. Who is your target audience?
  3. How would you describe your brand personality? (e.g., modern, playful, professional, minimalist)
  4. Do you have existing brand guidelines (logo, colors, fonts)? If yes, please attach or share a link.
  5. What websites, apps, or designs do you admire? (Share URLs if possible)
  6. Are there any specific features, pages, or elements you want included?
  7. Do you have content (text, images) ready, or will you need support sourcing/creating it?
  8. What is your ideal timeline for delivery?

Let me know if any of these are unclear — I’m happy to jump on a quick call if needed.

Thanks again for trusting me with your project!

Best,

[Your Name]
