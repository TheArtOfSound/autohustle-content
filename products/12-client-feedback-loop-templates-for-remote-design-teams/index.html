Template 1: Initial Design Concept Feedback

When to use: After presenting initial design concepts to clients for the first time.

Dear [CLIENT NAME],

Thank you for taking the time to review our initial design concepts for [PROJECT NAME]. We appreciate your feedback and would like to hear your thoughts on the direction we've taken so far. Please find below a summary of the concepts presented:

  • Concept [CONCEPT NAME 1]: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF CONCEPT 1]
  • Concept [CONCEPT NAME 2]: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF CONCEPT 2]
  • Concept [CONCEPT NAME 3]: [BRIEF DESCRIPTION OF CONCEPT 3]

Could you please provide your feedback on the following aspects:

  • Which concept do you like the most and why?
  • Are there any elements from one concept that you'd like to see combined with another?
  • Are there any changes or directions you'd like us to explore further?

Your input is invaluable to us, and we look forward to hearing your thoughts.

Best regards,

[YOUR NAME]
