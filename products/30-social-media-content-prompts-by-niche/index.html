30 Social Media Content Prompts by Niche

Updated: February 26, 2026

30 niche-specific social media prompts with ready-to-use captions.

What's Inside

  1. Fitness: Share a quick 30-second workout routine that can be done at home with minimal equipment. Ask followers to tag a friend who needs this workout in their life.
  2. Food: Post a mouth-watering image of your signature dish and share the story behind it. Ask followers to comment with their favorite comfort food.
  3. Travel: Share a stunning landscape photo from your latest adventure and describe the journey to get there. Encourage followers to share their dream travel destinations.
  4. Fashion: Create a flat lay of your favorite accessories and share styling tips for each piece. Ask followers to vote on their favorite accessory.
  5. Beauty: Post a before-and-after makeup look and share the products used. Encourage followers to share their go-to makeup products.
  6. Tech: Share a quick tech tip that saves time or improves productivity. Ask followers to share their favorite tech hacks in the comments.
  7. Parenting: Post a heartwarming photo of your child and share a funny or touching parenting moment. Encourage followers to share their own parenting stories.

11. DIY: Post a step-by-step guide to a simple DIY project. Encourage followers to share their own DIY creations.
